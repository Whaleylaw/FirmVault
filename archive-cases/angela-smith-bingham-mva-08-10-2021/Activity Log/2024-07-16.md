Similarly, I do not believe there is any basis under Kentucky law for Ms. Smith to recover for loss of household services, and Ms. Smith did not testify to any such loss of services.  At this time, I can offer $20,000, exclusive of PIP. This offer is inclusive of any other liens, and includes the execution of Ms. Smith of a formal settlement agreement.  Additional terms include 1) indemnity and hold harmless, 2) no admission of fault, 3) confidentiality, and 4) a complete release of all claims.
