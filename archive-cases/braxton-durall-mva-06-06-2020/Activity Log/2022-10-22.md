The only way BI policies stack is if the owner of the vehicle and the driver of the vehicle have policies of their own. For example if I was driving Sarena's car and got into an accident. My insurance policy would be primary and hers would be secondary.  In this case the sister would need to have her own policy of insurance. UIM could stack if the sister did not live with in the household AND she was the titled owner. Of course then there might be some coverage issues.
