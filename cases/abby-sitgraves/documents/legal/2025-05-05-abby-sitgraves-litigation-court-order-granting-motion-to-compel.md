acknowledged a duty to fully respond to Interrogatories and Requests for Production, and further
acknowledged delay in filing those responses. Therefore, the Court being otherwise sufficiently

that were propounded to each of them on February 17, 2025, within fourteen (14) days of entry of
this Order.
