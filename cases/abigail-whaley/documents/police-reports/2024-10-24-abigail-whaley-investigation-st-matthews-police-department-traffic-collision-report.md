Unit one was traveling northbound in the 1300 block of Herr Lane, merging into the left-hand turn
lane. Unit two was stopped in the southbound turning lane in the 1300 block of Herr Lane. Unit one stated
he started to merge into the turn lane but could not remember what happened after the accident. Unit two
stated she was stopped waiting to turn left into Westport Village when unit one struck her vehicle headon. A witness remained at the scene and corroborated unit two's statement. The driver of unit one had no
complaints of pain at the time of the report. The driver of unit two and the passenger had no complaints of
pain at the time of the report. Unit one's vehicle had severe damage to the front bumper and roof from
where the vehicle had overturned. Unit two's vehicle had front airbag deployment and severe damage to
the front bumper. Units one and two had to be towed from the scene by Tony's Wrecker Service.
