Please allow this letter to acknowledge your representation of the above listed claimant. 
Per your request for limits please be advised Transportation Claims is the in-house claims 
handling department for Crete Carrier Corporation. Crete Carrier Corporation is self 
insured to a limit well above the minimum limits required by the DOT. There is no 
declarations page to provide. 
As you have indicated a bodily injury claim will be presented on behalf of your client, be 
advised Section 111 of the Medicare, Medicaid and SCHIP Extension Act of 2007 requires 
us to submit information on your client to the Center for Medicare and Medicaid Services 
[CMS] to determine if they are Medicare eligible and, if so, whether any medical or 
prescription bills arising out of this accident have been paid by Medicare.To fulfill the 
initial reporting requirements, please provide your client's full legal name, social security 
number and date of birth. 
Your client also has a legal duty to mitigate his/her medical damages and not engage in 
conduct that would aggravate such damages. As part of that duty, your client must take 
reasonable steps of using available health insurance, Medicare, or Medicaid for medical 
care and treatment you indicate they will be receiving. Should your client fail to take such 
reasonable steps and consequently incur unadjusted and overinflated medical expenses, it 
would be a breach of his common law legal obligation to mitigate damages.
