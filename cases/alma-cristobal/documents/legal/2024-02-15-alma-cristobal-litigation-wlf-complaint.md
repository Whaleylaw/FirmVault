Comes the Plaintiff, Alma Cristobal, by counsel, and for her cause of action against the 
Defendant, states as follows: 
1. The Plaintiff, Alma Cristobal, at all times relevant to the events stated herein, was a 
resident of Louisville, Jefferson County, Kentucky, residing at the address set forth above. 
2. Upon information and belief, Defendant Crete Carrier Corporation (hereinafter 
“Defendant Crete”) at all times relevant to the events stated in this Complaint, was and remains a 

2 
Nebraska corporation in the business of trucking services, with its principal office and individual 
registered agent located at the address set forth above. 
3. Upon information and belief, Defendant Roy Hamilton (hereinafter “Defendant 
Hamilton”) at all times relevant to the events stated in this Complaint was an individual resident 
of Columbus, Ohio, residing at the address set forth above. 
4. At the time of the collision described herein, Defendant Hamilton was an employee 
working as a driver for Defendant Crete who was acting within the course and scope of his 
employment, and in furtherance of Crete’s business. 
5. On or about February 15, 2024, Defendant Hamilton did so negligently and carelessly 
operate his motor vehicle, a certain 2022 Freightliner DS, Vin No. 1FUJHHDR2NLMX3286, on 
Interstate 65 in Louisville, Kentucky thereby causing a collision with Plaintiff. 
6. The negligence of Defendant Hamilton in the operation of his vehicle on the date and 
at the place aforesaid was a substantial factor in causing Plaintiff serious and permanent injuries, 
including great pain of body and mind, both past and future. In addition, the Plaintiff has incurred 
medical expenses as a result of the collision and injuries she sustained and will incur future medical 
expenses for the treatment of his injuries. Further, Plaintiff lost time and money from his gainful 
employment, resulting in damages for past lost wages and has had his future ability to labor and 
earn money substantially impaired, all to his damage in a sum in excess of the minimal amount 
necessary to confer jurisdiction upon this Court. 
7. Pursuant to KRS 304.39-062(b), threshold requirements have been met by the 
Plaintiff in that Plaintiff has incurred over $1,000.00 in medical expenses and sustained serious 
injuries. 
8. Additionally, although Plaintiff continues to investigate the facts surrounding the 

3 
collision at issue, Plaintiff states that to the extent the discovery or proof developed in this case 
indicates that Defendant Hamilton was operating his vehicle in a grossly negligent or reckless 
manner, or otherwise in violation of KRS 411.184 and/or KRS 411.186, Plaintiff is entitled to 
pursue, and does hereby assert, a claim for punitive damages against all defendants herein, and in 
particular Defendant Crete should the proof developed establish that said Defendant authorized, 
ratified, or should have anticipated the negligent and/or unlawful conduct alleged herein. 
WHEREFORE, Plaintiff, by counsel, demands as follows: 
1. Judgment against the Defendants in such an amount which will fairly and reasonably 
compensate Plaintiff for the damages alleged herein; 
2. Prejudgment interest; 
 3. Post judgment interest; 
 4. Attorneys fees and court costs reasonably incurred or expended; 
 5. Trial by jury on all issues so triable; 
 6. Punitive damages to the extent a trier of fact determines the actions of the Defendants 
were malicious, oppressive, fraudulent, or reckless; and, 
 7. All just and proper relief to which she may appear entitled, including the right to 
amend this Complaint.
