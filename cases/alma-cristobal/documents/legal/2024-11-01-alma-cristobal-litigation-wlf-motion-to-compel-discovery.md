 Comes the Plaintiff, by counsel, and gives notice that she will, on Monday, November 11,

MOTION TO COMPEL DISCOVERY REQUESTS 
 Plaintiff, Alma Cristobal, by and through counsel, and pursuant to all applicable Civil 
Rules and/or Local Rules hereby moves the Court for an Order compelling Defendant Crete Carrier 
Corporation to respond fully to Plaintiff’s outstanding written discovery requests. In support 
thereof, Plaintiff states as follows: 
 Plaintiff, by counsel, served her First Set of Discovery contemporaneously with filing the 
Complaint on July 9, 2024. Upon request from defense counsel, Microsoft Word copies of the 
requests were sent via email on August 23, 2024. On September 3, 2024, defense counsel reached 
out to Plaintiff’s counsel via email and requested an extension to September 26, 2024 to respond 
to the discovery, to which Plaintiff’s counsel obliged. On October 7, 2024, Plaintiff’s counsel again 

attempted to follow up on the outstanding responses. To date, the outstanding responses have not 
been received by the undersigned. 
 Plaintiff’s counsel affirmatively states that he has attempted in good faith with Defendant’s 
counsel in an effort to obtain the responses identified herein, as detailed above, and believes that 
only a Court Order will result a due and sufficient response by the Defendant. An Order is 
appropriate under these circumstances pursuant to CR 37.01 
WHEREFORE, the Plaintiff, by counsel, moves the Court to enter the tendered Order.
