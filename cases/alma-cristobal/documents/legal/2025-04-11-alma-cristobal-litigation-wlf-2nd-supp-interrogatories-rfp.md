Comes the Plaintiff, Alma Cristobal, by and through counsel, and requests the Defendant, 
Crete Carrier Corporation (hereinafter “Crete” or “Defendant Crete”), to answer under oath the 
following Interrogatories and to produce the following documents and/or things within thirty (30) 
days of service hereof in accordance with the Kentucky Rules of Civil Procedure. 
These Interrogatories and Requests for Production of Documents shall be deemed 
continuing. Knowledge of the attorneys for the Defendant or the claims agent or any other agent 
of the Defendant, or of any employee or agent of the Defendant, shall be deemed to be the 
knowledge of the Defendant. 
Anything coming to your knowledge after filing Answers to these Interrogatories and 
Responses to these Requests for Production which would change your original answer or responses 
should be contained in an Amended Answer or an Amended Response and served upon Plaintiff’s 
attorneys immediately after the same is brought to your attention.
