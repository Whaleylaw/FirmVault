Comes the Plaintiff, Alma Cristobal, by and through counsel, and requests the Defendant, Crete Carrier Corporation (hereinafter “Crete” or “Defendant Crete”), to answer under oath the following Interrogatories and to produce the following documents and/or things within thirty (30) days of service hereof in accordance with the Kentucky Rules of Civil Procedure.

These Interrogatories and Requests for Production of Documents shall be deemed continuing. Knowledge of the attorneys for the Defendant or the claims agent or any other agent of the Defendant, or of any employee or agent of the Defendant, shall be deemed to be the knowledge of the Defendant. 

Anything coming to your knowledge after filing Answers to these Interrogatories and Responses to these Requests for Production which would change your original answer or responses should be contained in an Amended Answer or an Amended Response and served upon Plaintiff’s attorneys immediately after the same is brought to your attention.

<u>**DEFINITIONS**</u>

A.The words "and" and "or" shall be construed conjunctively or disjunctively as necessary to make the request inclusive rather than exclusive.

B.The use of the words "include(s)" and "including" shall be construed to mean "without limitation."

C.The words "related to" and "relating" mean relating to, referring to, reflecting, pertaining to, evidencing, or any way relevant to any issue, claim, defense, or question posed by the interrogatory or request within the meaning of CR 26 or any other applicable Kentucky Rule of Civil Procedure or Evidence. 

D.“Document” as used herein means anything created or reduced to writing whether produced on paper or by electronic mean including but not limited to letters, faxes, emails, telephone messages, texts, tweets, memoranda, data stored on any computer hardware or software, in the cloud, on an electronic system or otherwise.

E.Plural shall include the singular and the singular shall include the plural.

F.The term "present” used herein to refer to a point in time shall mean the date upon which Responses to the Requests are being made.

G.“You” or “Your” or “Defendant” as used herein means the Defendant Crete Carrier Corporation and any and all of Defendant Crete’s employees, agents, or third persons who are under the supervision or control of Defendant Crete.   

H.“Identify” or “explain” or “state” as used in these Interrogatories with respect to any person or thing means to state fully all who or what the thing or person is and if relating to a person to give the person’s full name, address, and other contact information to the extent known or available to the Defendant or other person contributing to the answers and responses and for that person to state and explain fully all of the information that person or persons has in connection with the subject matter of the request herein.

I. The terms “incident” or “collision” as used in these Interrogatories refers to the collision Plaintiff was involved in with Defendant Roy Hamilton which occurred on I-65 in Louisville, Jefferson County, Kentucky on February 15, 2024, as alleged in the Complaint.
