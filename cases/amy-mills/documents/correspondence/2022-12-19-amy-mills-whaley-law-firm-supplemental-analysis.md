RE: Amy Mills 
Dear Mr. Whaley: 
Upon your request, I prepared a supplemental analysis regarding Amy Mills based upon the earnings 
of counselors in the Southcentral Kentucky nonmetropolitan area. Ms. Mills is a LPCA (Licensed 
Professional Counselor Associate) and is 1 test away for LPC (Licensed Professional Counselor) 
status. 
The attached supplemental calculation is based upon the median earnings of Substance Abuse, 
Behavioral Disorder and Mental Health Counselors (21-1018) in the Southcentral Kentucky 
nonmetropolitan area, or $43,514 annually.1
 Ms. Mills absent-injury earning capacity is reasonably 
represented by this rate. Her with-injury earning ability is based upon her absent-injury earning ability 
is reasonably represented by the pre-injury rate reduced by 5.9%, which is the earnings differential 
between workers with a physical disability and their counterparts with a physical disability, or 
$40,947 annually. 
All other variables in the calculation are identical to those used in the original report dated October 
31, 2022. The lifetime loss of earnings in this calculation is $240,252, stated in terms of present 
value. Please advise if further information is required.
