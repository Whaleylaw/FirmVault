Dear Amy: 
 Please find enclosed the Defendant Forcht Bank’s 3rd set discovery requests to Plaintiff. Do not be alarmed, these 
documents are a normal part of the litigation process. They are sent to you by the Defendant’s Attorney to find out the 
specifics of your claim. If you need assistance completing the requests, please contact our office. 
 Many of the requested documents or items may already be contained in your file here in our office. We will 
provide those documents or items to the Defendant's attorney along with the material you provide. Some of the items 
requested by the Defendant’s attorney can only be gathered with your assistance. Collecting documents such as income tax 
records (if needed to verify a lost wage claim) can take time and may require obtaining tax records from the IRS. We suggest 
that you start gathering the documents or other items that the Defendant has requested. 
 There is a thirty day (30) deadline to respond to these requests. Therefore, we need your immediate assistance in 
completing the requests. Time is of the essence. Once you have provided this information, the Defendant's attorney will be in 
a better position to evaluate your claim. Often, the Defendant’s insurer or attorney will attempt to negotiate a settlement after 
obtaining your responses to the aforementioned requests for additional information. 
 The Defendant's attorney is entitled, by law, to review all information relating in any way to your claim. The 
sooner the information is provided, the sooner your case can be settled. Please accept our thanks in advance for your 
cooperation.
