Important message regarding your neuro-concussion IME client.
Your client will undergo significant neurocognitive and behavioral testing. The end results contain very 
objective measures that help us confirm the diagnosis of concussion. However, it should be noted that 
the test is very sensitive with regards to symptom magnification and validity. Please educate your 
client that being straightforward with answers and participating in the exam to the best of their 
ability is also very important. Otherwise, the test will determine possible malingering, symptom 
magnification or similar, or that the test is invalid.
That said, if we do find any of the above, we will contact you prior to furnishing a report so as to 
determine whether or not the report should be submitted. At this point, we will have approximately 6 
hours invested in the IME, so a full refund is not possible. However, we can offer a refund of $500 if a 
report is no longer needed.
