USE OF PREMISES 
_____ The Lessee will use and occupy the premises for the purpose(s) stated in the Contract and for no other purpose 
without the written consent of The Corbin Center/ Corbin Industrial Development Commission Executive Director. Lessee 
will conform to and comply with all Corbin Center rules and regulations, and, comply with all applicable municipal, county, 
state and federal ordinances, laws, rules and regulations during the use of the Corbin Center. Lessee will not use the 
premises to create any nuisance, or in any manner as may increase the risk of fire or rate of liability insurance on the Corbin 
Center. 
RELEASE 
_____ The Lessee hereby agrees, covenants and guarantees that The Corbin Industrial Development Commission dba The 
Corbin Center, its Board of Directors, and the City of Corbin, Kentucky, their assigns, employees, agents, members, officials 
and officers are hereby released and discharged in all ways from any and all claims, demands, liabilities and causes of action 
for any injuries, losses and damages of any kind or nature whatsoever arising directly or indirectly from the use of the 
Corbin Center premises and/ or equipment. 
INDEMNIFICATION 
_____ The Lessee hereby agrees, covenants and guarantees that it hereby shall indemnify and save harmless The Corbin 
Industrial Development Commission dba The Corbin Center, and the City of Corbin, Kentucky, their assigns, employees, 
agents, members, officials and officers from any and all claims, demands, liabilities and causes of action for damages of any 
kind or nature whatsoever arising directly or indirectly from the use of the Corbin Center premises and/ or equipment. 
INSURANCE 
_____ The Lessee shall obtain and maintain in force, public liability insurance, insuring against claims for bodily injury, 
wrongful death, or property damage occurring upon, in, or about the premises, with minimum limits of $1,000,000, with 
The Corbin Center, Corbin Industrial Development Commission and the City of Corbin, Kentucky to appear on said policy of 
insurance as an additional insured, and said insurance policy and carrier shall be acceptable to the Corbin Center. Further, 
prior to the use of the premises by Lessee, the Lessee shall provide to the Corbin Center a certificate of insurance in 
compliance hereof. 
OFF- CAMPUS CATERER LICENSE AND INSURANCE 
_____ All off- campus caterers must provide The Corbin Center/ Corbin Industrial Development Commission a copy of a 
valid catering license and shall obtain, and maintain in force, public liability insurance insuring against claims for bodily 
injury, wrongful death or property damage upon, in or about the premises, with minimum limits of $__________, with the 
Corbin Center/ Corbin Industrial Development Commission and the City of Corbin, Kentucky to appear on said policy of 
insurance as additional insured, and said insurance policy and carrier shall be acceptable to The Corbin Center/ Corbin 
Industrial Development Commission. Further, prior to the use of the property, caterer shall provide the Corbin Center/ 
Corbin Industrial Development Commission with a certificate of insurance in compliance hereof. 
CENTER POLICIES & PROCEDURES
_____ Should failure to make payment as agreed under this contract, the responsible party does hereby promise and agree 
to pay the Corbin Center/ Corbin Industrial Development Commission its reasonable attorney’s fees, court costs and all 
other expenses incurred in collecting or attempting to collect the obligations hereunder. For out- of- state clients, the laws 
of the Commonwealth of Kentucky shall govern this agreement and the parties do hereby consent to the jurisdiction of the 
Whitley District and/or Whitley Circuit court(s) in all matters related to its interpretation and enforcement. The Corbin 
Center/ Corbin Industrial Development Commission must receive your signed contract, full payment, room arrangements 
and other considerations at least thirty (30) days prior to your event. If you must cancel your events, please let us know at 
least ten (10) full business days prior to your scheduled event. Failure to notify the Corbin Center of cancellation will result 
in a 50% cancellation fee of the total rental dollar amount, if payment in- full has been made. Thank you for choosing The 
Corbin Center for your upcoming event. Please feel free to contact us [PHONE-1] if you require additional information. 
 _____ Use of tobacco products is not permitted in the Corbin Center, nor on the front patio. The designated 
smoking area is outside, 25 feet away from the doors near the vending machines in the lobby area.

 _____ No portion of any passageway or exit way shall by locked, blocked or bolted while the facility is in use. 
Interior and exterior doors may not be blocked in any manner due to fire codes; firefighting and emergency 
equipment (including emergency exit signs) may not be hidden or obstructed. 
 ______ Tables and chairs may be moved by the client to best accommodate your event, however, the portable 
walls in Room 103 (MPR) may only be moved by Corbin Center staff. 
 _____ Nothing may be taped, tacked, pinned or stuck on walls for any reason. 
 _____ Any item that can pierce or damage tables is not permitted. 
 _____ Candles are permitted providing they are in Corbin Center approved candle holders on a table covering. All 
other open flames are prohibited. 
 _____ The use, display or storage of propane tanks, LPG gas or other flammable liquids is strictly prohibited. 
 _____ Fog machines of any type are not permitted in the building. The fog generated from these machines will get 
in the ventilation system and trigger the fire alarm. Once triggered, the building must be evacuated, and the fire 
department will close the event. Rental fee will be forfeit and no refunds will be considered. 
 _____ Glitter, in any form, is not permitted inside the Corbin Center. Confetti may be used but clean- up is the 
responsibility of the client. An additional $50- $100 clean- up fee (if the Corbin Center has contracted to do cleanup for your event at a cost of $50, an additional $50 fee will be added) will be charged if client does not complete 
clean- up. 
 _____ Any items brought into the Corbin Center by the lessee or authorized representative of the lessee, should be 
removed by the conclusion of the contracted usage time. The Corbin Center is not responsible for any items left 
after the rental period has ended. 
 _____ Please throw away all trash in the cans provided. The Corbin Center staff is responsible for emptying 
garbage cans and basic cleanup following the event. However, any excessive cleaning deemed necessary by the 
Corbin Center is subject to an additional cleanup fee or loss of damage deposit, no less that $50 not to exceed 
$150.
 _____ Lessee must leave rented premises in the same condition it was in upon arrival. All damages to the Corbin 
Center property or equipment shall be the financial responsibility of the individual/ lessee/ organization listed on 
the rental contract. 
 _____ The individual/ lessee/ organization is responsible for the conduct of its representatives, members and/ or 
guests. 
 _____ No parking in fire lanes, service roads, loading dock area, or any location posted “NO PARKING”. Please be 
aware that there is a 2- way street (Corbin Center Drive) in front of the building; to ensure your safety, please look 
both ways before crossing roadway. 
 _____ Inclement weather does not constitute a refund of any portion of the rental fee. 
 _____ The Lobby area of the Corbin Center is a common area for all clients/ visitors and is not a play area for 
children attending any event. Children 15 years of age and under shall be accompanied by an adult while in any 
area of the Corbin Center.
