Comes the Defendant, Forcht Bank, NA, incorrectly named Forcht Bank, by and through counsel, pursuant to CR 34, and hereby requests and demands that the Plaintiff produce the following documents in the care, possession or control of the Plaintiff’s attorney, prepared by the Plaintiffs, her attorney, or any other person or entity, for inspection and/or copying within the time allowed by the civil rules:  

<u>**REQUEST NO. 1**</u>**:**Any and all medical bills from physicians or surgeons; hospital bills; outpatient treatment bills; physical therapy treatment bills; prescription and nonprescription drug bills; bills for medical appliances; and any and all other bills or documents to support the Plaintiff’s claim for medical expenses, including all attendant expenses for travel or lodging which the Plaintiffs claims were necessary as a result of the accident which forms the basis of her Complaint.

<u>**REQUEST NO. 2**</u>**:**Copies of any and all documents which the Plaintiff’s has to support each of the specific allegations of damages, including documentation of lost wages, past and future. 

<u>**REQUEST NO. 3**</u>**:**A copy of any and all pictures, plats, maps, diagrams or other documentation which in any way relate to the subject matter of the Plaintiff’s claim herein and/or which they intend to use for purposes of illustration or introduction into evidence at the trial of this action.

<u>**REQUEST NO. 4**</u>**:**Copies of federal and state income tax returns for the last five (5) years, including the current year. 

<u>**REQUEST NO. 5**</u>**:**Copies of any written or otherwise recorded statements which have been taken from any witness.

<u>**REQUEST NO. 6**</u>**:**Copies of any and all written reports, medical evaluations and/or medical records, relating in any way to the allegations contained in the Plaintiff’s Complaint.

<u>**REQUEST NO. 7**</u>**:**Copies of Plaintiff’s Social Security Disability applications or other Social Security Disability documents or records, if any.

<u>**REQUEST NO. 8:**</u>Please produce a signed medical authorization. 

<u>**REQUEST NO. 9**</u>:Please produce a signed employment authorization.

The Defendant will agree to produce to Plaintiff’s attorney, free of charge, copies of any records obtained via signed authorizations provided.

The Plaintiff are hereby requested to provide the foregoing documents by mailing copies of same to counsel for Defendant at the Defendant's expense; or to produce the originals for photocopying in the offices of counsel for the Plaintiff, within thirty (30) days from the receipt of this request.
