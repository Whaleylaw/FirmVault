Comes the Plaintiff, AMY MILLS, by Counsel, and for her Complaint against the 
above-named Defendant, states as follows:
PARTIES
1. Plaintiff, AMY MILLS, was at all times herein referred to, as a resident
of Knox County, Corbin, Kentucky.
2. Defendant, FORCHT BANK located at 820 Master Street, Knox County, 
Corbin, Kentucky 40701, was at all times herein referred to, as a corporation with a principal 
office located in Fayette County, Lexington, Kentucky and that this is a corporation actively 
conducting business as, among other things, a financial institute within the Commonwealth 
with the power to sue and be sued here subject to the jurisdiction of this Court.
3. At all times relevant hereto, Defendant owned, managed, maintained and/or 

2
supervised a financial institute located at 830 Master Street, Knox County, Corbin, Kentucky. 
4. At all times relevant hereto, Defendant, by and through its agents, ostensible 
agents, servants, employees and/or representatives, had a duty to maintain and keep said 
premises safe for the use of individuals legally coming upon same, along with warning its 
invitees of all known dangerous conditions. 
JURISDICTION & VENUE
5. Jurisdiction and Venue are proper in this Court as the Plaintiff is a resident of
Knox County, Corbin, Kentucky, in which injuries were sustained by the Plaintiff occurred in 
Knox County, Corbin Kentucky, and the claims made against the Defendant exceed the 
jurisdictional minimum limits of this Court.
6. Jurisdiction over the Defendant is proper pursuant to KRS 454.210 and/or KRS 
188.020 and/or CR 4. 
FACTS
7. On or about April 26, 2019 in Knox County, Corbin Kentucky, as a direct and 
proximate result of the negligent acts and/or omissions of Defendant, by and through its agents, 
ostensible agents, servants, employees and/or representatives, the Plaintiff, Amy Mills, while
lawfully upon said premises exercising due care for her own safety, slipped and fell due to wet 
floors when entering the side door.
8. As a direct and proximate result of the negligence of the Defendant, the Plaintiff 
sustained serious injuries, incurred medical expenses and was caused to suffer great pain, both 
mental and physical; damage in an amount which exceeds the jurisdictional minimum of this 
court.
9. Pursuant to KRS 304.39-062(b), threshold requirements have been met by the 

3
Plaintiff in that Plaintiff have incurred over $1,000.00 in medical expenses and sustained serious 
injuries.
WHEREFORE, Plaintiff demand as follows:
1. For an amount of compensatory damages that is fair and reasonable as 
established by the evidence;
2. For prejudgment interest from the date of injury until such time as 
judgment is paid;
3. For her costs herein expended; and
4. For any and all other relief to which she may be entitled.
