***** ***** *****
Comes the Defendant, Forcht Bank, by and through counsel, pursuant to CR 37.01, and for 
its Motion to Compel responses to discovery requests propounded upon Plaintiff, Amy Mills, 
hereby states as follows:
On or about March 5, 2021, the Defendants propounded a second set of Requests for 
Production of Documents upon Plaintiff. See Exhibit 1. Pursuant to the Civil Rules, Plaintiff’s 
discovery responses were to be tendered on or before April 5, 2021. To date, no response to the 
second set of requests for production have been received by the undersigned.
In an attempt to resolve this dispute extrajudicially, the undersigned sent correspondence 
to Plaintiff’s Counsel on July 7, 2021, requesting that the discovery responses be provided as they 
were overdue, or alternatively, call Defense Counsel to come to an agreement as to when the 
responsive documents would be provided. To date, no response has been received. Pursuant to CR 
37.01(b)(i), the Defendant is permitted to move this Court for an Order compelling the Plaintiff to 
respond to its discovery requests. Due to the length of time that has passed since the tendering of 
discovery, the Defendant respectfully requests that the Court compel the Plaintiff to respond to 
same within ten (10) days.
