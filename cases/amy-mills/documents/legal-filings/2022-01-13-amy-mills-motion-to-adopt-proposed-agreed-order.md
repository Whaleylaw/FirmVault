***** ***** *****
Come now the parties through undersigned counsel, in agreement to request the Court to 
Amend the Reference Date in the September 14, 2021 Scheduling Order to January 1, 2023. As 
grounds, Plaintiff has only recently been released from active treatment for her alleged injuries. 
The extension the current deadlines will allow counsel to complete the necessary record retrieval, 
consult with experts and mediate the case. A proposed Agreed Order is attached hereto.
NOTICE OF HEARING
All parties shall hereby take notice that the foregoing Motion shall come on for hearing 
before the Knox Circuit Court on January 20, 2022 at 9:00 a.m. or as soon thereafter as 
Counsel may be heard.
