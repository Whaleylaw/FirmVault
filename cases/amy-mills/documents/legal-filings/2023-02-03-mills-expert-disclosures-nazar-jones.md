# <u>Foundation</u><u> </u><u>and</u><u> </u><u>Summary</u><u> </u><u>of</u><u> </u><u>the</u><u> </u><u>Grounds</u><u> </u><u>for</u><u> </u><u>Expert</u><u> </u><u>Opinions:</u>

The summary of the grounds upon which this physician will base his opinions shall include the Plaintiffs medical records, the other documents identified below, the Plaintiffs medical history, and this expert's many years of experience in the field of medicine. As foundation, this doctor will also base opinions on the medical possibility of an slip and fall accident causing the type of injury sustained by the Plaintiff, the proximity in time of the accident to the onset of the Plaintiffs symptoms, and the existence of any more probable causes of the Plaintiffs injuries (as in other causes more probable than the accident forming the basis of

this litigation).

The facts, data, and foundation upon which opinions shall be based include any and all additional documents provided to this expert and/or other medical providers including, but not limited to, information provided by any governmental entity, information related to the accident forming the basis of this litigation, reports, disability information, information and/or documents produced or identified during this litigation, and any other documents and/or information given to this expert and/or the Plaintiffs other medical providers presented to this expert during and/or prior to this expert's testimony per the Kentucky rules.

As foundation, this physician will rely on and/or testify regarding the following:

- The manner and happening of the accident;

- Any exhibits attached to any depositions taken in this litigation;

- Medical records from the Plaintiffs medical providers;

- The Plaintiffs medical bills including, but not limited to, bills incurred for medical treatment, care, or diagnosis, pharmacy or medication bills, bills for medical devices, and bills incurred for diagnostic examinations;
- Any diagnostic reports and/or tests performed on the Plaintiff including, but not limited to, MRI's, x-rays, or CT scans;
- Photographs of any vehicles involved in the accident;

- Photographs or diagrams of the accident and/or accident scene;

- Photographs of the Plaintiff;

- Medical illustrations and literature regarding the various injuries sustained by the Plaintiff;
- Documents, illustrations, films, exhibits, periodicals, information, testimony,

records, reports, or learned treatises upon which experts in this expert's field generally rely, relied upon by any defense experts, and/or produced by any party during this litigation;

- Any depositions, affidavits, or written and/or recorded statements provided in relation to this litigation or the claims underlying same;
- A chart or charts summarizing the Plaintiff's medical bills, future medical bills, future medical care, past medical care, and/or damages;
- Any documents regarding property damage to the vehicles involved in the accident including, but not limited to, repair estimates or descriptions of necessary repairs;
- Written discovery responses of any party to this litigation;

- Any documents regarding the Plaintiffs work history and/or disability;

- Any work restrictions and/or impairment finding given to the Plaintiff and/or regarding the Plaintiff;
- The Plaintiffs medical history;

- The Plaintiffs personal and social history;

- Any medical devices used by the Plaintiff; and

- The mechanism of the Plaintiffs injuries.

This physician will base opinions on facts and data perceived by or made known to him at or before this expert's testimony as allowed by Kentucky law. Further, this expert will rely on other facts and data of the type reasonably relied upon by experts in this expert's field. In providing opinions, this expert will rely on facts and data given to this expert by others and/or the opinions of other experts.

Mrs. Linda L. Jones, MRC, MBA, MPA, CRC

Vocational Economics, Inc.

Mrs. Jones will testify to the vocational and economic lost incurred by the Plaintiff as a result of her injuries.

Mrs. Jones is a Senior Analyst with Vocational Economic, Inc. in Louisville, KY and has worked as a case manager at VEI for seven years before beginning her career as a Vocational-Economic Analyst in 2005 becoming a Senior Analyst in 2018.
