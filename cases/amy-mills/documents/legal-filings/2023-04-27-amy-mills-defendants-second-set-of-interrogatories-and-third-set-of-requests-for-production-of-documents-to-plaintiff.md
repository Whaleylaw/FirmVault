INTERROGATORIES
INTERROGATORY NO. 1: Please identify the names and addresses of 
each person or organization with whom you have been employed or participated in a practicum or 
internship in the areas of Substance Abuse, Behavioral Disorder and Mental Health Counseling, 
worked as a LPCA (Licensed Professional Counselor Associate), or worked toward a LPC 
(Licensed Professional Counselor) designation.
INTERROGATORY NO. 2: As to each person or organization referred to 
in the answer to the preceding interrogatory, please state:
A) The dates of each such employment, practicum or internship;
B) The nature of the work or duties;
C) The place or places where such work or duties were performed; 

D) The names and telephone numbers of any supervisors for each employment, 
practicum or internship; and
E) The average weekly or monthly compensation from such employment, practicum 
or internship.
REQUESTS FOR PRODUCTION
REQUEST NO. 1: Please provide copies of any and all applications for employment, 
practicums or internships in the areas of Substance Abuse, Behavioral Disorder and Mental Health 
Counseling, the date on which those applications were submitted, and the result of the application.
REQUEST NO. 2: Copies of any and all documents which the Plaintiff has to support
Interrogatories No. 1 and 2.
