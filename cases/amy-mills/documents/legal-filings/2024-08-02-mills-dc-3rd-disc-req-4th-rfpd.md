COMMONWEALTH OF KENTUCKY
KNOX CIRCUIT COURT
DIVISION TWO
CIVIL ACTION NO. 20-CI-00112 
AMY MILLS PLAINTIFF
V. DEFENDANT’S THIRD SET OF INTERROGATORIES AND FOURTH SET OF REQUESTS FOR PRODUCTION OF DOCUMENTS TO PLAINTIFF
FORCHT BANK DEFENDANT
***** ***** *****
Comes the Defendant, Forcht Bank, NA, by and through counsel, and hereby propounds 
the following Interrogatories and Request for Production of Documents to Plaintiff, to be answered 
under oath, in writing, and within thirty (30) days of the date of service herein, in accordance with
and pursuant to the Kentucky Rules of Civil Procedure. 
INTERROGATORIES
INTERROGATORY NO. 1: Please state each and every date on which you were 
examined or treated by any doctor, physician, hospital or medical provider with respect to any 
injury or disability which you claim was sustained or suffered as a result of the alleged occurrence
from January 1, 2023 through the present date, setting forth in detail as to each such date or 
examination or treatment;
A) The name and address of each doctor, physician or medical provider; 
B) The nature and extent of the examination or treatment received from each such 
doctor, physician or medical provider; 
C) Any diagnosis or prognosis made by each such doctor, physician, medical provider 
or any other person or organization, on account of each such person; and 
D) The amount charged by each such doctor, physician or medical provider for 
treatment or examination given, fully itemized as indicated in any bill rendered therefore. 

RESPONSE:
INTERROGATORY NO. 2: Are you presently receiving treatment or care for any 
medical condition involving any injury, illness or disability which is a result of this incident? If 
so, please state with respect to any such treatment;
A) The name and address of the physician providing the treatment;
B) The nature of the treatment being rendered; 
C) Dates of any future appointments currently known. 
RESPONSE:
REQUESTS FOR PRODUCTION OF DOCUMENTS
REQUEST NO. 1: Copies of any and all written reports, medical evaluations and/or 
medical records, including copies of MRIs and radiographic films, of the Plaintiff from January 1, 
2023 to present. 
REQUEST NO. 2: Copies of any and all medical bills from physicians or surgeons; 
hospital bills; outpatient treatment bills; physical therapy treatment bills; prescription and 
nonprescription drug bills; bills for medical appliances; and any and all other bills or documents 
to support the Plaintiff’s claim for medical expenses from January 1, 2023 to present. 
REQUEST NO. 3: Please produce a signed medical authorization (see attached). The 
Defendant will agree to produce to Plaintiff’s attorney, free of charge, copies of any records 
obtained via signed authorization provided.
