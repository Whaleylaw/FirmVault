Comes the Defendant, Forcht Bank, by and through counsel, and, and pursuant to CR
37.01, and respectfully requests this Court issue an Order Compelling Plaintiff, Amy Mills, to
answer Defendant’s Interrogatories and Request for Production of Documents propounded on
August 2, 2024. See Notice of Service attached hereto as Exhibit 1.The discovery pertains to the
identification of medical providers, and production of treatment records and bills from January 1,
2023 to present. Plaintiffs Counsel has advised that Plaintiff continues to seek medical treatment,
but has not provided sufficient information to the undersigned to request and obtain treatment
records and bills. See correspondence attached hereto as Exhibit 2.
As of the date of this Motion, Plaintiffs discovery responses are overdue. Pursuant to CR
37.01(b)(), the Defendant is permitted to move the Court for an order compelling Plaintiff to
respond to its discovery requests. Due to the significant length of time that has passed since the
tendering of discovery as well as the recent development that Plaintiff will be undergoing surgery
in early 2025, the Defendant respectfully requests that the Court compel the Plaintiff to respond to
same within fourteen (10) days.
NOTICE OF HEARING
All parties shall hereby take notice that the foregoing Motion to Compel Discovery shall
come on for hearing before the Knox Circuit Court on December 16, 2024 at 9:00 AM or as soon
thereafter as counsel may be heard.
