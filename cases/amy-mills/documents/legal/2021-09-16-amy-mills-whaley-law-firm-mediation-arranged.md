Dear Amy: 
 We are writing to let you know that we have arranged a non-binding mediation in an effort to resolve your 
claim. It is likely that we have explained this already, but mediation is a process by which the parties attempt to 
resolve a claim with the assistance of an impartial, third person. The third party, usually a retired judge or an 
attorney trained in mediation, points out the strengths and weaknesses of the claims made by both the Plaintiff and 
Defendant. Often, this mediation will result in a settlement of the Plaintiff's claim. Thus, it is possible that we will 
reach a settlement of your claim at the mediation. 
 No judgment is entered for either party. A successful mediation depends on the willingness of both the 
Plaintiff and Defendant to make concessions. Neither party will walk away totally happy, but, by reaching a 
settlement at the mediation, both parties will have avoided the risks associated with a trial. The parties split the cost 
of the hearing. Typically, the cost of settling a claim through mediation is about 10% of the cost of a trial. 
Your mediation is scheduled to take place on November 17, 2021 at 1:00p via a Zoom Meeting. The link 
will be provided the day of the mediation. Please be sure that your email address is correct in our file.

mediation on the date and time referenced above, and contact our office thirty (30) minutes before the mediation 
is scheduled to begin to discuss any questions you may have.
