This is why stopping is so difficult.

Why is smoking bad for me?

* Smoking increases your chance for having a heart attack, a stroke and/or cancer. * Smoking decreases the amount of oxygen that gets to your brain, heart, and other organs. * Smoking raises your blood pressure and makes it harder for your blood to circulate throughout your body. .
