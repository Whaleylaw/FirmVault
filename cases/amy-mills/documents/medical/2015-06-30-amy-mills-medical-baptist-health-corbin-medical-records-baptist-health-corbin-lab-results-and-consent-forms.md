Weliness and Health Promotion: for smokers, those who are around smokers (second-hand smoke), or those who quit smoking and falled.

Smoking cessation is quitting smeking. Smoking is a very difficult habit to break for most peopfe. The chemical, nicotine, which you Inhale into your lungs from a cigarette, causes your brain to have more dopamine. Dopamine Is a chemical that makes you feel good. Nicotine has many more harmful effects on your body. This is why it is so important to stop smoking. What is the best way to quit?

Set a specific date to stop smoking and tell other people that you are going to stop smoking on your quit date.

Talk to your doctor about medications that can be used to help you quit. Over the counter and prescription are available. Talk to ex-smokers about what helped them quit.

Before your quit date, only allow yourself to smoke in one or two places.

Pay attention to when, where, and with whom you smoke. Notice what things make you feel like smoking.

Avoid the places, situations, and people that cause you to want a cigarette, at least at first when you are quitting. Make a list of all of the good things that will happen if you quit smoking.
