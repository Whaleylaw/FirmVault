PATIENT INSTRUCTIONS
POST-ANESTHESIA
IMMEDIATELY FOLLOWING SURGERY: Do not drive, drink any alcohol, operate machinery, make important decisions, 
or sign any legal paperwork for the next twenty four hours after surgery or while taking narcotic pain medications or 
sedatives. You may experience a sore throat, mild nausea, dizziness, drowsiness, or a headache. These symptoms are 
normal after surgery and will subside in the next 24 hours. If you develop severe nausea and vomiting, a severe 
headache, or a high fever please notify your doctor immediately. 
DO NOT TAKE TYLENOL WITH YOUR PERCOCET, DRINK PLENTY OF WATER, AND TAKE WITH FOOD.
CALL DR.KHALILY'S OFFICE IF YOU NOTICE ANY SIGNS OR SYMPTOMS OF INFECTION SUCH AS: INCREASED 
REDNESS, SWELLING, FOUL SMELLING DRAINAGE, FEVER, RED STREAKS, OR CHILLS.
