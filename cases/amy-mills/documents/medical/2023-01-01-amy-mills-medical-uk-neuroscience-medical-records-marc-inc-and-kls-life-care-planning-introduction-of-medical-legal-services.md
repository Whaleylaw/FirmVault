Introduction Letter
Thank you for expressing interest in and reaching out to us for your medical legal work. Through this introduction 
letter, we aim to explain our backgrounds and the services that we can provide for you.
Marc P. Orlando, M.D. was a licensed physical therapist and went on to medical school at the University of Cincinnati. 
He completed a combined residency and board certifications in Internal Medicine and Physical Medicine and 
Rehabilitation. Dr. Orlando has over 20 years of work experience ranging from urgent care, hospital night coverage 
doctor, and transition into a full time Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation position. 
Additionally, Dr. Orlando has had leadership positions and was a Musculoskeletal Department Director with 
oversight over Orthopedics, Podiatry, and Rheumatology as well as Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation. He has 
served as a physiatrist in acute care inpatient rehabilitation programs as well as long term acute care hospitals 
including "Select" and "Regency" Hospitals. He has also performed acute rehabilitation in skilled nursing facilities. 
Currently Dr. Orlando is at Mayfield Brain & Spine, serving as the Medical Director of Pain Services at Mayfield Spine 
Surgery Center. His practice is largely a spine based practice and he performs interventional spinal procedures 
ranging from epidural steroid injections to spinal cord stimulators.
Dr. Orlando has been the CEO, Founder, and Medical Director of Medical Associates and Rehabilitation Consultants 
(M.A.R.C., Inc.) since 2003. His work includes both plaintiff and defense cases. He has worked in large multiple 
provider catastrophic cases as well as single provider incidents. This has included participation in Life Care Plans,
Medical Cost Projection recommendations, expert witnessing (including court appearances), file reviews as well as 
physician consulting. His recent focus has been on physician directed recommendations for life care planning and 
medical cost projections. Dr. Orlando has partnered with Kate Smith, who is a certified nurse life care planner. 
Kate Smith BSN, RN, CNLCP, LCP-C is a registered nurse who has worked in the neurosurgery and neurology field for 
over 10 years. She graduated with her Bachelor’s of Science in Nursing degree from the University of Cincinnati. She 
has neurosurgical ICU experience, caring for patients with strokes, spinal cord injuries, traumatic brain injuries, 
aneurysms, craniotomies, extensive spine surgeries, seizures, as well as other medical and surgical complications 
including but not limited to sepsis, respiratory failure, cardiac failure, gastrointestinal complications, and wounds. 
She has experience in an outpatient surgery center, working with same day cervical and lumbar surgeries, spinal cord 
stimulators, and pain pumps. Kate currently works in pain management, caring for patients with acute and chronic 
pain, issues of radiculopathy, cervicalgia, failed back syndrome, scoliosis, and spondylolisthesis. In 2022, Kate
obtained certification as a Nurse Life Care Planner. She works with both plaintiff and defense. Her work includes 
Medical Cost Projections, Life Care Plans, medical records reviews, as well as nursing consulting. She has been the 
CEO and Founder of KLS Life Care Planning, LLC since 2023. 
We have been hired both jointly and individually as it relates to future care planning.
