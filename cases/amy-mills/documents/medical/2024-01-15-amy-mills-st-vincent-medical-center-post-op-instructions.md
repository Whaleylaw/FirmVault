Smoking cessation is quitting smoking. Smoking is a very difficult habit to break for most people. The chemical, nicotine, which you inhale into your lungs from a cigarette, causes your brain to have more dopamine. Dopamine is a chemical that makes you feel good. Nicotine has many more harmful effects on your body. This is why it is so important to stop smoking. Why is quitting good for me? As soon as you quit smoking, your chances of having a heart attack, stroke, and other diseases start to go down. The longer you do not smoke, the smaller your risk for these diseases and problems.

What is the best way to quit?

Avoid the places, situations, and people that cause you to want a cigarette, at least at first when you are quitting. Make a list of all of the good things that will happen if you quit smoking.
