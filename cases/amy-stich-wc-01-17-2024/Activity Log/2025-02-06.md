Be honest with him about your work situation and if he is not comfortable with placing you at MMI, he may recommend a Home Based exercise program that will allow you to still treat (at your convenience, in your home) and only have to check in & measure progress on occasions. Please be aware that the Insurance company may schedule you a visit with their own independent doctor. We will help ensure that you receive plenty of notice for these appointments, but want you to be aware & can plan accordingly moving forward.
