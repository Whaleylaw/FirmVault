Good morning Amy,
    In the Intake Packet is a Fee Agreement that breaks down how our fees work. First & most important, we do not collect any attorney fees or expenses unless you receive an award. In Workers' Compensation claims, there is a sliding scale fee regulation. An attorney can only collect 20% of the first $25,000.00, 15% of the second $25,000.00 and 10% of the remaining settlement over $50,000.00. The state of Kentucky does cap attorney fees at $18,000.00. Expenses are separate from attorney fees and the attorney is entitled to be reimbursed for those expenses should an award be obtained.
    Now, I think we should be clear that you technically have 2 separate claims. Your workers compensation claim, because you were injured 'on the job' but you also have a premise liability claim against the location of the catering job, so please be sure to send us that information as well as discussed. Never hesitate to ask any questions you may have. We understand this is quite a lot of information and are happy to help you understand the process as we go along.
