To Whom It May Concern,
This law firm has been retained by the above named individual in regard to all claims arising out of the injuries sustained in the above described accident.   Please be so kind as to forward all future correspondence to this office.  Additionally, please provide this office with written acknowledgment of receipt of this letter.  Additionally, if you have obtained any recorded statements or if you have obtained photographs of any vehicles from this collision, please provide this office with copies of these documents.  
Please forward to this office an application for PIP benefits and a wage loss application.  If an application has already been completed, please provide this office with a copy.  We will keep you updated as our client’s medical condition progresses.
Please be advised that my client would like no hospital or emergency room bills to be paid unless specifically instructed to by this office. Additionally, my client would like to reserve $2,000.00 in PIP benefits until further notice. 
Please be advised that no information should be released without my client’s written consent to any person and/or organization other that this law firm and its representatives.  This includes any potential UM or UIM departments or adjusters at your company.  
Please forward to this office a copy of any and all policies including the entire declaration page for any policies that potentially provide PIP benefits to my client. 
It is also my understanding that my client is an insured under one or more policies of insurance with your company which contains provisions for under insured motorist coverage.  As you know, under Kentucky law, under insured motorist coverage constitutes personal insurance and my client is entitled to make claims under these policies if certain contractual requirements are satisfied. 
At this time, my client is pursuing a claim against the tortfeasor for the above described claim.  However, my client sustained very severe injuries in this accident.  Therefore, please allow this letter to serve as notice that my client is reserving their right to pursue an under insured claim under any policy of insurance for which they qualify. At this time, we are attempting to comply with all applicable requirements pertaining to the under insured policies.  Therefore, please forward a complete copy of said policies to our attention along with a short letter outlining any requirements for pursuing any under insured claim and any deadlines for which my client must comply.
I appreciate your attention to this matter and look forward to working with you as we attempt to amicably resolve this claim.  If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact me.  I look forward to hearing from you soon.

Sincerely,
