* * * * * * * * * * *
Comes the Defendant, Estate of Deshawn Ford, by counsel, and for its Answer to Plaintiff’s
Third Amended Complaint herein, states as follows:
FIRST DEFENSE
1. The Third Amended Complaint fails to state a cause of action upon which relief may be 
granted against this Defendant.
SECOND DEFENSE
2. The claims of the Plaintiff are barred and/or limited by KRS 304 subtitle 39, known as the 
Kentucky Motor Vehicle Reparations Act.
THIRD DEFENSE
3. The claims of the Plaintiff may be barred by the applicable statute of limitations.
FOURTH DEFENSE
4. The claims of the Plaintiff may be barred and/or limited due to insufficiency of service of 
process and lack of personal jurisdiction.

Page 2 of 3
FIFTH DEFENSE
5. Admits the allegations contained in numerical paragraphs 1 and 4 of Plaintiff’s Third
Amended Complaint.
6. States that it is without sufficient information to form an opinion as to the truth or falsity 
of the allegations contained in numerical paragraphs 2, 7, 8, 9, and 10 of Plaintiff’s Third 
Amended Complaint and therefore, denies same.
7. Denies the allegations contained in numerical paragraph 3 of Plaintiff’s Third Amended 
Complaint.
8. Denies the allegations of negligence as set forth in numerical paragraph 6 of Plaintiff’s 
Third Amended Complaint and further states that it is without sufficient information to 
form an opinion as to the truth or falsity of the remaining allegations contained therein 
and therefore, denies same.
WHEREFORE, Defendant, Estate of Deshawn Ford, demands as follows:
1. That the Third Amended Complaint of Plaintiff, Anella Noble, against it be dismissed, with 
prejudice.
2. That it recover its costs herein incurred.
3. Trial by Jury of all issues properly so triable.
4. All other proper relief to which it may appear to be entitled.
