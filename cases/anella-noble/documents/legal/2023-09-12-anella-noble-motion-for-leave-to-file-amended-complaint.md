THIRD AMENDED COMPLAINT 
VIRGINIA SEWELL, et al. DEFENDANTS 
* * * * * * * 
Comes the Plaintiff, Anella Noble, by and through counsel, and gives notice that she will, 
on Monday, September 18, 2023, at or around the hour of 10:45 a.m. or as soon thereafter as 
Counsel may be heard, make the Motion, and tender the Order set forth below. 
MOTION FOR LEAVE TO FILE AMENDED COMPLAINT 
Comes the Plaintiff, Anella Noble, by and through counsel, and hereby moves the Court 
for leave to file the attached Amended Complaint. In support hereof, the Plaintiff states as follows: 
Following identification of Defendant Deshawn Ford, Plaintiff filed her Second Amended 
Complaint on May 9, 2023. After service was unsuccessful, it was discovered that Defendant Ford 
passed away on June 4, 2023. Subsequently, Scott Barton, Jefferson County Public Administrator, 
was appointed as Administrator for the Estate of Deshawn Ford on August 30, 2023. A copy of 
the Order Appointing is attached hereto as Exhibit 1. Plaintiff wishes to amend her Complaint to 
substitute Scott Barton as Administrator for the Estate of Deshawn Ford as the proper defendant 
to this action. 
This Amendment is permitted under CR 15.01 and CR 25.01 and is in the interest of justice. 
Further, Plaintiff requests that Summons be issued for Defendant Scott Barton as Administrator 

2 
for the Estate of Deshawn Ford with regard to Plaintiff’s Third Amended Complaint. Plaintiff 
requests that Summons not be issued for Defendants Virginia Sewell or State Farm Mutual 
Automobile Insurance Company, who have already been properly served and remain a party to the 
case, and they shall be deemed served with the Amended Complaint upon filing. 
WHEREFORE, Plaintiff moves the Court for leave to file his Amended Complaint and 
proposes an Order granting this Motion and to request that the Court ensure issued process thereon.
