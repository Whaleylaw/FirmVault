Narrative
Dispatched code 3 on a MVA. Arrived on scene along with LMPD. 2 vehicles were involved, a white suv and a white sedan. Both vehicles did not appear to have any
damage to them. The white sedan had 3 occupants that were female. The patient was a 20 year old female restrained passenger in the back seat. Patient was 37
weeks pregnant with her first child. Patient states that they were stopped at a stop sign when their vehicle got struck in the back by a white suv. The occupants in the
suv jumped out a fled the scene. No airbag deployment in either vehicle. The patient stated that the car just shook. The patient denies head or neck pain. The patient
states that she has been having lower back pain but just wants to make sure that nothing has happen to the baby because of the accident. Patient skin is pink, warm,
and dry. Pupils are PERRL. Lung sounds are clear equal and bilateral. Patient has proper PMS function in all extremities. Patient abdomen is soft, non tender. Patient
denies LOC, SOA, headache, dizziness, weakness, nausea, chest pain or abdomen pain. Patient vitals are within normal limits and are as noted. Patient requested
transport to Baptist East Hospital for further evaluation. Transported code 1 to Baptist East Hospital. Continued to monitor patients vitals and condition without
remarkable changes. Arrived at Baptist East Hospital without any incidents. Transferred care to ED staff along with a verbal patient care report for further evaluation.
