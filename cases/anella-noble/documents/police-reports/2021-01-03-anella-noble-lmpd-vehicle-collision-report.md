video: | have located video responsive to your

Extension for release is necessary because the
requested records require additional steps
before it can be made available for public
release. As the requested record was created
during the course of police business, it is
expected to contain a mixture of exempt and
non-exempt information. The exempt
information is likely of a personal nature of the
involved person(s) or CjlS-protected
information, which is prohibited from release.
To go further into detail which is specific to the
process of researching, locating and redacting

body/dash camera video requests. Currently,
our unit only has four members who are solely
responsible for the entire department's open
records requests where body/dash camera
video are being requested. (As well as any
audio/video which are included in case file
requests). For each request we are assigned we
must conduct a search by officer and date. If
video is located, we must determine whether
an investigation/court case related to the
incident is open. We then must download each
video, one at a time, and then watch and
manually redact any exempted information.
We do this to protect the privacy of our citizens
as well for CJIS security requirements.
Unfortunately, only one item/image/sound may
be redacted at a time; if there are multiple
exempt items, they must be redacted
individually. There is no automatic process to
redact exempt items. For every minute of video
it is estimated it takes 7 minutes of
“redaction/review”. Some requests take as little
as 30 minutes to an hour to complete, while
some can take over a week. And often one
request doesn't equal one video. Some
requests can have two, five, ten, etc.
For all the reasons stated above, pursuant to
KRS 61.872(5), the non-exempt responsive
records will be made available on or before
October 8, 2024.
To remain fair and unbiased to requestors, we
process in the order they are received. Our unit
currently has an estimated 1,000+ pending
requests for video. We are working as diligently
as possible given the resources and personnel
allotted to our unit. If we get to your request
ahead of the quoted date, it will be provided
earlier.
Video may be provided faster via subpoena if
applicable.
