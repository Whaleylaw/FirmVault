Agreement Creating Lien and Assignment by Patient of Proceeds of Recovery
1, ASn\ee Will GQWS (“Patient”), understand and acknowledge thatI am
fully and directly responsible to Kentucky Pain Associates, hereinafter referred to as
“KPA”, for all fees and other charges relating to medical treatment and services provided
to me.
In consideration for KPA agreeing to await payment, and in order to provide KPA with
additional assurance ofpayment and to induce KPA to render services to me, I hereby
irrevocably assign to KPA from the proceeds that may be recovered on my behalf as a
result of any insurance claim, compromise, settlement, arbitration, mediation, lawsuit,
award, judgment or verdict, or any other collection activities on any claim from
compensation for damages for personal injury sustained by me (collectively, “Recovery”),
an amount equal to all such sums as by be due KPA for all medical treatment or services
rendered to me by KPA I, further, grant KPA a lien on the Recovery in the amount owed
for the services provided.
I hereby irrevocably authorize and direct my attorney(s), any and all insurance carriers,
and all others to honor this irrevocable assignment and, upon receipt of an invoice from
KPA, immediately to pay directly to KPA the full amount of all such sums as may be due
and owing KPA, or any balance thereof from any and all monies received or paid on my
behalf as a result of the Recovery.
I further understand that in the event I dispute the amount of any unpaid KPA bills, my
attorney(s) is/are ethically bound to hold moneys in such an amount in attorney’s escrow
or trust account and withhold such amount in dispute from the moneys distributed to me
from any Recovery, until such time as the dispute is settled by a competent authority or
agreement between me and KPA.
I further understand that this Lien and Assignment in no way makes Patient’s obligation
to pay all amounts due KPA contingent upon achieving the Recovery or any agreement or
refusal by any insurance company to pay, in whole or part, any KPA bill(s), and I further
understand that I remain personally responsible for payment of these bills.
The right to recover proceeds assigned under this Lien and Assignment may be assigned
by KPA toa third-party, and Patient authorizes and directs Patient’s attorney(s) and all
others, upon notification of a third-party assignment, to accord the same rights and
privileges to any third---party assignee as attorney(s) or others would accord to KPA
under the terms of this Lien and Assignment.
Amounts payable to KPA will be sent to Attorney and patient in statement form at time of
discharge or request by Attorney. The amount is subject to change as more services are
provided. Patient directs Patient’s attorney(s) and all others notified of this assignment
contact KPA for the final lien amount owed to KPA prior to disbursing any proceeds to
Patient. KPA will not provide any bill or record to any attorney without attaching a copy
of this Lien and Assignment and receipt of any bill from KPA will acknowledge receipt
and notification of this assignment.
