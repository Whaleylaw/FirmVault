Record request #24-922 has been closed. The closure reason supplied was:

*This comes as an update on your attached open records request. Specific to
the request for body camera video. Our video records specialist identified
video responsive to this request but those records are denied release
through open records pursuant to KRS 61.878(1)(h) in conjunction with KRS
17.150(2). LMPD is a law enforcement agency. The requested records were
compiled in the process of detecting/investigating a statutory violation
and is part of a pending criminal case in Jefferson County District Court,
case number *23-T-026891*. Any premature release of these records could
jeopardize the pending prosecution by identifying witnesses not otherwise
known and tipping them off to the direction of the ongoing criminal case,
impact witness recollection of the incident, and taint the jury pool by
permitting the “case” be tried in the court of public opinion rather than
in court with the benefit of procedural and evidentiary rules. Open Records
is not meant to circumvent the discovery process. This request will now be
closed.*
