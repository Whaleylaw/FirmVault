Comes the Plaintiff, Caryn McCay, by and through counsel, and requests the Defendant, Allstate Property  Casualty Insurance Company (known hereafter as “Allstate”), to answer the following Interrogatories and Requests for Production of Documents within thirty (30) days of service hereof, in accordance with all applicable Rules of Civil Procedure.

These Interrogatories shall be deemed continuing. Knowledge of the attorneys for the Defendant or the claims agent or any other agent of the Defendant, or of any employee of the Defendant, shall be deemed to be the knowledge of the Defendant. Anything coming to your knowledge after filing an Answer to the following Interrogatories which would change your original answers or responses should be contained in an Amended Answer or an Amended Response and served upon Plaintiffs’ attorneys immediately after the same is brought to your attention.

**These Interrogatories shall not be deemed Answered by any response which refers Plaintiffs to the policy and/or declarations page attached thereto. This Interrogatory seeks from the Defendant its understanding and declarations concerning the status of coverage, amount of coverage, and other issues relative to coverage involved in this matter.**
