Cervical Sprain
A cervical sprain is also called a neck sprain. It is a stretch or tear in one or more ligaments in the neck. Ligaments are tissues that
connect bones to each other.
Neck sprains can be mild, bad, or very bad. A very bad sprain in the neck can cause the bones in the neck to be unstable. This can

What is a heart attack?
A heart attack is damage or death to a part of the heart from severely decreased or lack of blood flow to the heart. Over time, arteries

• Heart attack is the leading cause of death in the United States.
