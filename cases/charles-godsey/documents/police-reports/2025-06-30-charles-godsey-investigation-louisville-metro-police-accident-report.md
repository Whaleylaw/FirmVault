Officers were dispatched to the listed location on a non-injury accident. Upon arrival, officers spoke with
both units. Unit 1 stated that she was traveling SB on Hazelwood Ave when due to inattention, was unable
to stop due to wet road and hit Unit 2 in the right passenger side of the listed vehicle. Unit 1 stated that she
did not need EMS on scene. Unit 2 stated he was traveling SB on Hazelwood Ave, turning into his driveway
when Unit 1 hit him in the right passenger side of the vehicle. Unit 2 vehicle refused EMS. Both vehicle had
moderate damage to them.
