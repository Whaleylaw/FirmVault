Comes the Plaintiff, by counsel, and gives notice that he will, on Monday, October 14,

the instant Motion to Compel, pursuant to C.R. 37.01 and all other applicable rules of the Court, 
and tender the Order set forth below. 
MOTION TO COMPEL DISCOVERY RESPONSES 
Comes now the Plaintiff, Charles Johnson, by and through counsel, and pursuant to all 
applicable Civil Rules and/or Local Rules hereby move the Court for an Order compelling the 
Defendant Robert Lowe to respond fully to Plaintiff’s outstanding written discovery requests. In 
support thereof, Plaintiff states as follows: 
Plaintiff served his First Set of Interrogatories and Requests for Production of Documents 
upon Defendant Robert Lowe contemporaneously with the Complaint on April 10, 2024. On June 
25, 2024, Defendant Lowe by counsel filed his Answer to the Complaint, but did not respond to 
the discovery requests. Upon request, Plaintiff served a Microsoft Word version to defendant’s 
counsel on August 26, 2024. 
On September 3, 2024, Plaintiff’s counsel attempted to follow up on these outstanding 
responses via email to defense counsel. Thereafter, on September 6, 2024, counsel for Defendant 
Lowe sent an email to the undersigned, indicating their intent to have responses to discovery within 

2 
the coming weeks. However, these answers were not received. Despite good faith attempts by the 
undersigned to resolve this discovery dispute and obtain responses to Plaintiff’s discovery from 
the Defendant, they have not been obtained to date. 
Plaintiffs’ counsel affirmatively states that he has attempted to confer in good faith with 
Defendant’s counsel in an effort to obtain the responses identified herein, as detailed above, and 
believes that only a Court Order will result a due and sufficient response by the Defendant. An 
Order is appropriate under these circumstances pursuant to CR 37.01. 
WHEREFORE, the Plaintiff, by counsel, moved the Court to enter her tendered Order.
