Narrative
EMS dispatched for a motor vehicle accident. On arrival one patient is found sitting in drivers seat of involved vehicle. Pt is A&O x4 and able to answer all questions
appropriately. Pt is able to recall events of accident and denies any loss of consciousness. Displays no signs of altered mental status. Pt is c/o traumatic thoracic pain,
as well as pain to bilateral lower extremities. Pt states he was restrained, airbags did deploy and hit patient in the chest. Pt denies any head or neck pain or
tenderness. Pt was up and walking prior to arrival and did not require extrication. No intrusion into passenger compartment seen. Windshield is intact. He is able to
ambulate without difficulty and describes no additional pain doing so. Pt reports it is painful to take a deep breath. Lung sounds are clear and equal. Seatbelt sign is
not present. No abdominal swelling or tenderness noted. On assessment pt is found to have abrasions immediately inferior to both knees. No other bleeding,
deformity, or obvious injury noted. All times estimated.
