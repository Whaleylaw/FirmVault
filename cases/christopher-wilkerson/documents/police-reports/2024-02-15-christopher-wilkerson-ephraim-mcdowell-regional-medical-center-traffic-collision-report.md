On the [DOB-1] at approximately 0955 hours, deputies were dispatched to the area of US-127 in
reference to an overturned dump truck near the Lincoln County line. Unit 1 was resting on the passenger
side in the north bound lane with the front facing the east side of the roadway. Unit 1 was traveling south.
The operator of Unit 1 stated that a black Kia passenger car was traveling in front of him and "slammed on
the brakes" causing him to make evasive maneuvers to avoid striking the rear of the black Kia. The
operator said that his weight shifted causing him to overturn. A large amount of gravel was observed in the
roadway at the time of response. The operator of Unit 1was transported to Ephraim McDowell Hospital for
treatment of possible minor injuries.
Deputies interviewed two witnesses and they stated that they observed Unit 1 tailgating the black Kia
passenger car while blowing his air horn. Witnesses stated that the operator of Unit 1was upset that the
Kia cut him off as the two southbound lanes merged into one lane. Parts of the roadway had to be repaired
by the Kentucky Department of Transportation due to damage sustained during the collision. The dump
truck was towed by Skinner's Towing.
