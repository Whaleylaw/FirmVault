Good morning Mr. Tawfik,

I hope this email finds you well. I’m writing with a concern regarding the release for my client, Cire Flowers, Sr., in the settlement of the above-named case. Specifically, the indemnity language concerning PIP subrogation claims is causing some unexpected challenges.
As you’ll recall, our pre-mediation demands clearly stated that any settlement would be exclusive of PIP or indemnity obligations. While I don’t believe this issue came up during mediation discussions, I’m certain it was not part of the resolution we contemplated —particularly given the limited funds available between multiple claimants.
Interestingly, the release for Cire Flowers, Jr. does not contain this indemnity language concerning PIP liens, creating a discrepancy between the two documents. To address this and better align the release with the parties’ intentions, I kindly request that the release for Cire Flowers, Sr. be amended to remove the indemnity language related to PIP subrogation claims.
I have attached both releases for your reference. Please let me know if you are willing to make this amendment or if further discussion is necessary to resolve this matter.
Thank you for your attention to this issue, and I look forward to your response.
