Come the Plaintiffs, Colleen Colvin and Shannon Colvin, by counsel, and for their cause 
of action against the Defendant, states as follows: 
1. The Plaintiffs, Colleen Colvin and Shannon Colvin, at all times relevant to the events 
stated herein, were residents of Radcliff, Hardin County, Kentucky, residing at the address set forth 
above. 
2. Upon information and belief, at all relevant times, the Defendant, Paul Rafferty 
(hereinafter “Defendant Rafferty”), was a resident of Greensburg, Green County, Kentucky, 
residing at the address set forth above. 

2 
3. On or about October 1, 2023, Defendant Rafferty did so negligently and carelessly 
operate their motor vehicle, a certain 2020 Jeep Compass, Vin No. 3C4NJDAB3LT205250 at the 
intersection of East Dixie Avenue and Hodgenville Road in Elizabethtown, Hardin County, 
Kentucky thereby colliding Plaintiffs’ vehicle. 
4. The negligence of Defendant Rafferty in the operation of their vehicle on the date 
and at the place aforesaid was a substantial factor in causing Plaintiffs serious and permanent 
injuries, including great pain of body and mind, both past and future. In addition, the Plaintiffs 
have incurred medical expenses as a result of the collision and injuries they sustained and will 
incur future medical expenses for the treatment of their injuries. Further, Plaintiffs lost time and 
money from their gainful employment, resulting in damages for past lost wages and has had their 
future ability to labor and earn money substantially impaired. 
5. The aforementioned damages incurred by Plaintiffs are a sum in excess of the 
minimal amount necessary to confer jurisdiction upon this Court. 
6. Pursuant to KRS 304.39-062(b), threshold requirements have been met by the 
Plaintiffs in that Plaintiffs have incurred over $1,000.00 in medical expenses and sustained serious 
injuries. 
WHEREFORE, Plaintiffs, by counsel, demand as follows: 
1. Judgment against the Defendant in such an amount which will fairly and reasonably 
compensate Plaintiffs for the damages alleged herein; 
2. Prejudgment interest; 
 3. Post judgment interest; 
 4. Attorneys fees and court costs reasonably incurred or expended; 
 5. Trial by jury on all issues so triable; 

3 
 6. Punitive damages to the extent a trier of fact determines the actions of the Defendants 
were malicious, oppressive, fraudulent, or reckless; and, 
 7. All just and proper relief to which she may appear entitled, including the right to 
amend this Complaint.
