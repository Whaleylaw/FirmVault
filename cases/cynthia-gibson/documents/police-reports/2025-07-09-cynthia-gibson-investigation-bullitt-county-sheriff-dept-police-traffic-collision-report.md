Compton, Terry 1 OF 4

Dispatched to a 2 vehicle collision on I65 North at the 121mm.
Upon arrival BCEMS had removed the operator of Unit 2 from the vehicle & was in the process of loading
her in the Squad for transport to University of Louisville for treatment of possible minor injuries.
Both parties in Unit 1 refused EMS.
Operator of Unit 2 stated that Unit 1 appeared to be traveling too fast, when approaching another vehicle in
the #1 (Fast) lane which had applied their brakes, Unit 1 was unable to stop & spun out of control. After
spinning from the #1 lane into the path of Unit 2 which was traveling North in the #2 (Middle) Unit 1 made
contact with Unit 2 causing severe damage to both vehicles.
Unit 1 sustained severe damage to the passenger front tire area.
Unit 2 sustained severe damage to the front passenger tire area also.
Both Units had to be towed due to the damage sustained.
Unit 1 was towed to the BCSO tow lot via Anthonys.
Unit 2 was towed to the BCSO tow lot via A Cheap Tow.
