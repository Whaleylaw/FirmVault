Come the Plaintiffs, Cecil Finley and Dana Jackson, by counsel, and for their causes of 
action against the Defendant, states as follows: 
1. The Plaintiff, Cecil Finley, at all times relevant to the events stated herein, was a 
resident of Louisville, Jefferson County, Kentucky, residing at the address set forth above. 
2. The Plaintiff, Dana Jackson, at all times relevant to the events stated herein, was a 
resident of Glen Allen, Henrico, Virginia, residing at the address set forth above. 
3. Upon information and belief, Defendant Justin Kiebler (hereinafter “Defendant 
Kiebler”) was a resident of Louisville, Jefferson County, Kentucky, residing at the address set 
forth above. 

2 
4. On or about January 24, 2024, Defendant Kiebler did so negligently and carelessly 
operate his motor vehicle, a certain 2018 Toyota Corolla, Vin No. 2T1BURHE7JC993375, on at 
or around 4525 S. 3rd Street in Louisville, Kentucky thereby causing a collision with Plaintiffs’ 
vehicle. 
5. The negligence of Defendant Kiebler in the operation of his vehicle on the date and 
at the place aforesaid was a substantial factor in causing Plaintiffs serious and permanent injuries, 
including great pain of body and mind, both past and future. In addition, the Plaintiffs have 
incurred medical expenses as a result of the collision and injuries they sustained and will incur 
future medical expenses for the treatment of their injuries. Further, Plaintiffs lost time and money 
from their gainful employment, resulting in damages for past lost wages and has had their future 
ability to labor and earn money substantially impaired. 
6. The aforementioned damages incurred by Plaintiffs are a sum in excess of the 
minimal amount necessary to confer jurisdiction upon this Court. 
7. Pursuant to KRS 304.39-062(b), threshold requirements have been met by the 
Plaintiffs in that Plaintiffs have incurred over $1,000.00 in medical expenses and sustained serious 
injuries. 
WHEREFORE, Plaintiffs, by counsel, demand as follows: 
1. Judgment against the Defendant in such an amount which will fairly and reasonably 
compensate Plaintiffs for the damages alleged herein; 
2. Prejudgment interest; 
 3. Post judgment interest; 
 4. Attorneys fees and court costs reasonably incurred or expended; 
 5. Trial by jury on all issues so triable; 

3 
 6. Punitive damages to the extent a trier of fact determines the actions of the Defendant 
were malicious, oppressive, fraudulent, or reckless; and, 
 7. All just and proper relief to which Plaintiffs may appear entitled, including the right 
to amend this Complaint.
