Comes the Plaintiff, Cecil Finley, by and through counsel, and gives notice that he will, on 
Monday, October 28th, 2024, at or around the hour of 1:00 p.m. or as soon thereafter as Counsel 
may be heard, make the Motion and tender the Order set forth below. 
MOTION FOR LEAVE TO FILE AMENDED COMPLAINT 
Comes the Plaintiff, Cecil Finley, by and through counsel, and hereby moves the Court for 
leave to file the attached Amended Complaint. In support hereof, the Plaintiff states as follows: 
Plaintiff wishes to add Dana Jackson as a plaintiff to this action. Dana Jackson was a 
passenger in the vehicle being driven by Plaintiff Cecil Finley at the time of the collision at issue 
herein. Defendant Justin Kiebler has been properly served with the initial Complaint but has not 
filed any responsive pleadings. 
This Amendment is permitted under KRCP 15.01 and is in the interest of justice. Further, 
Plaintiff requests that Summons not be issued for Defendant Justin Kiebler, who has already been 
properly served, and that he shall be deemed served with the Amended Complaint upon filing. 
WHEREFORE, Plaintiff moves the Court for leave to file his Amended Complaint and 
proposes an Order granting this Motion and to request that the Court ensure issued process thereon.
