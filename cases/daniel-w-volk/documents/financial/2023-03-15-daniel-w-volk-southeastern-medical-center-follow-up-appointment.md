Additional Services Your Policy Provides: Preferred Provider Network

As part of our continuing effort to provide full service to our policyholders to assist you in finding the proper medical care, you are eligible for access to a Preferred Provider Network (PPN) of doctors and facilities under your medical coverage provided by your personal auto policy.

What is a PPN?

A PPN is a group of medical service providers who have joined a network and agreed to accept pre-arranged discounts for necessary medical services.

What does this mean for you?

Our PPN network can offer assistance in locating a highly qualified physician in your area. The PPN network has contracted with a group of doctors and facilities (labs, x-ray facilities, specialists, etc.) that meet rigorous credentialing requirements and provide you simple and easy access to these services and professionals. If you presently have limited medical coverage on your policy, the use of PPN providers can extend the limit of that coverage. For example: Presently: If you have $1,000 of medical coverage under your policy and a non-PPN provider provides medical treatment that totals $1,000, your policy's medical benefit would then be exhausted. Using a PPN doctor: If you receive medical treatment from a medical provider that had contracted for a 20% discount below the charged amount of $1,000, $800 would be paid for the same treatment and $200 would remain available under the medical benefit.*

*This is only one example as each medical service provided and each medical provider's contract status will determine what reduction, if any, in medical cost will apply. Please keep in mind that there is no cost to you tor this service and you are not required to use this service.

Where is the nearest PPN provider that you could receive treatment with?

Please contact Coventry Integrated Network at 1-[PHONE-10] between 8 a.m. and 5 p.m., Monday through Friday and inform them you are a Safeco policyholder and the type of treatment you require and where you would like to be treated. They will provide you the names of local medical providers from which to choose. You may also visit www.coventryautosolutions.com. Select the "Locate a Provider" link (upper righthand corner) to search for a medical provider near you. When you arrive for your scheduled appointment, show the provider this notice, which includes important facts and telephone numbers. OR When accessing this site, click on "Client Login" and, once in the Client Login window, enter Client ID "LMAUTO" to search for a conveniently located medical provider.

The next step is to schedule your visit with the provider you chose. Remember to take this notice with you to all visits. Safeco Insurance reserves the right to refuse payment if coverage issues arise or if the policy was canceled before the accident occurred.

Your personal injury and medical benefits and the premium you pay on your policy will not change whether or not you select a medical provider from this voluntary program or one of your own choice or any combination of the above. There will be no penalties imposed if you chose a provider who is not included in the network.

Your Safeco Claims Representative will be available to answer any of your questions and will follow up with you regarding any further assistance you may need.
