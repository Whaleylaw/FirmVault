Texas Roadhouse takes every customer’s claim seriously and we complete a full investigation into every 
claim made. As you can appreciate, we cannot pay every claim presented to Texas Roadhouse. But when 
our investigation leads us to believe Texas Roadhouse is responsible, we pay what we owe. When our 
investigation shows Texas Roadhouse is not responsible, we will advise you of the same. The results of

Texas Roadhouse does not have a medical payments provision under our policy of insurance, and we do 
not pay medical providers as a health insurer. When a guest makes a claim, responsibility is determined, 
and if we accept responsibility, we gather all medical records and evaluate the claim before any offer of 
payment is made. The offer is the entire amount we will pay for the claim, regardless of how many 
medical providers, or lien holders, exist within the claim. The guest is responsible for satisfying any bills 
or liens that exist against them.
