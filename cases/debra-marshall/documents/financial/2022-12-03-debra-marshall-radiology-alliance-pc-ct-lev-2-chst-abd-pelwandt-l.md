369.107 Legal recognition of electronic records, electronic signatures, and

(1) (2)

electronic contracts.

A record or signature may not be denied legal effect or enforceability solely because it is in electronic form.

A contract may not be denied legal effect or enforceability solely because an electronic record was used in its formation.

Ifa law requires a record to be in writing, an electronic record satisfies the law.

Ifa law requires a signature, an electronic signature satisfies the law.
