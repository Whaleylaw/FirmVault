MOTION TO CONSOLIDATE 
Comes now the Defendant, Debra Marshall, by and through counsel, and hereby moves the 
Court, pursuant to CR 42.01, to consolidate the instant action with the Barren Circuit Court action 
styled Debra Marshall v. Jasmine Reed, Civil Action No. 23-CI-00659. 
As grounds for same, Defendant states that she filed her Complaint for damages against 
Jasmine Reed on December 7, 2023. Jasmine Reed was the driver of the vehicle owned by Edie 
Renick and insured by Kentucky Farm Bureau Mutual Insurance Company. Jasmine Reed was 
served with the Complaint via certified mail on December 13, 2023. 
Both cases arise from the same automobile accident that occurred on December 3, 2022, 
in Barren County, Kentucky. Furthermore, Defendant Marshall states that resolutions of the 
claims, in their entirety, involves resolution of common issues of fact and law regarding liability 

2
for the accident and damages allegedly suffered from the accident. As these claims involve 
common questions of fact and law, they are appropriate for consolidation by this Court. 
WHEREFORE, the Defendant, Debra Marshall, respectfully requests the court grant the 
instant motion and tender the attached Order.
