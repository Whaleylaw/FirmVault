COMMONWEALTH OF KENTUCKY 
BARREN CIRCUIT COURT 
CASE NO. 23-CI-00659 
DEBRA MARSHALL PLAINTIFF 
v. 
JASMINE REED DEFENDANT 
PLAINTIFF’S FIRST SET OF REQUESTS FOR 
ADMISSION TO DEFENDANT JASMINE REED 
*** *** *** 
Comes the Plaintiff, Debra Marshall, by and through counsel, and requests the Defendant, 
Jasmine Reed (hereinafter “Reed” or “Defendant Reed”), to answer under oath the following 
Requests for Admission within thirty (30) days of service hereof in accordance with the Kentucky 
Rules of Civil Procedure as these requests are being served simultaneously with the Complaint. 
These Requests for Admission shall be deemed continuing. Knowledge of the attorneys for 
the Defendant or the claims agent or any other agent of the Defendant, or of any employee or agent 
of the Defendant, shall be deemed to be the knowledge of the Defendant. Anything coming to your 
knowledge after filing Answers to these Interrogatories and Responses to these Requests for 
Production which would change your original answer or responses should be contained in an 
Amended Answer or an Amended Response and served upon Plaintiff’s attorneys immediately 
after the same is brought to your attention. 
DEFINITIONS 
 A. The words "and" and "or" shall be construed conjunctively or disjunctively as 
necessary to make the request inclusive rather than exclusive. 
 B. The use of the words "include(s)" and "including" shall be construed to mean 
"without limitation."

 C. The words "related to" and "relating" mean relating to, referring to, reflecting, 
pertaining to, evidencing, or any way relevant to any issue, claim, defense, or question posed by 
the interrogatory or request within the meaning of CR 26.02(1). 
 D. “Document” as used herein means anything created or reduced to writing whether 
produced on paper or by electronic mean including but not limited to letters, faxes, emails, 
telephone messages, texts, tweets, memoranda, data stored on any computer hardware or software, 
in the cloud, on an electronic system or otherwise. 
 E. Plural shall include the singular and the singular shall include the plural. 
 F. The term "present” used herein to refer to a point in time shall mean the date upon 
which Responses to the Requests are being made. 
 G. “You” or “Your” or “Defendant” as used herein means the Defendant Jasmine Reed 
and any all of the Defendant’s employees, agents, or third persons who are under the supervision 
or control of the Defendant. 
 H. “Identify” or “explain” or “state” as used in these Interrogatories with respect to 
any person or thing means to state fully all who or what the thing or person is and if relating to a 
person to give the person’s full name, address, and other contact information to the extent known 
or available to the Defendant or other person contributing to the answers and responses and for 
that person to state and explain fully all of the information that person or persons has in connection 
with the subject matter of the request herein. 
I. The “incident” or “collision” as used in these Interrogatories refers to the collision 
Plaintiff was involved in with Defendant Reed which occurred on Dixie Highway in Cave City, 
Barren County, Kentucky on December 3, 2022, as alleged in the Complaint.
