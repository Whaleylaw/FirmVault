MOTION TO COMPEL DISCOVERY RESPONSES 
Comes now the Plaintiff, Debra Marshall, by and through counsel, and pursuant to all 
applicable Civil Rules and/or Local Rules hereby move the Court for an Order compelling the 
Defendant Jasmine Reed to respond fully to Plaintiff’s outstanding written discovery requests. In 
support thereof, Plaintiff states as follows: 
Plaintiff served her First Set of Interrogatories and Requests for Production of Documents 
upon Defendant Jasmine Reed contemporaneously with the Complaint on December 7, 2023. On 
January 26, 2024, counsel for Defendant Reed requested a thirty-day extension to respond to the 
outstanding requests. After said extension had lapsed, Plaintiff’s counsel followed up via email on 
March 19, April 9, and May 31, 2024. Despite good faith attempts by the undersigned to resolve 
this discovery dispute and obtain responses to Plaintiff’s discovery from the Defendant, they have 
not been obtained to date. 

2 
Plaintiffs’ counsel affirmatively states that he has attempted to confer in good faith with 
Defendant’s counsel in an effort to obtain the responses identified herein, as detailed above, and 
believes that only a Court Order will result a due and sufficient response by the Defendant. An 
Order is appropriate under these circumstances pursuant to CR 37.01. 
WHEREFORE, the Plaintiff, by counsel, moved the Court to enter her tendered Order.
