that Stephanie Nail was a listed driver on this policy. Theperson operating the2014 Chevrolet SS
at the time ofthe motor vehicle accident was not identified.
IL.

either specifically denied or is denied as Allstate lacks sufficient information to respond to the
same.
Il.
States that the Complaint fails to state a cause of action against Allstate upon which relief
may be granted.

IV.
Allstate is without sufficient information to admit or deny the allegations asserted against
Defendant Stephanie M. Nail
V.
Based upon information and belief, it has been asserted that the foregoing 2014 Chevrolet
SS insured by Allstate had been stolen and/or converted from the possession ofStephanie M. Nail
and was being operated bythe unknown thief at the time of the foregoing motor vehicle accident
and, to that extent, no liability coverage is warranted under the applicable Allstate policy for any
such claims arising out of this accident.
Vi.
Allstate affirmatively asserts that the Plaintiffs lack standing to assert any claims against
Allstate in its capacity as the insurer ofthe 2014 Chevrolet SS at issue.
VIL.
Allstate relies affirmatively on all applicable policy defenses, terms, and conditions set
forth in the relevant Allstate policy incorporated herein by reference.
