MOTION
Comes the Defendant, Allstate Insurance Company, by counsel, and moves the Court to
enter the following Order bifurcating the claims asserted in the Plaintiffs’ Amended Complaint
against said Defendant alleging "bad faith" or a violation of the Kentucky Unfair Settlement
Practices Act, K.R.S. 304.12-230.
It isnow well-settled inKentucky that such claims are to be bifurcated andheld in abeyance
pending the outcome ofthe merits ofthe case filed against the tortfeasor as a matter of judicial
economy and to avoid any potential resulting prejudice. Wittmer v. Jones, Ky., 864 S.W.2d 885.
