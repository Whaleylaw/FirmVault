Come the Plaintiffs, by counsel, and gives notice that they will, on Monday, August 21, 2023, at or around the hour of 8:45 a.m. EST, or as soon thereafter as Counsel may be heard, make the instant Motion to Compel, pursuant to C.R. 37.01(b) and all other applicable rules of the Court, and tender the Order set forth below.

MOTION TO COMPEL DESPOTION OF DEFENDANT NAIL

Come now the Plaintiffs, Destiny Adkins and Juanita Downs, by and through counsel, and pursuant to all applicable Civil Rules and/or Local Rules hereby move the Court for an Order compelling the Defendant Stephanie Nail to attend and participate in a deposition.

In support, Plaintiffs state that they first arranged the deposition of Defendant Nail on June 6, 2023, via email to her counsel of record. After some scheduling discussion, Defendant’s counsel agreed to dates for Plaintiffs’ and the Defendant’s depositions. Exhibit 1.

On June 6, 2023, by counsel, Plaintiffs filed a Notice to Take Deposition of Defendant Stephanie Nail. Exhibit 2. This deposition was scheduled for July 12, 2023, at the defense counsel’s office. Defendant Nail did not appear for this deposition, and it was subsequently cancelled.

Exhibit 3. Another written request to defense counsel on July 27, 2023, again requesting dates to




reschedule said deposition. Exhibit 4. To date, there has been no response.

Plaintiffs’ counsel affirmatively states that he has attempted to confer in good faith with Defendant’s counsel in an effort to obtain the deposition identified herein, as detailed above, and believes that only a Court Order will result a due and sufficient response by the Defendant. An Order is appropriate under these circumstances pursuant to CR 37.01.

WHEREFORE, the Plaintiff, by counsel, moved the Court to enter her tendered Order.
