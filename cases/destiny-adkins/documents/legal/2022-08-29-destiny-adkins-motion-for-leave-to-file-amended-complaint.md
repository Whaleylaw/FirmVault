AMENDED PERSONAL INJURY COMPLAINT 
NEGLIGENCE, FRAUD, NEGLIGENT ENTRUSTMENT, BAD FAITH 
Comes the Plaintiffs, Destiny Adkins & Juanita Downs, by Counsel, and for their 
Amended Complaint against the above- named Defendants, states as follows: 
1. The Plaintiff adopts, reiterates, and incorporates by reference, as though fully set out 
herein, each and every allegation of her original Complaint including numerical 
paragraphs 1 – 15 of the Original Complaint. 

2. Plaintiff adopts and reiterates each and every allegation as if set forth fully herein and 
incorporate the same by reference. 
3. The liability of Allstate’s insured in this instance is clear and the resulting medical 
treatment of Plaintiff, reasonable and necessary, and otherwise, Allstate is obligated to 
make a good faith effort to settle Plaintiff’s claim against its insured and has failed to do 
so. 
4. That Allstate has effectively denied such claim by refusing to make a good faith attempt 
at compromising Plaintiff’s claim against its insured and such denial lacks a reasonable 
basis in law or fact and was made with Allstate’s knowledge that no reasonable basis exists 
for such denial or was made with a reckless disregard for whether such a claim exists and 
otherwise, Allstate’s acts and omissions amount to a bad faith attempt at settling a valid 
claim on behalf of its insured. 
5. The acts and omissions of Allstate in the handling of Plaintiff’s claim against its insured 
were intended to harass and deceive Plaintiff and its insured. 
6. The acts and omissions of Allstate in the handling of Plaintiff’s claim against its insured 
was made with oppression, fraud and malice and further, is conduct that amounts to a 
reckless indifference to its insured and others and is compensable under common law bad 
faith and as a violation of Kentucky statutes and specifically, The Unfair Claims 
Settlement Practices act, for which Allstate is liable to Plaintiff. 
WHEREFORE, Plaintiffs demand as follows: 
1. Judgment against the Defendants, jointly and individually, in an amount which 
will fairly and reasonably compensate the Plaintiff for his damages alleged 

herein; 
2. Trial by jury; 
3. Plaintiffs’ costs herein expended; and 
4. Any and all other relief which the Plaintiff may appear to be entitled, including 
the right to amend this Complaint, if necessary.
