MOTION TO SUBSTITUTE 
* * * * * 
Come now the Plaintiffs, by counsel, and respectfully request the defendant, Unknown 
Driver, be dismissed from this action and substituted with the proper individual, Demetrius Turner. 
The undersigned states that due investigation has revealed that Defendant Nail’s significant 
other at the time of the incident herein was Demetrius Turner. Defendant Nail testified in her 
deposition that she and Demetrius Turner had been dating one another for at least six years and 
were in a relationship at the time of the subject collision. Demetrius Turner is also the father of 
Defendant Nail’s infant daughter. 
As Defendant Nail further testified during her deposition, Demetrius Turner had previously 
driven her car less than two months prior to the subject collision, during which time he was charged 
with operating a vehicle on a suspended license. Upon due inquiry, Plaintiffs believe Demetrius 
Turner may have been responsible for the collision and damages at issue herein. 

Importantly, Plaintiff Juanita Downs’s claim is still within the Statute of Limitations in 
accordance with the KMVRA at the time of filing the instant Motion and Amended Complaint. 
WHEREFORE, the Plaintiffs move the court to enter their tendered Order permitting them 
to file the Amended Complaint to substitute the proper party in this action and for an Order 
directing the Court Clerk to issue process, such amendment to relate back to the original filing date 
of the Complaint.
