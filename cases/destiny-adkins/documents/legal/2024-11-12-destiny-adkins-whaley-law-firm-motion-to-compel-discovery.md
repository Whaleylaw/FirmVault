 Come the Plaintiffs, by counsel, and gives notice that they will, on Monday, November 25,

MOTION TO COMPEL DISCOVERY REQUESTS 
 Plaintiffs, Destiny Adkins and Juanita Downs, by and through counsel, and pursuant to all 
applicable Civil Rules and/or Local Rules hereby moves the Court for an Order compelling 
Defendant Demetrius Turner to respond fully to Plaintiff’s outstanding written discovery requests. 
In support thereof, Plaintiff states as follows: 
 Plaintiffs, by counsel, served their First Set of Discovery Requests upon Defendant Turner 
on August 28, 2024. Thereafter, on September 17, 2024, counsel for Defendant Turner provided 
responses to only Plaintiff’s Requests for Admission. On October 22, 2024, the undersigned 
attempted to follow up on the outstanding discovery responses via email to defense counsel. After 
receiving no response, the undersigned sent another follow-up email on November 1, 2024. To 
date, the outstanding responses have not been received by the undersigned. 

 Plaintiff’s counsel affirmatively states that he has attempted in good faith with Defendant’s 
counsel in an effort to obtain the responses identified herein, as detailed above, and believes that 
only a Court Order will result a due and sufficient response by the Defendant. An Order is 
appropriate under these circumstances pursuant to CR 37.01 
WHEREFORE, the Plaintiff, by counsel, moves the Court to enter the tendered Order.
