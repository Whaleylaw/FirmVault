Officer dispatched to the listed location on injury accident involving two vehicles. Upon arrival officer
located both unit 1 and unit 2 with severe damage and an LG&E pole with damage. Four of the vehicle
occupants were suffering from multiple different injuries. Officers spoke to the driver of unit 1 who stated
that she was attempting to stop at the intersection but her brakes were not working. Officers spoke to the
driver of unit 2 who stated that he had a green light and unit 1 driver failed to yeild the right of way causing
the collision. Unit 1 was traveling east on Dumesnil St. at the Cypress St. intersection. Unit 2 was
traveling south on Cypress St. at Dumesnil St. intersection.
