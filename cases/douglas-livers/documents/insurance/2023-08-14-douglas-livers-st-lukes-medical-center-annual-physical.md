Re: Douglas Livers v Allstate Property & Casualty Ins. Co. Dear Aaron and Bryce:

You should be aware that Allstate Property and Casualty Insurance Company’s investigation and the defense of the claims made by Mr. Douglas Livers is being conducted under a reservation of rights. Allstate, in investigating, evaluating or negotiating for any compromised settlement or in any way acting or failing to act, does not intend to waive any of its rights or admit any of its obligations under the terms and conditions of the auto policy issued to Rodney Kellams, Policy No. [POLICY-1] or under any applicable law.

Allstate is making this reservation of rights because coverage may not exist under the policy for the following reasons:

Mr. Douglas Livers was employed by Mr. Kellams and working in the course and scope of his employment when this automobile accident happened. Mr. Livers has testified under oath that he had been working for Mr. Kellams for at least a year before this accident happened, and he was using Mr. Kellams truck (that was insured under Mr. Kellams personal auto policy) in the course of his employment with Mr. Kellams. Mr. Livers has testified that he was on the way to pick up another employee and then go to a storage unit to receive instructions from Mr. Kellams about the day’s work when he was involved in an accident on January 19, 2023.

The information known to date suggests the at fault driver was uninsured. A copy of the police report is attached along with the policy.




Mr. Kellams policy contains certain exclusions of coverage for the claim made by Mr. Livers, including the following under Uninsured Motorists Insurance coverage SS- ACR108:

Exclusions-What is not Covered We will not pay any damages an insured person is legally entitled to recover because of:...

4. bodily injury - if the payment would directly or indirectly benefit any worker’s compensation or disability benefits insurer, or a self-insurer; and

8. bodily injury or property damage arising out of the use of:

a) an insured auto while used by an insured person to carry persons, products or property for any form of compensation, including, but not limited to fees, delivery charges or wages generally. . .

Additionally, on page 6 of 16 the General Provisions of the policy it states as follows:

Fraud or Misrepresentation

The policy is void from the inception if it was obtained or renewed through material representation, fraud or concealment of material fact. That means that we will not be liable for any claims or damages that would otherwise be covered.

Please note that nothing that Allstate does in the handling of the claims made by Mr. Livers related to this accident, including but not limited to its coverage investigation, should be deemed as a waiver of any rights that Allstate has under the policy at law. Other coverage defenses may exist which have not yet come to Allstate’s attention. Allstate, therefore, reserves the right to add any additional defenses should they become known during the investigation of this claim.

Additionally, please be advised that the service of this notice upon you does not deprive you of any rights that you may have against the company, and your acknowledgement of this notice shall not be considered a waiver of your rights, if any, under said policy.
