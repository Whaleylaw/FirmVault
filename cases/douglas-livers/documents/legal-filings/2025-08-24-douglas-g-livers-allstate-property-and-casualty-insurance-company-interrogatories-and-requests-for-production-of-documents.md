4, If you are claiming future medical, hospital and related
expenses, please provide a complete explanation for each such
claimed expense. For example, please state the injury or condition

and address of each doctor, chiropractor, osteopath, clinic,
hospital or service which has advised you that future expense will

5. If you are claiming lost wages incurred to date, for
said claim, fully explain the claimed loss. For example, please
state the following:

(a) The name, address and business of each of your
employers;
(b) Time (in hours) that was lost from each employment
as a result of this accident;
(c) The date(s) that such time was lost from each
employment;
(d) The gross amount of wages or earnings lost from
each employment;
(e) The net take-home pay lost from each employment;
(f) The hours per day and days per week you worked (or
were to have worked) at each employment;
(g) Your basic wage or salary and the period of
computation {i.e., hour, week, month, year, piece work,
commission), including for each employment any raises, increases
in salary or other changes in compensation;
(h) The date each said employment commenced, terminated
and the reason for said termination; and

(i) The reason that each hour, day, etc., was lost from
each employment (i.e., doctor appointments, hospitalization, too
sore to work, doctor's orders, etc.).
(4) The total amount of your wage loss claim along with
a comprehensive explanation of each and every formula, calculation
or method used to calculate the total amount of said claim.

6. If you are claiming future lost wages, for said claim,

7. If you are claiming permanent injury and/or a reduction

each and every formula, computation or method used to calculate
the total of said claim.

8. If you are making a claim for past pain and suffering,

the total of said claim.

claim.
