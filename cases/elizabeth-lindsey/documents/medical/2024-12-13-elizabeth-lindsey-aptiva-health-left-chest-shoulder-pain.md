sustained as a result of a motor vehicle accident that occurred on [DOB-2]. Patient

when a car went through the intersection and hit the front corner of her car. She states
there was significant damage to the car, to the point where her husband had a large
laceration on his leg. Both of her children were taken to Children's Hospital. One with a

shoulder, especially with range of motion. She denies numbness or tingling. She denies
radicular pain. She denies neck pain. She denies loss of consciousness. She denies
headaches. She is concerned over the fact that the pain has not gotten significantly better

was subsequently sent from there to UC Health, where x-rays from neck to pelvis were
