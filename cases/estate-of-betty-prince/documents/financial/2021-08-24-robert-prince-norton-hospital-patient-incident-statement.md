WRITTEN STATEMENT OF ROBERT PRINCE 
WITNESS & HUSBAND OF DECENDANT BETTY PRINCE 
“I was praying that the Norton’s personnel would assist my wife but they would not help her or let me help her.” 
a) I assisted my wife, Betty Prince, from the parking lot to the waiting room of Norton Hospital. 
b) We were in the waiting room when they called her name. 
c) They watched me pick her up from the chair and walk her across the waiting room. 
d) It was obvious my wife was too weak and needed assistance walking. 
e) When we got to the door, the Norton personnel said I could not assist my wife any further. 
f) We were not offered any other assistance or even a wheelchair. 
g) I was praying that the Norton’s personnel would assist my wife but they would not help her or let me help 
her. 
h) My wife made it into the office and got registered. 
i) The lady that registered her came out first and left my wife to come out by herself, unassisted, last. 
j) My wife made it to the hallway and fell backwards striking her head on the floor. 
k) The impact busted the back part of her head open. 
l) I was sitting in the waiting room where I could see what happened. 
m) I went to her when she fell and she was holding her head up with a large puddle of blood on the floor. 
n) I moved over out of the blood so she could lay back down and there was another puddle of blood. 
o) I looked around and there were 4 or 5 Norton personnel standing around with a security guard. 
p) None of the Norton personnel was trying to help. 
q) They were just watching me trying to help her. 
r) I had to ask for a towel or something to slow down the bleeding. 
s) They finally handed me a towel so I could help her. 
t) The Norton personnel still did not offer to help her or assist in anyway. 
u) Five (5) to ten (10) minutes later, after being summoned twice, ER personnel finally came to help her.
