ORDER.
Due to the surge in COVID-19 related cases in Trimble County and under the
Recommendation of the Kentucky Supreme Court, the Trimble District Court hereby
enters the following ORDER, effective November 16, 2020 until June 1, 2021:
1. THE TRIMBLE DISTRICT COURT WILL USE ZOOM VIDEOCONFERENCING
TO REMOTELY CONDUCT ALL PROCEEDINGS. There shall be no in-person
proceedings during this period, except as directed by the Court or
requested by attorney or litigant and approved by the Court.
2. The Court directs the Clerk of the Trimble District Court to send the attached
notice to all litigants and attorneys involved in cases pending before the Court
between November 16, 2020 and June 1, 2021.
3. No jury trials will be conducted between November 16. 2020 and June 1, 2021.
Any scheduledjury trials will be remanded and placed on a court docket to be
rescheduled for a later date.
