v.<u>**REQUESTS FOR ADMISSIONS AND INTERROGATORY**</u>

<u>**PROPOUNDED TO PLAINTIFF**</u>

NORTON HOSPITALS, INC. DEFENDANT

*  *  *  *  *  *  *  *

Comes the Defendant, Norton Hospitals, Inc., by counsel, and propounds the following Requests for Admissions to be answered, under oath, by Plaintiff within thirty (30) days from the date of service hereof.

<u>**REQUEST NO. 1**</u>:

Please admit or deny that the Plaintiff or the Plaintiff’s representative, did not consult with any qualified health care professional prior to the filing of the instant action, to determine if the medical care provided to Betty Prince by this Defendant at any time deviated from the accepted standard of care.

<u>**RESPONSE:**</u>

<u>**REQUEST NO. 2**</u>:

Please admit or deny that no qualified health care professional has criticized the medical care rendered by this Defendant at any time to Betty Prince as having deviated from the accepted standard of care.

<u>**RESPONSE:**</u>

<u>**REQUEST NO. 3**</u>:

Please admit or deny that Plaintiff is unable to state through expert testimony that the medical care rendered at any time by this Defendant, to a reasonable degree of medical probability, caused Betty Prince’s injuries.

<u>**RESPONSE:**</u>

<u>**REQUEST NO. 4**</u>:

Please admit or deny that Plaintiff is unable to state through expert testimony that the medical care rendered at any time by this Defendant, to a reasonable degree of medical probability, was a substantial factor in the cause of Betty Prince’s injuries.

<u>**RESPONSE:**</u>

<u>**REQUEST NO. 5**</u>:

Please admit or deny that the Plaintiff is unable to state through expert testimony that the medical care rendered at any time by this Defendant to Betty Prince deviated from the accepted standard of care.

<u>**RESPONSE:**</u>

<u>**REQUEST NO. 6**</u>:

Please admit that the Plaintiff is not making a claim in this case that any act or omission of the Defendant caused or contributed to the death of the Plaintiff’s decedent, Betty Prince. 

<u>**RESPONSE:**</u>

<u>**INTERROGATORY NO. 1:**</u>

If the Answers to the above Requests for Admissions, Nos. 3, 4, 5 or 6 are anything other than an unqualified admission, please state the name and address of each and every expert witness who will testify and give the basis of the expert witness opinions in a detailed summary of those opinions in accordance with CR 26.02.
