DIVISION ONE (1)
JUDGE ERIC HANER
ROBERT PRINCE as Administrator of 
The Estate of BETTY PRINCE PLAINTIFF
v. REQUESTS FOR ADMISSIONS AND INTERROGATORY
PROPOUNDED TO PLAINTIFF
NORTON HOSPITALS, INC. DEFENDANT
* * * * * * * *
Comes the Defendant, Norton Hospitals, Inc., by counsel, and propounds the following 
Requests for Admissions to be answered, under oath, by Plaintiff within thirty (30) days from the 
date of service hereof.
REQUEST NO. 1:
Please admit or deny that the Plaintiff or the Plaintiff’s representative, did not consult with 
any qualified health care professional prior to the filing of the instant action, to determine if the 
medical care provided to Betty Prince by this Defendant at any time deviated from the accepted 
standard of care.
RESPONSE:
REQUEST NO. 2:
Please admit or deny that no qualified health care professional has criticized the medical 
care rendered by this Defendant at any time to Betty Prince as having deviated from the accepted 
standard of care.
RESPONSE:

2
REQUEST NO. 3:
Please admit or deny that Plaintiff is unable to state through expert testimony that the 
medical care rendered at any time by this Defendant, to a reasonable degree of medical probability, 
caused Betty Prince’s injuries.
RESPONSE:
REQUEST NO. 4:
Please admit or deny that Plaintiff is unable to state through expert testimony that the 
medical care rendered at any time by this Defendant, to a reasonable degree of medical probability, 
was a substantial factor in the cause of Betty Prince’s injuries.
RESPONSE:
REQUEST NO. 5:
Please admit or deny that the Plaintiff is unable to state through expert testimony that the 
medical care rendered at any time by this Defendant to Betty Prince deviated from the accepted 
standard of care.
RESPONSE:
REQUEST NO. 6:
Please admit that the Plaintiff is not making a claim in this case that any act or omission of 
the Defendant caused or contributed to the death of the Plaintiff’s decedent, Betty Prince. 
RESPONSE:

3
INTERROGATORY NO. 1:
If the Answers to the above Requests for Admissions, Nos. 3, 4, 5 or 6 are anything other 
than an unqualified admission, please state the name and address of each and every expert witness 
who will testify and give the basis of the expert witness opinions in a detailed summary of those 
opinions in accordance with CR 26.02.
