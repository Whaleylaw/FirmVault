In order to preserve and maintain the confidentiality of certain documents (“Confidential 
Documents”) to be produced by the Defendant in the above-captioned action, the parties having 
agreed, and the Court being otherwise sufficiently advised, 
IT IS HEREBY AGREED, ORDERED AND ADJUDGED, that the Confidential 
Documents shall be produced to the Plaintiff pursuant to the following restrictions: 
1. The Confidential Documents and all information related thereto (with the exception 
of any information related thereto that is obtained from sources that are not Confidential 
Documents) shall be kept confidential and no portion shall be used for business or competitive 
purposes or used in any fashion causing dissemination to the public; 
2. Any party to this action to whom any portion of the Confidential Documents is 
disclosed may provide such Confidential Documents and information to persons whose assistance 
is required by that party’s counsel in conducting this litigation. Any party or party’s counsel may 
also provide such Confidential Documents to, or discuss them with, witnesses or prospective 
witnesses. However, all such persons to whom such Confidential Documents are provided or with 
whom they are discussed, first must be made fully aware of the terms of this Protective Order and
