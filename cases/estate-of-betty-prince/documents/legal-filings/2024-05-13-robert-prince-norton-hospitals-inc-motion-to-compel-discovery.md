MOTION TO COMPEL DISCOVERY RESPONSES 
Plaintiff, Robert Prince as Administrator of the Estate of Betty Prince, by and through 
counsel, and pursuant to all applicable Civil Rules and/or Local Rules hereby move the Court for 
an Order compelling the Defendant Norton Hospitals, Inn. to respond fully to Plaintiff’s 
outstanding written discovery requests. In support thereof, Plaintiff states as follows: 
On March 19, 2024, Plaintiff served his Supplemental Discovery Requests upon Defendant 
Norton Hospitals, Inc. via email to her counsel of record, accompanied by a Notice of Service filed 
with this Court. 
On April 22, 2024, Plaintiff’s counsel attempted to follow up on these outstanding via 
email to defense counsel. After receiving no response, Plaintiff’s counsel again reached out to 
defense counsel via email. Again, Plaintiff’s counsel received no response. Exhibit 1. Despite good 
faith attempts by the undersigned to resolve this discovery dispute and obtain responses to 
Plaintiff’s discovery from the Defendant, they have not been obtained to date. 

Plaintiffs’ counsel affirmatively states that he has attempted to confer in good faith with 
Defendant’s counsel in an effort to obtain the responses identified herein, as detailed above, and 
believes that only a Court Order will result a due and sufficient response by the Defendant. An 
Order is appropriate under these circumstances pursuant to CR 37.01. 
WHEREFORE, the Plaintiff, by counsel, moved the Court to enter the tendered Order.
