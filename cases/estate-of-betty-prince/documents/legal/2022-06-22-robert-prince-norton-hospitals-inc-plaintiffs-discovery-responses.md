REQUEST NO. 1:
Please admit or deny that the Plaintiff or the Plaintiffs representative, did not consult with
any qualified health care professional prior to the filing of the instant action, to determine ifthe
medical care provided to Betty Prince by this Defendant at any time deviated from the accepted
standard of care.

care professional prior to the filing of the complaint. Without waiving said objection, ADMIT.

standard of care.

DENY
