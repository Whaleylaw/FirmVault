Comes the Plaintiff, by counsel, and gives notice that he will, on Monday, October 2, 2023, 
at or around the hour of 8:45 a.m. EST, or as soon thereafter as Counsel may be heard, make the 
instant Motion to Compel, pursuant to CR 37.01(b) and all other applicable rules of the Court, and 
tender the Order set forth below. 
MOTION TO COMPEL 
Plaintiff, Robert Prince as Administrator of the Estate of Betty Prince, by and through 
counsel, moves this Court pursuant to CR 37 for an order compelling the Defendant, Norton 
Hospitals, Inc. to provide their responses to the Interrogatories and Requests for Production of 
Documents propounded upon them by Plaintiff in this regard. 
In support, Plaintiff states that Defendant Norton Hospitals, Inc. was served with 
Interrogatories, Requests for Production of Documents, and Requests for Admission on June 28, 
2022. On July 27, 2022, Defendant served to Plaintiff only its Responses to Requests for 
Admission. 
On July 21, 2023, the undersigned sent a letter to the Defendant’s counsel in an effort to 
follow up on the outstanding requests. (Attached hereto as Exhibit 1). After receiving no response, 
Plaintiff’s counsel again contacted counsel for the Defendant via email on August 22, 2023, to 

2 
follow up. That day, counsel for Defendant indicated that responses to the outstanding discovery 
were being completed and would be served “by the end of the week”. A copy of this email 
conversation is attached hereto as Exhibit 2. 
 On September 15, 2023, Plaintiff’s counsel again attempted to follow up with counsel for 
the Defendant to determine when responses could be expected. To date, there has been no response. 
To date, Defendant Norton Hospitals Inc. has not responded to the outstanding Interrogatories and 
Requests for Production of Documents, preventing this case from moving forward. Plaintiff’s 
counsel affirmatively states that he has attempted to confer in good faith with Defendant’s counsel 
in an effort to obtain the discovery identified herein, as detailed above, and believes that only a 
Court Order will result in a due and sufficient response by the Defendant. An Order is appropriate 
under these circumstances pursuant to CR 37.01. 
WHEREFORE, Plaintiff, by counsel, respectfully requests the Court enter the attached 
Order compelling Defendant Norton Hospitals, Inc. to provide responses to Plaintiff’s written 
discovery requests within fourteen (14) days from entry of the attached proposed Order.
