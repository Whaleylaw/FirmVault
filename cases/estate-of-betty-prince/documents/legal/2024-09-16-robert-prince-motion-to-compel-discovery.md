NOTICE – MOTION – ORDER 
*** *** *** 
 Comes the Plaintiff, by counsel, and gives notice that he will, on Monday, September 16, 
2024, at or around the hour of 8:45 a.m. EST, or as soon thereafter as Counsel may be heard, make 
the instant Motion to Compel, pursuant to C.R. 37.01 and all other applicable rules of the Court, 
and tender the Order set forth below. 
MOTION TO COMPEL DISCOVERY REQUESTS 
 Plaintiff, Robert Prince as Administrator of the Estate of Betty Prince, by and through 
counsel, and pursuant to all applicable Civil Rules and/or Local Rules hereby moves the Court for 
an Order compelling Defendant Norton Hospitals, Inc. to respond fully to Plaintiff’s outstanding 
written discovery requests. In support thereof, Plaintiff states as follows: 
 Following the Court’s entry of an Order Compelling discovery responses, on June 17, 
2024, Defendant Norton served incomplete responses to Plaintiff’s requests. The deficiencies in 
the responses were outlined by the undersigned in a letter to defense counsel dated June 26, 2024 
and attached hereto as Exhibit 1. While there have been some minor discussions regarding the 
outstanding discovery, and some training materials were provided, the remaining deficiencies have 
not been addressed and the undersigned has not received any response to the letter sent previously. 
 Plaintiff’s counsel affirmatively states that he has attempted in good faith with Defendant’s 
counsel in an effort to obtain the responses identified herein, as detailed above, and believes that 

only a Court Order will result a due and sufficient response by the Defendant. An Order is 
appropriate under these circumstances pursuant to CR 37.01 
WHEREFORE, the Plaintiff, by counsel, moves the Court to enter the tendered Order.
