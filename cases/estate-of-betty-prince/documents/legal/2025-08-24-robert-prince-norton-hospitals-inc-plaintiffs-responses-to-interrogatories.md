INTERROGATORY NO. 3: 
State in specific detail each and every fact which Plaintiff and Plaintiff’s counsel claim constitutes 
evidence of negligence by Norton Hospitals, Inc., in this action, including also the name of 
the specific agent, servant or employee of Norton Hospitals, Inc. involved and the date, time 
and place of each alleged incident or occurrence of such negligence as well as each specific 
standard of care which each such person allegedly violated including the source of each 
standard. 
ANSWER: 

The Norton Hospital Inc. staff failed to meet the standard of care by failing to recognize and know 
when a patient is at high risk for falls. 
The standard of care requires any professional working in a health care facility to recognize, be 
aware of and take into consideration all risks a patient demonstrates that would increase her 
risk of falling 
The Norton Hospital Inc. staff failed to meet the standard of care by failing to implement proper 
fall precautions for Mrs. Prince, up to & including failing to notify a health care professional 
to provide assistance with her mobility. 
The standard of care requires ‘any’ professional working in a health care facility to ensure fall 
precautions are in place for all patients in the facility, particularly for those at high risk for 
falls including the elderly, who have demonstrated unsteadiness and weakness. 
The standard of care requires professional in ‘any’ area of a health care facility to notify a health 
care professional to provide assistance for patients who are at risk of falls. Allowing the health 
care professional to provide contact guard assist and/or stand by assist or the provision of a 
wheel chair when transferring an elderly patient at risk for falls and/or who are unfamiliar 
with the location. 
For further information please refer to the Affidavit of Donna S. Jones, RN.
