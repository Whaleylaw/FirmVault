*** *** *** *** 
Comes the Defendant, Trinity Cohara, by counsel, and, pursuant to Civil Rule 37.01 of the 
Kentucky Rules of Civil Procedure, hereby moves the Court to compel Plaintiffs, Greg Neltner 
and Cecilia Neltner, to respond to the First Set of Interrogatories and Requests for Production of 
Documents served upon them by the Defendant. In support of her Motion, the Defendant states as 
follows: 
Defendant Cohara served her First Set of Interrogatories and Requests for Production of 
Documents upon Plaintiffs, Greg and Cecilia Neltner, on or about May 6, 2024 (Notice of Service 
attached hereto as Exhibit 1). On June 17, 2024, Undersigned Counsel sent Plaintiffs’ Counsel a 
letter, requesting that he provide the Plaintiffs’ overdue responses to Trinity Cohara’s 
Interrogatories and Requests for Production of Documents within ten days (attached hereto as 
Exhibit 2). Plaintiffs’ Counsel did not respond. 
To date, the Defendant Cohara has not received Plaintiffs’ discovery responses or a request 
for an extension of time to respond to the same. In other words, notwithstanding the good faith 
efforts of the undersigned to resolve this aforementioned discovery dispute, this Motion to Compel 
has become a necessity.
