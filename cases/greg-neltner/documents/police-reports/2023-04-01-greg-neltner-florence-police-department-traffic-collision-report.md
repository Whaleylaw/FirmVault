Unit #1 stated she was in the far right EB lane on KY 18 when she attempted to cross 3 lanes of traffic to
turn left onto the on ramp to NB 75. While crossing the lanes of traffic, she turned in front of Unit #2 causing
an accident. She then continued onto the on ramp after the collision and stopped halfway down.
Unit #2 stated he was traveling straight in the left EB lane on KY 18 when Unit #1 suddenly turned in front
of him leaving him no time to react and avoid the collision.
No injuries were reported.
