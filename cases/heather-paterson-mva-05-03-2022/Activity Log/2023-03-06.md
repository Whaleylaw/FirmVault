Dear Tracy, I am in receipt your letter denying PIP coverage.In the letter you list an exclusion which excludes coverage when the insureds vehicle is being used as a "public or livery conveyance."  It then goes on to include any time any person is logged into a "transportation network platform."  This is very clearly intended to exclude people who are using their cars as an Uber, Lyft driver, or some similar activity. Ms. Paterson was not engaged in this type of activity. Nor was she using her "covered auto" as a "public or livery conveyance."  "Public or livery conveyance" would be the transportation of people or goods for hire. This is not what Ms. Paterson was engaged in.

Please provide the basis for your conclusion that Ms. Paterson was using her vehicle as a  "public or livery conveyance." If the policy provides a definition of  "public or livery conveyance" please provide that as well.
