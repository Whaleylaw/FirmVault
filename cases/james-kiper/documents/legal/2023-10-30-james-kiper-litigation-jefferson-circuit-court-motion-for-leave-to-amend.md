Comes the Plaintiff, James Kiper, by and through counsel, and gives notice that he will, on

MOTION FOR LEAVE TO FILE AMENDED COMPLAINT 
Comes the Plaintiff, James Kiper, by and through counsel, and hereby moves the Court for 
leave to file the attached Amended Complaint. In support hereof, the Plaintiff states as follows: 
Plaintiff wishes to add Travelers Insurance Company as a defendant to this action. 
Travelers Insurance Company has a policy of coverage requiring it to provide personal injury and 
uninsured/underinsured benefits that inure to the benefit of Plaintiff. 
This Amendment is permitted under KRCP 15.01 as no responsive pleading has been 
served in this case and is in the interest of justice. Further, Plaintiff requests that Summons be 
issued for Defendant The Standard Fire Insurance Company d/b/a Travelers Insurance Company 
with regard to Plaintiff’s First Amended Complaint. Plaintiff requests that Summons not be issued 

for Defendant Musimbi Amani, who had already been properly served, and that such defendant 
shall be deemed served with the Amended Complaint upon filing. 
WHEREFORE, Plaintiff moves the Court for leave to file his Amended Complaint and 
proposes an Order granting this Motion and to request that the Court ensure issued process thereon.
