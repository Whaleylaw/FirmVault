TO DEFENDANT TRAVELERS INSURANCE COMPANY 
*** *** *** 
Comes the Plaintiff, James Kiper, by and through counsel, and requests the Defendant, 
Travelers Insurance Company (known hereafter as “Travelers”), to answer the following 
Interrogatories and Requests for Production of Documents within thirty (30) days of service 
hereof, in accordance with all applicable Rules of Civil Procedure inasmuch as these discovery 
requests are served contemporaneously with Plaintiff’s Complaint. 
 These Interrogatories shall be deemed continuing. Knowledge of the attorneys for the 
Defendant or the claims agent or any other agent of the Defendant, or of any employee of the 
Defendant, shall be deemed to be the knowledge of the Defendant. Anything coming to your 
knowledge after filing an Answer to the following Interrogatories which would change your 
original answers or responses should be contained in an Amended Answer or an Amended 
Response and served upon Plaintiffs’ attorneys immediately after the same is brought to your 
attention. 

INTERROGATORIES
