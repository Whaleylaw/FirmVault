Re: James Kiper v. Musimbi Amani, et al. 
 Supplemental Discovery Requests
Counsel, 
Upon review of your client’s supplemental discovery responses and the accompanying 
EDR report, I wanted to follow up regarding several outstanding issues. 
Interrogatory No. 11 asked for the identification of any individuals or entities involved in 
obtaining data from the vehicle. The response objected on work product grounds and referred only 
to the report, which does not appear to identify anyone involved in retrieving or analyzing the data. 
Interrogatory No. 12 requested information about the equipment, materials, or software 
used to obtain the data. Again, the response objected and referred to the report, which does not 
include this information. 
Request for Production No. 9 asked for all reports, raw data, transmissions, or other 
information recovered from the vehicle’s black box. The response refers only to the attached 
report. However, that same report had already been provided previously, and no raw or native data 
files appear to have been produced. 
If this information is included elsewhere or if I’ve overlooked something, please let me 
know. Otherwise, I would appreciate it if you could confirm whether you intend to supplement the 
responses to fully address these requests. Thank you for your consideration in this regard and I 
hope to hear from you soon.
