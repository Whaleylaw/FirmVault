A rotator cuff injury is damage to the muscles or tendons of your rotator cuff. The rotator cuff is made up of a group of 
muscles and tendons that hold the shoulder joint in place. The damage may include stretching of your muscle or tears 
in the tendons. It may also include inflammation of the bursa (small sack of fluid around the joint).
