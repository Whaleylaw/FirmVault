AULT, SUSAN 1 OF 4

Upon arrival to the scene, Traffic Control Officer (TCO) T. Baker observed that Unit 1 had left the scene and
Unit 2 had moved to a nearby parking lot. The operator of Unit 2 indicated that he had some 'whiplash' but
did not request EMS to be called to the scene.
The operator of Unit 2 stated that he had stopped in traffic on northbound Preston Highway just be fore
Gilmore Lane when Unit 1 impacted from the rear. He stated that he was able to take some pictures of
Unit 1 and that the operator of Unit 1 insisted that police did not need to be called and that the damage was
'not that bad'. The operator of Unit 1 then left the scene. The operator of Unit 2 followed Unit 1 north on
Preston where Unit 1 impacted another vehicle (report 80-22-071440). The operator of Unit 2 stated that
Unit 1 hit a third vehicle (this vehicle did not stay on scene for a report) and left the scene.
Unit 1 information was obtained through pictures of the vehicle and temporary license tag taken by the
operator of Unit 2 (from both reports mentioned).
