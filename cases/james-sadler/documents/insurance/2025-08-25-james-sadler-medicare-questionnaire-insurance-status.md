MEDICARE QUESTIONNAIRE 
Medicare is prohibited by Federal Law from being the primary payer of medical expenses 
covered by other insurance, and has broad rights to recover conditional payments made when 
Medicare should be secondary payer. We are required by Federal Law to protect Medicare’s 
interests by reporting all settlements, judgments, awards, and other payments made to Medicare 
beneficiaries involved in liability, no-fault, or workers’ compensation claims covered by our 
insurance. Thus, this questionnaire will be used to help determine Medicare secondary payer 
status. Please note that failure to fully and accurately complete this form may cause claim 
payments to be delayed.
