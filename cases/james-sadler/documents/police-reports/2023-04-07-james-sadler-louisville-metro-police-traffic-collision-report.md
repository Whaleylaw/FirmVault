AULT, SUSAN 1 OF 6

Upon arrival to the scene, Traffic Control Officer (TCO) T. Baker observed that Unit 1, Unit 2 and Unit 3
remained in the roadway at the final position of the collision. The operator of Unit 1 and the operator of
Unit 3 indicated that they did not have injuries requiring EMS to be called to the scene. The operator of
Unit 2 was treated and transported to the hospital by EMS for non-life threatening injuries.
Unit 1 was traveling east on Hikes Lane at the listed location. The operator of Unit 1 stated that he
believed he had a yellow traffic signal and he was proceeding through the intersection.
Unit 2 was traveling south on Breckenridge Lane at the listed location. The operator of Unit 2 stated that
he had a green traffic signal when he entered the intersection.
Unit 3 was traveling north on Breckenridge Lane at the listed location. The operator of Unit 3 stated that he
had a green traffic signal when he entered the intersection.
The listed witness stated that he observed Unit 1 come into the intersection on a clearly red traffic signal.
The left side of Unit 1 impacted the front of Unit 2. Unit 1 was rotated 180 degrees and the left side of Unit
1 impacted the front of Unit 3.
