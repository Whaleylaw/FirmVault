A message was sent to you regarding record request #22-4088:

This comes as an update on your attached open records request. Specific to the request for body camera video. Our video records specialist identified video responsive to this request but those records are denied release through open records pursuant to KRS 61.878(1)(h) in conjunction with KRS 17.150(2). LMPD is a law enforcement agency. The requested records were compiled in the process of detecting/investigating a statutory violation and is part of a pending criminal case in Jefferson County District Court, case number 21-T-028955. Any premature release of these records could jeopardize the pending prosecution by identifying witnesses not otherwise known and tipping them off to the direction of the ongoing criminal case, impact witness recollection of the incident, and taint the jury pool by permitting the “case” be tried in the court of public opinion rather than in court with the benefit of procedural and evidentiary rules. As evidenced in CourtNet, case 21-T-028955 is set for arraignment on May 20th 2022. Open Records is not meant to circumvent the discovery process.

You may resubmit your request for video at a later date.
