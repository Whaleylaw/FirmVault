Narrative
M106A dispatched Code 1 to the report of an MVA. While en route, LMPD advised EMS to step up for patient status. On scene, EMS was directed to the patient. Patient
was found sitting in the back passenger seat of a vehicle, not belonging to her. Patient is A&Ox2 with a GCS of 15. Pupils are PERRL, PMS in all extremities, lung sounds
are clear and equal. Patient complains of slight headache, rated 3/10. Patient denies LOC and blood thinners. Patient was restrained and airbags deployed. EMS
applied a C-Collar and assisted patient to the stretcher. Patient was placed in a position of comfort and secured with side rails and safety straps. EMS moved patient
to the ambulance for further assessment, treatment and transport.
In the ambulance, patient was further assessed. Patient does not recall event, repeated questioning and negative seat-belt signs. Report called in to hospital via
Mobile Radio. Phys 302 advised patient to be seen in "Room 9." Patient is transported Code 3 to the University of Louisville Hospital. Patient is continuously assessed
and monitored. Patient became A&Ox3 while maintaining GCS of 14. Patients vitals remained stable throughout.
