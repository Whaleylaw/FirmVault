NOTICE OF SUBROGATION CLAIM OF ERISA SELF-FUNDED HEALTH PLAN
Dear Sir or Madam:
According to our investigation, Blue Cross and Blue Shield of North Carolina administered medical
benefits on behalf of the health plan for the patient, Owen Lindsey, as a result of the incident that occurred on
12/1/24. This letter serves as notice to you that the health plan is subrogated to the patient’s personal injury
claim for the medical benefits paid by the health plan on the patient’s behalf. The health plan is an ERISA selffunded plan that preempts state law. As such, the health plan is permitted to pursue a direct subrogation claim
against Progressive Group of Ins Companies’s insured regardless of state law.
Blue Cross and Blue Shield of North Carolina, on behalf of the health plan, engaged The Rawlings
Company, LLC (“TRC”) to pursue its subrogation claim. The health plan’s subrogation claim cannot be
negotiated, settled, resolved, or compromised in any way by thepatient or thepatient’s representative. Before the
patient’s claim for damages is resolved, Progressive Group of Ins Companies must contact TRC in order to settle
thehealth plan’s direct subrogation claim for medical benefits.
To satisfy the health plan’s subrogation claim, issue a separate check payable to TRC. If Progressive
Group of Ins Companies fails to settle the subrogation claim directly with TRC, then Progressive Group of Ins
Companies will potentially expose your insured to additional liability and a possible excess judgment. Moreover,
this failure could result in additional claims against Progressive Group of Ins Companies. Securing a release
and/or an indemnification agreement from thepatient or their representative will not absolve Progressive Group
of Ins Companies’s obligation to satisfy the subrogation claim.
The health plan’s subrogation claim applies to any amount now due or that may become payable because
of payment of medical benefits on patient’s behalf. You may only verify the exact amount of the health plan’s
subrogation claim by contacting me directly. Please acknowledge receipt of this notice by completing the
attached form and returning it to me. Please contact me ifyou have any questions.
