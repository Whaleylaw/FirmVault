NOTICE – MOTION – ORDER 
*** *** *** 
 Comes the Plaintiff, by counsel, and gives notice that he will, on Monday, May 26, 2025, 
at or around the hour of 8:45 a.m. EST, or as soon thereafter as Counsel may be heard, make the 
instant Motion to Compel, pursuant to C.R. 37.01 and all other applicable rules of the Court, and 
tender the Order set forth below. 
MOTION TO COMPEL DISCOVERY REQUESTS 
 Plaintiff, Jerome Hedinger, by and through counsel, and pursuant to all applicable Civil 
Rules and/or Local Rules hereby moves the Court for an Order compelling Defendant 432 Atwood, 
LLC to respond fully to Plaintiff’s outstanding written discovery requests. In support thereof, 
Plaintiff states as follows: 
 Plaintiff, by counsel, served the First Set of Discovery to Defendant 432 Atwood, LLC via 
email to its counsel on February 17, 2025. On March 19, 2025, the undersigned reached out to 
defense counsel via email to follow up on the outstanding requests. At this time, the undersigned 
provided defense counsel with an additional 30 days to respond. Plaintiff’s counsel again 
attempted to follow up on the outstanding responses on April 23 and May 1, 2025. To date, the 
outstanding responses have not been received by the undersigned. 

 Plaintiff’s counsel affirmatively states that he has attempted in good faith with Defendant’s 
counsel in an effort to obtain the responses identified herein, as detailed above, and believes that 
only a Court Order will result a due and sufficient response by the Defendant. An Order is 
appropriate under these circumstances pursuant to CR 37.01 
WHEREFORE, the Plaintiff, by counsel, moves the Court to enter the tendered Order.
