Mr. Whaley & Mr. Koon,

 

Please be advised that my associate, Margo Meneffee, is assisting me with 
the handling of this claim. Please note my name as attorney-of-record along 
with Ms. Menefee and copy me on all future correspondence. We are writing 
in response to Ms. Tuttle’s correspondence of 11/8/23 (see below).

 

Ms. Tuttle states, “Mrs. Robinson does/did not suffer from any prior 
disabilities/injuries in the preceding 12 months of her injury.” This 
assertion may be correct, but we have no way to verify same without being 
provided the information requested on the Form 105. We consider this a 
routine discovery request.  

 

Ms. Tuttle also states,  “Any treatment prior to 12 months is irrelevant 
unless it was actively debilitating at the time of her injury which again 
she did not suffer from any active disabilities at the time.”  We know of 
no authority supporting the “12 month” assertion. Furthermore, we cannot 
evaluate these assertions unless we are provided the requested Form 105 
information.

 

There are additional matters we need to investigate, namely any 
pre-existing condition(s) ratable under the AMA *Guides *per* Wetherby*. We 
also need to investigate the medical treatment history as relates to 
possible causation issues.  We cannot properly investigate these matters 
without being provided the requested medical history. The refusal to 
provide same for the last ten years will restrict our ability to properly 
evaluate our client’s exposure and evaluate this claim for settlement. 

 

Based on the foregoing, we reiterate our request for Ms. Robinson’s medical 
treatment history for the last ten years so we can complete our 
investigation. We will provide you with copies of all medical records 
provided. Failure to provide this requested information will render us 
unable to properly evaluate this claim for resolution. We are confident 
that a mutually agreeable settlement would benefit both parties. 

 

Should you have any questions or wish to discuss any of these maters 
personally please contact Ms. Menefee or me at your convenience. Thank you 
for your attention to this matter. 

 

P.S. Historically, we have enjoyed a good working relationship with 
claimant attorneys and settle most of our claims. Those are certainly our 
goals in this instance.
