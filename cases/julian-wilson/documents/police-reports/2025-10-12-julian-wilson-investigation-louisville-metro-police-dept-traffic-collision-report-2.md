bound traffic had stopped and was allowing Unit 1 to exit. Unit 1 did not notice Unit 2 traveling in the left
lane of west bound traffic on Outer Loop and made contact with the passenger side of Unit 2. Passenger of
Unit 2 had some complaint of right shoulder pain from the impact however refused EMS and was
transported by family to the hospital. No injuries recorded for Unit 1. BWC activated.
