Dear Sir or Madam:

As we previously informed you, the above-named covered individual(s) is a TeamCare beneficiary and has received, and may in the future receive, medical and/or disability benefits provided by TeamCare as a result of bodily injury sustained in the captioned accident. TeamCare claims a lien and right of subrogation and/or reimbursement as stated herein and, for that reason, we request that, prior to settlement of this case, you contact the undersigned in order to learn and arrange to pay the correct and current amount of the benefits provided by TeamCare.

TeamCare is a Taft-Hartley and Employee Benefit Plan under the protection of the Employee Retirement Income Security Act of 1974, 29 U.S.C. 1001 et seg. Pursuant to TeamCare’s Active Plan Document, it is entitled to reimbursement and/or is subrogated to the rights of recovery the captioned member or covered individual(s) may have arising from the captioned accident and by reason of homeowners, public liability and group insurance including, but not limited to third party medical reimbursement, under and uninsured motorist and no fault and personal injury protection (“PIP”) coverages. For the reason stated, TeamCare asserts a lien, to the extent of the benefits paid, upon claims, demands or causes of action that the captioned member or covered individual(s) may have against you arising from the captioned accident. TeamCare also asserts its lien to the extent of the present value of future medical expenses TeamCare is reasonably certain to be required to provide the covered individual(s) as a consequence of the injury in question.
