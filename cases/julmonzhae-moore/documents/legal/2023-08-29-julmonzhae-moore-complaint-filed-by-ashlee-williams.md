 Come the Plaintiffs, Ashlee Williams, Dewayne Ward, and Julmonzhae Moore, by counsel, and 
for their Complaint herein state as follows: 
 1. The Plaintiff, Ashlee Williams (hereinafter “Plaintiff Williams”), at all times relevant 
to the events stated in this Complaint, was and remains a resident of Louisville, Jefferson County, 
Kentucky. 
 2. The Plaintiff, Dewayne Ward (hereinafter “Plaintiff Ward”), at all times relevant to 
the events stated in this Complaint, was and remains a resident of Louisville, Jefferson County, 
Kentucky. 

2 
 3. The Plaintiff, Julmonzhae Moore (hereinafter “Plaintiff Moore”), at all times relevant 
to the events stated in this Complaint, was and remains a resident of Louisville, Jefferson County, 
Kentucky. 
 4. Upon information and belief, the Defendant, Naomi Robinson (hereinafter 
“Defendant Robinson”) at all times relevant to the events stated in this Complaint was and remains 
a resident of Jeffersonville, Clark County, Indiana, residing at the address set forth above. 
 5. On or about August 29, 2023, Defendant Robinson did so negligently and carelessly 
operate her motor vehicle, a certain 2022 Chevrolet Malibu, Vin No. 1G1ZD5ST6N, at the 
intersection of Charlestown Road and I-265 East in New Albany, Indiana, thereby causing a 
collision with Plaintiffs. 
 6. The negligence of Defendant Robinson in the operation of her vehicle on the date 
and at the place aforesaid was a substantial factor in causing Plaintiffs serious and permanent 
injuries, including great pain of body and mind, both past and future. In addition, the Plaintiffs 
have incurred medical expenses as a result of the collision and injuries they sustained and will 
incur future medical expenses for the treatment of their injuries. Further, Plaintiffs lost time and 
money from their gainful employment, resulting in damages for past lost wages and have had their 
future ability to labor and earn money substantially impaired. 
7. The aforementioned damages incurred by Plaintiffs is in a sum in excess of the 
minimal amount necessary to confer jurisdiction upon this Court. 
 WHEREFORE, Plaintiffs demand as follows: 
 1. Judgment against the Defendant in a fair and reasonable amount sufficient to 
compensate Plaintiffs for their damages alleged hereinabove; 
 2. Prejudgment interest;

 3. Post judgment interest; 
 4. Trial by jury on all issues so triable; 
 5. Their costs herein incurred, including applicable attorneys fees; 
 6. Punitive damages to the extent a trier of fact determines the actions of the Defendant to 
have been malicious, oppressive, fraudulent, or reckless; and, 
 7. Any and all other proper relief to which Plaintiffs may appear entitled, including the right 
to amend this Complaint.
