RT shoulder pain, and headache. Patient was noted to be a unrestrained backseat passenger with airbag deployment,
self extricated. Patient admits to hitting her head, Unknown LOC. Full patient assessment performed with pt
being axo3, +abc’s, skin conditions noted, and vitals assessed. Patient refused to let EMS place c-collar when
patient complained of neck pain while en route to hospital. Patient was placed on 2lpm o2 via capno cannula due
to spo2 dropping to 94% when on room air, increasing back up to 100% on the 2lpm. Patient noted to be guarding
and complaining of ULQ pain, with no bruising or bleeding. Patients pupils equal and reactive. Patient was
assisted to stretcher x2 person and secured x3, patient refused to let EMS move her onto stretcher. UofL
contacted due to MOI and patient complaints with physician 102 advising room 9 upon arrival. Patient was
transported from scene to UofL room 9 emergent. Full report given to receiving staff upon arrival with patient
being kept in room 9. Patient was transferred from stretcher to room 9 bed via sheet draw. All signatures
obtained.
