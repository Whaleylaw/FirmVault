The following was written by STONE, NOAH
On Tuesday, August 29, 2023 at 8:40 P.M., Officer Stone responded to a crash on Charlestown Road at intersection the of |-265 E. The
weather was clear and the road surface was dry.
Vehicle 1, Black Chevrolet Malibu, Going straight.
Vehicle 2, Black Kia Optima, Turning left.
Upon arrival to crash scene | observed Vehicle 2 to be facing Northbound on Charlestown Road, and Vehicle 1 in the grass area to the East
of Charlestown Road. | observed Driver 1 laying on the ground with the complaint of back and neck pain, 1 passenger of vehicle 2 sitting in
the grass with the complaint of head pain and stated she was not wearing a seat belt, and 1 passenger of vehicle 2 on the ground having a
seizure. Driver 2 had the complaint of pain coming from their right ribs and left knee, and refused medical treatment. 1 passenger of vehicle 2
had the complaint of pain coming from their face and refused medical treatment.
Driver 1 and 2 passengers of vehicle 2 were transported to the University of Louisville Hospital.
On scene, Driver 2 stated that he was in the left turn lane Southbound on Charlestown Road, with a green light turning left. Driver 2 stated
that Vehicle 1 crashed into the front passenger side of Vehicle 2 while Vehicle 2 was turning left. ECM
| contacted Driver 1 on August 30, 2023, at approximately 1:34 P.M. Driver 1 stated that they were driving Northbound on Charlestown Road.
Driver 1 advised they were going through the intersection of Charlestown Road and |-265 E, and that Driver 1 hada yellow light.
Both vehicles were towed by Bennetts Towing to Bennetts Towing lot.
