Counsel: KRS 342.020(11) states that an injured worker claiming compensation shall waive physician-patient privilege as to any condition reasonably related to the condition for which compensation is claimed,  but it doesn't specify how. If you request the records with a Form 106 and a subpoena, I would grant whatever extra time it may take to obtain them. Legal counsel for U of L may get involved, and that could delay the case.

If Ms. Gardner is reluctant to sign the U of L release because of specific conditions such as mental, HIV/AIDS, or substance abuse records, I generally would allow her to forgo authorizing the disclosure of those records, unless a specific reason can be stated, since the waiver of the privilege only extends to conditions reasonably related to the conditions which are claimed.
