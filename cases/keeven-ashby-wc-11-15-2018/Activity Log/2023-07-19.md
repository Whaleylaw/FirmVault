This is a common defense in back injuries. Most back injuries are labeled
'pre-existing, dormant, degenerative conditions' that are brought into
'disabling reality' regardless of causation/mechanism, usually blaming DDD.
The repetitive motions of bending & twisting on the job is the cause of it
being 'brought into disabling reality'. However, I do see where there is
mention of the 'crushed disc' as if to indicate a prior unreported injury
but could be due to the heavy lifting as well as the bending & twisting.
The additional treatment/surgery is due to the original surgery
complications.
