Come the Defendant, Walgreen Co., by counsel, and for its Responses to Plaintiff's
Requests for Admissions, state as follows:
REQUESTS FOR ADMISSION
REQUEST NO. 1: Please admit that on the date and at the time of the accident at issue in this
case, the Plaintiff was lawfully on the Premises located at 115 KY 81 N, Calhoun, Kentucky, 42327.
RESPONSE: Deny —other than the allegations set forth in Plaintiff's Complaint,
this Defendant has no knowledge or awareness of Plaintiff being on the
premises located at 115 KY 81 N, Calhoun, Kentucky 42327 on February 25,
2023.
REQUEST NO. 2: Please admit that at the time of the incident at issue in the Complaint the
Plaintiff fell over, or tripped over, or otherwise fell due, in part, to the condition of the Parking
Lot at issue in this case, which was located on the Premises.
RESPONSE: Deny — this Defendant has no knowledge or awareness of Plaintiff
allegedly falling in the parking lot at 115 KY 81 N, Calhoun, KY 42327 on
February 25, 2023.
REQUEST NO. 3: Please admit that at all times relevant to the issues involved in this lawsuit
Defendant was in control of the restaurant Premises and the Parking Lot, and/or was otherwise
responsible for maintaining the Premises in a reasonably safe condition or was responsible for
warning the public of any potential hazards on the Premises.
RESPONSE: Deny.
