Come the Defendant, Walgreen Co., by counsel, and for its Answer to the Complaint
herein, state as follows:
FIRST DEFENSE
1. The Complaint fails to state a cause of action upon which relief may be granted
against this Defendant.
SECONDDEFENSE
2. The claims of the Plaintiff, Kimberly Brasher, against this Defendant is barred in
whole or in part by the applicable statute of limitations.
THIRD DEFENSE
3. Admits the allegations contained in numerical paragraphs 1 and 2 of the Complaint.
4. Denies the allegations contained in numerical paragraphs 3, 9 and 10 of the
Complaint.
5. Admits Defendant, Networks USA XIV, Inc. was the owner of the property
referenced in the Complaint but deny the remaining allegations contained numerical paragraph 4
of the Complaint.
6. States that it is without sufficient information to form an opinion as the truth or
falsity of the allegations contained in numerical paragraphs 5, 6, 7 and 8 of the Complaint and
therefore denies same.
