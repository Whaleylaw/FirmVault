Comes the Plaintiff, Kimberly Brasher, by counsel, and Defendants, Networks USA XIV,
Inc. and Reit Property Management, Inc. and Walgreen Co., by counsel, and stipulate that
maintenance of the parking area where this alleged accident occurred rested with Walgreen Co.
and not Reit Property or Networks, USA XIV, Inc. and therefore agree as follows:
1) Defendant, Walgreen Co., shall be the sole named Defendant in this action.
2) Plaintiff's claims against Defendants, Networks USA XIV, Inc. and Reit Property
Management, Inc. shall be dismissed, without prejudice.
IT IS FURTHER ORDERED that the style of the within action shall henceforth be
Kimberly Brasher v. Walgreen Co.
The Court being sufficiently advised, IT Is SO ORDERED.
