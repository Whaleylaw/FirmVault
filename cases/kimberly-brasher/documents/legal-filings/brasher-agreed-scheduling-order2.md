**AGREED SCHEDULING ORDER**

______________________________________________________________________________

The Parties hereby agree to the following Agreed Scheduling Order.  The Arbitrator being otherwise sufficiently advised, HEREBY ORDERS:

1. The final pretrial conference for this matter shall be conducted on January 12, 2026, at 8:30 am.  
2. The plaintiff shall disclose any experts they wish to testify at the trial on this matter no later than October 15, 2025.  Concurrent with the filing of Expert Disclosures, Plaintiff shall furnish dates to conduct depositions of their expert witnesses.  
3. Defendants shall disclose any experts they wish to testify at the trial of this matter no later than December 1, 2025.  Concurrent with the filing of Expert Disclosures, Defendants shall furnish dates to conduct depositions of their expert witnesses.
4. Fact Discovery is to be completed by February 8, 2026.
5. The parties shall complete all discovery depositions, including expert depositions no later than February 8, 2026.
6. The parties shall exchange witness and exhibit lists no later than April 16, 2026.
7. Dispositive Motions and any Motions in limine shall be filed by April 17, 2026.  Any responses to Dispositive Motions or Motions in limine shall be filed by April 24, 2026.  Any replies to responses to Dispositive Motions or Motions in limine shall be filed by April 29, 2026.

In the absence of good cause shown, no witness shall be permitted to testify, and no exhibit or testimony shall be admitted into evidence, except upon compliance with the conditions of this Order.
