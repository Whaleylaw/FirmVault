Units 3 and 2 were stopped at the traffic control device (red light) at the intersection of 7th St Rd and
Arcade Ave (2800 Blk of 7th St Rd), when Unit 1 rear ended Unit 2 pushing Unit 2 into Unit 3. Unit 2 front
end went under the left rear side of Unit 3.
Driver of Unit 1 stated that she was driving home and does not remember what happened. Driver of Unit 1
stated that she was not injured and refused EMS.
Driver of Unit 2 stated that he was stopped at the red light behind the Unit 3 when he was struck by Unit 1
and pushed under the bus. Driver and Passenger of Unit 2 had multiple injuries and were transported by
EMS to University of Louisville hospital.
Driver of Unit 3 stated that he was stopped at the red light when his bus was struck by Unit 2. Driver of
Unit 3 stated that he believed that Unit 1 pushed Unit 2 under his bus. Driver stated he was not injured and
refused EMS.
