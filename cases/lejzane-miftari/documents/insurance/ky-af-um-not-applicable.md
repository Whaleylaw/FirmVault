Dear Mr. Justin Chumbley;

We are in receipt of your notice of claim and request for benefits under Uninsured Motorist bodily injury coverage for your client Lejzane Miftari. 

After thorough review of the facts of this loss, we have determined Uninsured Motorist coverage will not apply for the claim of Lejzane Miftari under this policy. 

Following a full investigation of the facts and circumstances surrounding the subject loss, we have concluded the following:

Ms. Miftari caused this rear end collision and noted for “inattention” by law enforcement
The investigating officer, statements and property damage indicate there were no contributing factor on part of the uninsured motorist 
Ms. Miftari is not legally entitled to recovery from the owner or operator of an uninsured motor vehicle because of bodily injury as a result of her negligence in causing this loss
The loss vehicle Ms. Miftari was operating at the time of the loss is insured under this policy for liability coverage

As such, we will not be making any voluntary payments under the Uninsured Motorist Bodily Injury coverage.  Our decision is based on the following provisions in your policy:

The applicable policy reads, in pertinent part, as follows:

PART C > UNINSURED/UNDERINSURED MOTORIST BODILY INJURY COVERAGE 

INSURING AGREEMENT – UNINSURED MOTORISTS BODILY INJURY COVERAGE 

If you pay a premium for these coverages, we will pay damages, except punitive or exemplary damages, which an insured is legally entitled to recover from the owner or operator of an uninsured motor vehicle or underinsured motor vehicle because of bodily injury: 

1. Sustained by an insured; and 
2. Caused by an auto accident.

DEFINITIONS

However, uninsured motor vehicle does not include any vehicle or equipment:

7. Which is insured for coverage under Part A of this policy

However, underinsured motor vehicle does not include any vehicle or equipment:

9. Which is insured for coverage under Part A of this policy.

Ms. Miftari is liable for causing this rear end collision. As such uninsured/underinsured motorist bodily injury coverage is not applicable. Additionally, if the intent is to assert a liability claim for damages under this policy; the covered auto being operated at the time of the loss, insured under Part A liability coverage of this policy, does not meet the definition of an uninsured/underinsured motor vehicle.  

Our current position is based on the information available to date. Should you have additional information to submit for our consideration, we will be happy to review the same. 

In notifying you of this denial of Uninsured Motorist bodily injury coverage, Integon Indemnity Corporation hereby reserves its right to assert any additional defenses that may become known during the course of further investigation.
