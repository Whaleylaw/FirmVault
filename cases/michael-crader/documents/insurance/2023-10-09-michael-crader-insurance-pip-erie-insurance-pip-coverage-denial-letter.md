Dear Attorney Justin Chumbley:
Erie Insurance Exchange received notice of an injury loss under the Auto Insurance Policy - Kentucky, Policy
#Q120715121, with effective dates of December 7, 2022 through December 7, 2023. This claim concerns an
accident which was reported to have occurred on May 19, 2023 on or around 7857 Highway 460 West in Hazel
Green, Kentucky.
Your client, Michael Crader, reported being injured driving a 2023 Can-Am Defender. A claim has been
presented for Personal Injury Protection coverage for injuries your client sustained. It has been confirmed that
your client received Personal Injury Protection coverage through Kentucky Farm Bureau who insures the striking
vehicle.
Also, please note the 2023 Canam Defender is not listed under your client's Auto Insurance Policy.
Please review the policy language below from your client's Kentucky Auto Policy AP-KY (Ed. 10/14) UF-B316:
AFKP01 (Ed. 11/17) UF-B038
PERSONAL INJURY PROTECTION ENDORSEMENT —KENTUCKY
NONDUPLICATION OF BENEFITS
No person "we" protect will recover duplicate benefits for the same elements of loss under this or any
other similar motor vehicle insurance including self-insurance.
EXCLUSIONS —What We Do Not Cover
"We" do not provide coverage for bodily injury sustained by:
11. "anyone we protect" while "occupying" or struck by a "motor vehicle" owned by "you" ora
"relative" and not insured for "PIP" under this policy.
As the 2023 Can-Am Defender is not insured for Personal Injury Protection with Erie Insurance, we would not
extend Personal Injury Benefits for this loss.
