COMMONWEALTH OF KENTUCKY
MORGAN CIRCUIT COURT
CIVIL ACTION NO. 24-Cl-00065
Electronically Filed
MICHAEL CRADER PLAINTIFF
VS. ANSWER
MEGAN COMBS DEFENDANT
Comes the defendant, Megan Combs, by counsel, and for her Answer to the plaintiff's
Complaint states as follows:
FIRST DEFENSE
1. The plaintiff's Complaint fails to state a cause of action against the defendant
upon which relief can be granted.
2. The defendant is without sufficient knowledge or information to admit or deny
the allegations contained in paragraph 1 of the plaintiff's Complaint.
3. The defendant denies the allegations contained in paragraphs 2, 3 and 4 of the
plaintiff's Complaint.
4, The defendant is without sufficient knowledge or information to admit or deny
the allegations contained in paragraph 5 and paragraph 6 of the plaintiff's
Complaint.
5. All allegations in the plaintiff's Complaint not specifically admitted herein are
denied.

SECONDDEFENSE
The defendant pleads and relies upon the failure on the part of the plaintiff, to exercise the
requisite degree of due and reasonable care for his own safety and welfare, and said negligence on the
part of the plaintiff was a substantial factor in causing the injuries and damages of which he now
complains, and the defendant asserts said negligence, whether comparative, contributory, or otherwise,
as a bar, in whole or in part, to the claims asserted against the defendant.
THIRD DEFENSE
The defendant affirmatively states that the injuries of which the plaintiff now complains were
caused or brought about by some other means, or were the result of the negligence of some third party
whose negligence was the primary and efficient cause of the accident and the plaintiff's alleged injuries,
or were the result of an unavoidable accident, upon all of which the defendant specifically pleads as a bar
to the claims asserted bythe plaintiff.
FOURTH DEFENSE
The defendant pleads and relies on the applicable statue of limitations as a bar to the
claim asserted bythe plaintiff against the defendant.
FIFTH DEFENSE
The defendant pleads and relies upon the provisions of KRS 304.39 et seq., as a bar, in whole or
in part, to the claims asserted by the plaintiff.
SIXTH DEFENSE
The defendant, atthis time, affirmatively pleads and relies on the defenses as designated in CR
8.03 and CR 12.02 subject to facts to be determined through discovery.

SEVENTH DEFENSE
The defendant states that there is no basis for the plaintiff's request for attorney fees either based
on the facts as alleged in the Complaint or under any applicable law, therefore, that portion of the
plaintiff's prayer for relief should be stricken.
WHEREFORE, having fully answered the plaintiff's Complaint, the defendant Megan Combs,
demands that the Complaint be dismissed and that the plaintiff take nothing thereby and demands
judgment for her costs and all other relief that which she may appear entitled.
