Comes the defendant, Megan Combs, by counsel, and pursuant to CR 33, propounds the
following Interrogatories to the plaintiff, Michael Crader, with the requirement that said
Interrogatories be answered under oath within thirty (30) days after service hereof:
INTERROGATORY NO. 1: State the plaintiff's full name, address, marital status, social
security number, date of birth, and driver's license number.
INTERROGATORY NO. 2: State the name and address of any and all persons known
to the plaintiff or his attorney who were witnesses to the accident that is the basis for this cause
of action.
INTERROGATORY NO. 3: In addition to those named in Interrogatory No. 2 above,
please state the name and address of any and all persons known to the plaintiff, or his attorney,
who have any knowledge of any facts and circumstances concerning the subject matter of this
litigation.
INTERROGATORY NO. 4: Please state in itemized form all medical expenses incurred
by the plaintiff which he claims are a direct and proximate result of the automobile accident
which is the basis of his Complaint. Include the name and address of the provider of medical
1

services, the date of examination or treatment, the type of treatment received, and the charge
or expense incurred.
INTERROGATORY NO. 5: Please state whether or not the plaintiff claims to have lost
any wages or earnings as a result of the injuries allegedly sustained in the automobile accident
which is the basis of the plaintiff's Complaint; if "yes," state in itemized form all such lost wages
or earnings, state the name and address of the plaintiff's employer, the salary or hourly wage of
the plaintiff, the specific dates which the plaintiff alleges to have lost wages or earnings, and the
name ofthe plaintiff's immediate supervisor.
INTERROGATORY NO. 6: Please state specifically the injuries you are claiming were
caused by the automobile accident that is the basis for your Complaint.
INTERROGATORY NO. 7: Please state whether or not the plaintiff claims that any of
the injuries from the accident are permanent injuries, and if the plaintiff is claiming any
permanent injury, state specifically all permanent injuries suffered by the plaintiff and the name
and address of each doctor, physician, surgeon, chiropractor, or other provider of medical care
services who has diagnosed that the injuries are permanent in nature.
INTERROGATORY NO. 8: Please state the name and address of each and every
doctor, physician, surgeon, chiropractor or other provider of medical services who has treated
the plaintiff or examined said plaintiff for purposes of consultation or evaluation, for the injuries
allegedly sustained in the accident that is the subject of this litigation, and provide the date of
examination or treatment, and the type of treatment received.
INTERROGATORY NO. 9: Please state the most recent medical treatment received by
the plaintiff as a result of injuries allegedly sustained in this accident, the date ofthe most recent

treatment, and the doctor or physician providing said treatment, and state whether or not the
plaintiff currently has an appointment or is scheduled for any medical treatment in the future,
and the appointment date and the name of the doctor, physician or chiropractor where said
treatment is scheduled.
INTERROGATORY NO. 10: In addition to those given in answer to Interrogatories No.
7 and 8 above, state the name and address of each and every doctor, physician, surgeon,
chiropractor or other provider of medical services who has examined and/or treated the plaintiff
in the last ten (10) years, and state the date of each examination or treatment and state the
condition or conditions for which each has examined and/or treated the plaintiff.
INTERROGATORY NO. 11: if you have had any x-rays, MRIs, CT scans, nerve
conduction tests, or any other diagnostic tests or procedures, identify the test or procedure, the
name and address of the hospital, clinic, doctor’s office, or other facility where the test or
procedure was performed, the date of the test or procedure, and the doctor, physician or other
medical provider who referred you for each test or procedure and whether or not you have
possession of any original or copy of x-ray films, MRI, CT scans, or any test results or reports
related thereto.
INTERROGATORY NO. 12: State whether or not the plaintiff has rejected the
provisions of the Kentucky Motor Vehicle Reparations Act.
INTERROGATORY NO. 13: State in itemized form any and all payments that have been
made to the plaintiff, or that have been made on behalf of said plaintiff, as a result of the
automobile accident that is the basis of the plaintiff's Complaint, and please identify the source
or provider ofthese benefits or payments and the amount ofthe payment or benefits received.

INTERROGATORY NO. 14: If you have been reimbursed byany person or organization
for all or part of your lost income, state the name and address of the person or organization
reimbursing you for your lost income, and state the total amount which has been reimbursed to
you.
INTERROGATORY NO. 15: State whether or not the plaintiff is currently employed,
and if so, please state the name and address of said plaintiff's employer, the salary or hourly wage
earned by the plaintiff, and the name of the plaintiff's immediate supervisor.
INTERROGATORY NO. 16: State the specific facts on which the plaintiff relies to
support the allegation in his Complaint that the defendant, Megan Combs, operated her motor
vehicle in a negligent manner, and state the name and address of all persons who have
knowledge of such facts.
INTERROGATORY NO. 17: Please state any witnesses the plaintiffs intend to call to
testify upon the trial of this action. State the name and address of any witnesses and state the
subject matter on which each witness is expected to testify.
INTERROGATORY NO. 18: State whether or not the plaintiffs intend to call any expert
witness or witnesses, medical or otherwise, to testify upon the trial of this action. Ifthe answer
to this Interrogatory is "yes," state the name and address of any and all expert witnesses and
state the subject matter on which each expert witness is expected to testify, and state the
substance of the facts and opinions to which the expert is expected to testify and a summary of
the grounds for each opinion pursuant to the provisions of CR 26.02(4)(a)(I).
INTERROGATORY NO. 19: State whether the plaintiff has been involved in any other
motor vehicle accidents, either before or subsequent to the accident that is the basis of this

Complaint, and if so, please state as follows: (a) date of accident; (b) name of all persons involved
in the accident; (c) name of the police agency that investigated the accident; (d) whether or not
the plaintiff was acting in the course of employment at the time of the accident; (e) the extent of
any injuries sustained by the plaintiff in the accident; (f) whether or not the accident resulted in
litigation and if so, the style and current status ofthe litigation.
INTERROGATORY NO. 20: State whether the plaintiff has ever filed a claim for workers
compensation benefits, Social Security Disability benefits, and/ or Supplemental Security Income
(SS!); and if “yes”, for each time you have applied for such benefits. (a) state the date(s) on which
you applied for benefits; (b) describe the type of injury or specific reasons for your application;
(c) state the name and address of the plaintiffs employer at the time of the claim; (d) state the
name and address of all doctors, physicians, surgeons, chiropractors or any other provider of
medical care who examined or evaluated the plaintiff for the injury and/or condition that was
the basis for our application; and (e) what, if any, benefits have you received?
INTERROGATORY NO. 21: Has the plaintiff ever undergone or received any treatment for
any mental and/or emotional disturbance or condition, including, but not limited to, alcohol
abuse and/or drug abuse; if “yes” identify the specific basis for any treatment, the date(s) of
treatment, and the individual, clinic, or facility that provided treatment.
INTERROGATORY NO. 22: Pursuant to CR 8.01, state the total amount of damages
being claimed by the plaintiff in his Complaint.
INTERROGATORY NO. 23: Have you received, and/or do you expect to receive,
benefits or payments from Medicare or Medicaid for treatment related to the injuries you are

claiming from this motor vehicle accident? If “yes”, state the total amount of any applicable
medicare or medicaid lien.
INTERROGATORY NO. 24: Please provide any and all social media posts, including but not
limited to facebook, twitter, instagram and topix, that you have either personally made or have
observed regarding this accident.
INTERROGATORY NO. 25: Do you agree to supplement your answers to these
interrogatories if additional or supplemental information becomes known or available to you or
your attorney?
