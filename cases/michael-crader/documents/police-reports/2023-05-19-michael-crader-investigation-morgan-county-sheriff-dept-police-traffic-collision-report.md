Unit 2 was operating West on U.S. Highway 460 West. Unit 2 is a side-by-side being used for farm use.
Unit 2 had a small trailer attached which contained one bale of hay. Unit 2 was makinga left turn onto a
small gravel road on their farm. Unit 2 operator advised he gavealeft arm signal indicating he he was
makinga left turn then began to turn.
Unit 1 was operating West on U.S Highway 460 West and was behind Unit 2. Unit 1attempted to go
around Unit 1on the left side. The front right side of Unit 1struck the front left side of Unit 2. Unit 1operator
advised she approached Unit 2 and attempted to pass. She advised she did not notice Unit 2 turning until it
was too late. She advised she then struck Unit 2. After the collision, Unit 1 traveled off the roadway on the
left side, and struck an earthen embankment. Unit 1came to rest against a fence which sustained damage.
Unit 2 came to rest inthe roadway.
Unit 4 operator and both rear passengers complained of minor injuries and were transported to St. Claire
Regional Medical Center by Morgan County EMS. The front passenger of Unit 1sustained suspected
serious injuries. She was airlifted to Cabell Huntington Huntington Hospital by Airmethods.
Unit 2 operator was ejected from the driver seat and landed inthe roadway. He was airlifted to the
University of Kentucky Medical Center byAir Evac 118. Unit 1operator sustained anapparent serious
injury and complained of back pain. Unit 2 passenger advised she wasn't injured.
The non-venicle property damage that was sustained included a wooden fence. The property owner of the
fence is Unit 2 operator.
The point of impact was determined to be in the East Bound Lane of U.S. Highway 460 West. A double
yellow dividing line was present at the collision scene indicating a no passing zone.
Victoria Halsey was operating a vehicle directly behind Unit 1and wittnessed the collision. She advised she
was operating West on U.S. Highway 460 West and was passed by Unit 1approximately half of a mile prior
to the collision. She advised she was operating approximately fifty five miles per hour and it appearred that
Unit 1 was driving at a high rate of speed. She stated she observed Unit 2 turning and and Unit 1 went
around Unit 2 and hit it.
