Officers were dispatched to the listed intersection for a report of a vehicle accident. When officers arrived
on scene, they found Unit 2 still in the intersection with Unit 1 further along EB on Algonquin Pkwy.
The listed operator of Unit 1 stated she was going EB on Algonquin when she hit Unit 2, causing severe
front-end damage to both vehicles. The operator of Unit 1 stated that she could not remember if she had
the green light or not. After striking Unit 2, Unit 1 traveled further EB along Algonquin to eventually stop
when it hit a tree. Unit 1 had both the steering wheel and curtain/feet airbags deploy.
The passenger of Unit 1 was taken to St Mary’s Hospital for arm and rib pain.
The listed operator/owner of Unit 2 stated she was going NB on Cypress St when she had the green light
and while crossing the intersection, she was struck by Unit 1. This spun Unit 2 around to face the opposite
direction. Unit 2 received severe damage to its front end. Unit 2 had both the steering wheel and
curtain/feet airbags deployed.
The operator of Unit 2 was taken to Norton West off 28th and Broadway for neck pain.
Pictures of both vehicles were taken and uploaded to AXON.
