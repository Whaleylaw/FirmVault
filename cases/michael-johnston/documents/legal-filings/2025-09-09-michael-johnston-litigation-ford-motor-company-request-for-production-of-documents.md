COMMONWEALTH OF KENTUCKY

DEPARTMENT OF WORKERS’ CLAIMS

BEFORE HON._____________, ALJ

CLAIM NO. 2023-84880

MICHAEL JOHNSTONPLAINTIFF

v. **REQUEST**** FOR PRODUCTION OF DOCUMENTS**

FORD MOTOR COMPANYDEFENDANT

* * * * *

Comes the Defendant, Ford Motor Company, by counsel, and requests Plaintiff to produce the following documents within thirty (30) days pursuant to CR 34:

1. Copies of any unpaid or outstanding medical bills, expenses, or charges.
2. If Plaintiff has worked anywhere other than for Defendant since date of alleged accident, please produce wage records, earning statements, and pay stubs for such employment.
3. If Plaintiff is claiming concurrent employment on date of accident, please produce wage records for such concurrent employment.
4. If Plaintiff has applied for or received Social Security Disability benefits, please produce all records pertaining to application and receipt of any such benefits, including records regarding disability benefits, disability determination, expert reports, and medical records.  In lieu of producing these documents, the Plaintiff may sign and return the attached Release and Consent form to the Social Security Administration authorizing the undersigned counsel to request these documents.
5. Please produce a signed and executed HIPAA-compliant Release and Consent of Disclosure form (attached) authorizing undersigned counsel to request any and all records, documents and imaging films from Plaintiff’s treating medical providers. 
6. A signed and executed Release and Consent to Disclosure form (attached) authorizing undersigned counsel to request any and all records, documents and information in the Kentucky Labor Department’s possession pertaining to any workers’ compensation claim involving Plaintiff.
7. Please produce a signed and executed Release and Consent to Disclose form (attached) authorizing the undersigned counsel to request any and all employment and/or wage records, documents and information from Plaintiff’s previous or current employers.

This is a continuing request, and Plaintiff shall produce any other records, documents or papers requested above that come into Plaintiff’s possession, or the possession of Plaintiff’s counsel, after the initial response is provided.

This, the 9th day of September 2025.
