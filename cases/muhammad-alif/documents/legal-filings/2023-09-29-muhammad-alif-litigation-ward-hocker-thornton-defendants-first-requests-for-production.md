Plaintiff’s attorney, prepared by the Plaintiff, his attorney, or any other person or entity, for 
inspection and/or copying within the time allowed by the civil rules: 
REQUEST NO. 1: Any and all medical bills from physicians or surgeons; hospital 
bills; outpatient treatment bills; physical therapy treatment bills; prescription and nonprescription 
drug bills; bills for medical appliances; and any and all other bills or documents to support the

which the Plaintiff claims were necessary as a result of the accident which forms the basis of his
Complaint.

each of the specific allegations of damages, including documentation of lost wages, past and 
future.

documentation which in any way relates to the subject matter of the Plaintiff's claim herein
