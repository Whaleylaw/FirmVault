Come the Defendants, Jacquelyn B. Wickcliffe and Harbor House of Louisville, by and through counsel, and hereby propounds the following Interrogatories to the Plaintiff, Muhammad Alif, to be answered under oath, in writing, and within thirty (30) days of the date of service herein, in accordance with and pursuant to Rule 33 of the Kentucky Rules of Civil Procedure.

<u>**INTERROGATORY NO. 1**</u>**:**Please identify yourself fully, giving your full name, age, birth date, social security number, residence address, business address and occupation.

<u>**INTERROGATORY NO. 2**</u>**:**State when, where and by whom you were first treated for your injuries. 

<u>**INTERROGATORY NO. 3**</u>**:**Please state each and every date on which you were examined or treated by any doctor, physician, hospital or medical provider with respect to any injury or disability which you claim was sustained or suffered as a result of the alleged occurrence, setting forth in detail as to each such date or examination or treatment;

A)The name and address of each doctor, physician or medical provider;

B)The nature and extent of the examination or treatment received from each such doctor, physician or medical provider;

C)Any diagnosis or prognosis made by each such doctor, physician, medical provider or any other person or organization, on account of each such person; and 

D)The amount charged by each such doctor, physician or medical provider for treatment or examination given, fully itemized as indicated in any bill rendered therefore.

<u>**INTERROGATORY NO. 4**</u>**:**Are you presently receiving treatment or care for any medical condition involving any injury, illness or disability which is a result of the alleged occurrence?  If so, please state with respect to any such treatment;

A)The name and address of the physician providing the treatment; and

B)The nature of the treatment being rendered.

<u>**INTERROGATORY NO. 5**</u>**:**State the date on which you fully recovered from any injury, illness or disability which is a result of the alleged occurrence.

<u>**INTERROGATORY NO. 6**</u>**:**If you claim you are not fully recovered; state the nature of the injuries, conditions and disability from which you are presently suffering.

<u>**INTERROGATORY NO. 7**</u>**:**State whether or not you are claiming any permanent injuries or disfigurement resulting from the alleged occurrence and, if so, state the type and nature of any disability or disfigurement, and the name of any doctor that you expect to so testify.

<u>**INTERROGATORY NO. 8:**</u>Have you ever sustained personal injuries in any type of accident before or after the one referred to in your Complaint?  If so, state:

A)The date and place of each such accident;

B)The nature of the injury sustained in each;

C)The names and addresses of all hospitals and doctors from whom treatment was received;

D)The names and addresses of all persons, if any, against whom claims were made; and

E)The name and location of each court or commission in which suit was brought or claim made.

<u>**INTERROGATORY NO. 9**</u>**:**Are you claiming any loss of wages as a result of the alleged occurrence?  If so, please state:

A)The total amount claimed; and

B)The factual evidentiary basis you intend to rely on in proving this claim.

<u>**INTERROGATORY NO. 10**</u>**:**Are you claiming any loss of future earning capacity as a result of the alleged occurrence?

A)The total amount claimed; and

B)The factual and evidentiary basis you intend to rely on in proving this claim.

<u>**INTERROGATORY NO. 11**</u>**:**Please state the names and addresses of each person or organization by whom or with whom you were employed at the time of the occurrence or have been employed with during the five year period immediately preceding the alleged occurrence.

<u>**INTERROGATORY NO. 12**</u>**:**As to each person or organization referred to in the answer to the preceding interrogatory, please state:

A)The dates of each such employment;

B)The nature of the work or duties;

C)The place or places where such work or duties were performed; and

D)The average weekly or monthly compensation from such employment.

<u>**INTERROGATORY NO. 13**</u>**:**Please state the names and addresses of each person or organization by whom or with whom you have been employed since the time of the occurrence.

<u>**INTERROGATORY NO. 14**</u>**:**Have any of your medical bills been paid by any person or entity other than yourself, including any insurance company, agency, corporation, or government, and, if so, state:

A)By whom and the date paid; and

B)The specific medical bills and the amount paid on each bill. 

<u>**INTERROGATORY NO. 15**</u>**:**Are you now receiving or have you ever received any disability pension, income or insurance or any workman's compensation from any agency, company, person, corporation, state or government, and, if so, state:

A)The nature of any such payments;

B)The dates you received such income;

C)For what injuries or disability did you receive it and how such injury occurred or disability arose; and

D)By whom paid and the amounts received.

<u>**INTERROGATORY NO. 16**</u>**:**Please describe in full detail, including the exact time, date and place, how the alleged occurrence happened, giving all events in detail and the order in which they occurred, before, at the time of, and after the occurrence, which had any bearing on the cause and manner of the happening of the alleged occurrence.

<u>**INTERROGATORY NO. 17**</u>**:**To the best of your knowledge, please identify and provide the address of any and all persons with knowledge of the facts and circumstances regarding the alleged occurrence.

<u>**INTERROGATORY NO. 18**</u>**:**Has a written or otherwise recorded statement been taken from any witness?  If so, for each statement, state:

A)The name and address of the person from whom this statement was taken;

B)The name and address of the person who took the statement; and

C)The name and address of each person who has a copy of the original of any such statement.

<u>**INTERROGATORY NO. 19**</u>**:**Please state the substance of any conversation you have had with the Defendant(s) and/or any agent, servant and/or employee of the Defendant(s) with regard to the subject matter of this litigation, identifying the name of the person with whom such conversation was had.

<u>**INTERROGATORY NO. 20**</u>**:**As to each person that you expect to testify as an expert witness at the trial of this matter, please state the following:

A)The name, address and occupation of that witness;

B)The subject matter or area on which such expert is expected to testify;

C)The substance of the facts and opinions to which the expert is expected to testify and a summary of the grounds for each opinion, and

D)Whether the witness will testify in person or by deposition.

<u>**INTERROGATORY NO. 21**</u>**:**As to your claim for pain and suffering, both past and future, please state:

A)The total amount of money claimed; and

B)The factual and evidentiary basis you intend to rely on in proving this claim.

<u>**INTERROGATORY NO. 22**</u>**:**Please give the name, address and telephone number of all persons you intend to call as witness to testify at trial.

<u>**INTERROGATORY NO. 23:**</u>Are you eligible for Medicare or Medicaid?  If so, please state whether a Medicare or Medicaid lien exists with regard to your injuries allegedly resulting from the subject accident, and state the amount of said lien.

<u>**INTERROGATORY NO. 24:**</u>Please identify by name and address all physicians, clinics, hospitals, chiropractors, physical therapists, or other healthcare providers with whom you have sought treatment within the past fifteen years, and for each provider identified, please state the approximate date(s) or time period during which you sought treatment, and the condition which was treated.  

<u>**INTERROGATORY NO. 25:**</u>Please identify your primary healthcare provider(s) or family doctor(s) with whom you have treated for the past fifteen years.

<u>**INTERROGATORY NO. 26:**</u>Have you ever applied for disability, unemployment, or worker’s compensation?  If so, please specify the reason(s) and date(s) on which you applied, and the outcome of the application.

<u>**INTERROGATORY NO. 27:**</u>Please identify all hospitals or pharmacies where you have had prescriptions filled for the past 5 years.
