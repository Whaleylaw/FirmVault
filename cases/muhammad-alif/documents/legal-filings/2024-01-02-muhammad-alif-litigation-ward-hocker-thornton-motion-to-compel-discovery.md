***** ***** *****
Comes the Defendants, Jacquelyn B. Wickliffe and Harbor House of Louisville, by and 
through counsel, pursuant to CR 37.01, and for their Motion to Compel responses discovery 
requests propounded upon the Plaintiff, Muhammad Alif, hereby states as follows:
On or about September 29, 2023, Defendants propounded Interrogatories and Requests for 
Production of Documents upon Plaintiff. See Exhibit 1. Pursuant to the Civil Rules, Plaintiff’s 
discovery responses were to be tendered by October 30, 2023. To date, no responses to any 
discovery have been received by the undersigned. 
In an attempt to resolve this discovery dispute extrajudicially, the undersigned contacted 
Plaintiff’s counsel on November 29, 2023 seeking the status of service of discovery responses. See
Exhibit 2. Plaintiff’s counsel has not responded to the November 29, 2023 letter. 
As of the date of this Motion, Plaintiff’s discovery responses are now three (3) months 
overdue. Pursuant to CR 37.01(b)(i), the Defendant is permitted to move the Court for an order 
compelling Plaintiff to respond to its discovery requests. Due to the significant length of time that 
has passed since the tendering of discovery, the Defendant respectfully requests that the Court 
compel the Plaintiff to respond to same within fourteen (14) days.
