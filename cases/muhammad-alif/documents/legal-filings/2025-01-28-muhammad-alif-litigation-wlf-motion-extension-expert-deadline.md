MOTION FOR EXTENSION OF TIME 
Comes now the Plaintiff, Muhammad Alif (hereinafter “Plaintiff”), and respectfully moves 
this Honorable Court for an extension of the expert disclosure deadline in this case. In support 
thereof, Plaintiff states as follows: 
 Plaintiff initiated this action on September 8, 2023. Following the exchange of written 
discovery and the taking of depositions, Plaintiff moved this Court for a trail date. The Court 
subsequently issued a Scheduling Order, setting a deadline of February 3, 2025, for the designation 
of expert witnesses. 
 Since the initiation of this case, Plaintiff has diligently sought to secure a qualified expert 
witness to support his claims. Plaintiff has engaged in an exhaustive search, consulting with 
multiple experts who, due to scheduling or availability issues, have been unable to commit to this 
case within the current timeframe. Despite Plaintiff’s best efforts, unforeseen circumstances have 
delayed the finalization of an expert witness. The specific nature of the case requires an expert
