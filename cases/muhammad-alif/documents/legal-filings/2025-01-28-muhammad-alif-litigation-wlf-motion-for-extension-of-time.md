 Comes the Plaintiff, Muhammad Alif, by counsel, and gives notice that he will, on Monday, 
February 3, 2025, at or around the hour of 11:45 a.m., EST, or as soon thereafter as Counsel may 
be heard, make the instant Motion and tender the Order set forth herewith. 
MOTION FOR EXTENSION OF TIME 
Comes now the Plaintiff, Muhammad Alif (hereinafter “Plaintiff”), and respectfully moves 
this Honorable Court for an extension of the expert disclosure deadline in this case. In support 
thereof, Plaintiff states as follows: 
 Plaintiff initiated this action on September 8, 2023. Following the exchange of written 
discovery and the taking of depositions, Plaintiff moved this Court for a trail date. The Court 
subsequently issued a Scheduling Order, setting a deadline of February 3, 2025, for the designation 
of expert witnesses. 
 Since the initiation of this case, Plaintiff has diligently sought to secure a qualified expert 
witness to support his claims. Plaintiff has engaged in an exhaustive search, consulting with 
multiple experts who, due to scheduling or availability issues, have been unable to commit to this 
case within the current timeframe. Despite Plaintiff’s best efforts, unforeseen circumstances have 
delayed the finalization of an expert witness. The specific nature of the case requires an expert 

 2 
with specialized knowledge and experience, and identifying an available expert within the current 
timeframe has proven more challenging than anticipated. 
 Plaintiff’s counsel has acted in good faith and with due diligence to comply with the 
Court’s deadlines. This request is not made for purposes of delay but to ensure justice and allow 
for a faith and thorough presentation of the evidence. Granting this extension will ensure that the 
case proceeds based on a complete and properly supported evidentiary record. Securing the 
appropriate expert witness is critical for the fair adjudication of the issues in this case. Plaintiff 
seeks to avoid any potential imbalance that may arise from the absence of expert testimony and 
wishes to provide the Court and the jury with the most thorough and credible evidence available. 
 Granting this extension will not unduly prejudice the Defendant(s). The trial date remains 
June 3, 2025, and this extension will afford both parties adequate time to prepare. Plaintiff 
respectfully requests an extension of thirty (30) days to the current deadline to allow sufficient 
time to secure an expert witness and ensure comprehensive preparation for trial. 
 WHEREFORE, Plaintiff respectfully requests this Court grant an extension of the expert 
witness deadline to March 5, 2025, and for such other and further relief as the Court deems just 
and proper.
