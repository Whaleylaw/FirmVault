Having come before the Court on Defendants, Jacquelyn B. Wickliffe and Harbor House 
of Louisville’s Motion for CR 35 Examinations and for Costs, and this Court having considered 
said motion, having reviewed the record herein, and having been otherwise sufficiently advised;

within 14 days of this Order;

to Defendants. An Affidavit of Counsel regarding Fees and Costs shall be submitted by counsel.
