***** ***** *****
Notice is hereby given that counsel for the Defendants, Jacquelyn B. Wickliffe and Harbor 
House of Louisville, will proceed to take the deposition of Dr. William Hwang on July 17, 2025 
at 11:00 a.m. EST/ 10:00 a.m. CST. This deposition will be taken remotely by a stenographer as 
well as recorded by video by the court reporter using Zoom’s recording function, which will be 
considered the official video copy for the record. As the court reporter will also be attending 
remotely, the deponent will be sworn in remotely pursuant to Kentucky law.
The official video recording by the court reporter will lock or “pin” the screen of the 
deponent so that the video recording equipment will remain stationary at all times on the witness 
pursuant to CR 30.02(4)(b). However, if exhibits are shared electronically, such exhibits will be 
side-by-side with the witness so that the witness can always be seen on the recording. CR 
30.02(4)(b) permits video recordings to display exhibits or other demonstrative proof. Finally, as 
this will be a video deposition, all objections will be reserved and shall not be stated on the video 
recording except for objections relating to the form of the question pursuant to CR 30.02(4)(e).
Said deposition is to be used for discovery and all other purposes allowed under the 
Kentucky Rules of Civil Procedure.
