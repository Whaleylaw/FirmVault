Personal Injury Fee Agreement 
(For Representation for any bodily injury claim up to Trial in Civil Court) 
I hereby retain and employ the law office of The Whaley Law Firm, PSC (herein after Law Office) as my attorneys to 
represent me in my claims arising from an auto accident on or about ___________________________, 20___ in 
_________________________ county, in the state of ______________. 
I hereby authorize any action on my behalf, including the institution of legal proceedings that The Whaley Law Firm, 
PSC. deems proper and necessary. 
I desire The Whaley Law Firm, PSC to provide legal representation on my behalf and I understand that the Law Office is 
willing to provide said representation for the consideration and according to the terms set forth: 
I agree and understand that I will pay The Whaley Law Firm, PSC. an attorney fee of 
33.336% (thirty-three and one third precent) of any and all amounts recovered from each 
and every Defendant prior to a lawsuit being filed. I agree and understand that I will pay 
The Whaley Law Firm, PSC. an attorney fee of 40% (Forty percent) after suit has been 
filed on any and all amounts recovered from each and every Defendant. 
In addition to the attorney fee described above, I hereby agree and understand that all costs incurred in pursuit of my 
claims shall be paid by The Whaley Law Firm, PSC. and reimbursed from any amounts recovered. Said costs include, but 
may not be limited to court costs, subpoena costs, costs for photocopies, postage, depositions, court reporters, travel, cost 
for obtaining medical records/bills and any other evidence independently organized and a settlement proposal prepared, 
the cost of expert witnesses, interest on case development loans incurred by the Whaley Law Firm, PSC. for and on my 
behalf or the behalf of my claims/cases. The attorney fee shall be calculated upon the full amount of any recovery prior to 
any deduction of expenses discussed herein. 
I also understand that if there is no monetary recovery, The Whaley Law Firm, PSC. will not seek reimbursement of any 
case expenses or other costs, or interest from me. In other words, I understand that if I do not receive a monetary recovery, 
I will not be responsible for reimbursing The Whaley Law Firm, PSC. for any expenses or costs that The Whaley Law 
Firm, PSC. incurs on my behalf or on the behalf of my claims/cases. 
I hereby grant The Whaley Law Firm, PSC. full permission to disclose my medical records and medical bills to any and 
all persons, businesses, insurance companies, or any other individuals or entities whom they deem helpful or useful to 
my claims/cases. This specifically includes any person The Whaley Law Firm, PSC. may decide to have review, organize, 
or otherwise view my medical records and any person they may chose to help prepare a settlement package on my behalf 
or on behalf of my claims/cases.
