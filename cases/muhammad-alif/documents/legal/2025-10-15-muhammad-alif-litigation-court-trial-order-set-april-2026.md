This matter came before the Court at a pretrial conference on October 14, 2025. The parties
requested a new jury trial date be scheduled. This proceeding was reported by digital recording no. 30-07-
25-DR-038.
Wherefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that: 
1. This matter is set for a trial on April 14, 2026, at 10:00 a.m. The Court has allotted three (3) days for 
the trial. No new final pretrial conference is scheduled at this time.
2. No continuance shall be granted, even by agreement of the parties, without prior approval of the 
Court. Any request for a continuance shall comply with CR 43.03.
3. At least 45 days before the trial, the parties shall attempt to resolve their differences through 
mediation.
4. The following time frames may be changed by written agreement of counsel without notice to or 
approval of the Court.
5. At least 120 days before trial, Plaintiff shall identify each person whom he expects to call as expert 
witnesses at trial and comply with CR 26.02(4)(a)(I) regardless of whether this information has 
previously been requested through interrogatories. Failure to do so may result in the suppression of 
the expert’s testimony.
