(a) Dr. William Hwang

Dr. Hwang 1s a physician who treated plaintiff for traumatic brain and other injuries sustained in the motor vehicle collision of November 8, 2022. Dr. Hwang’s curriculum vitae is attached hereto as Exhibit 1. It is anticipated that Dr. Hwang will testify regarding Mr. Alif’s injury history (i.e. relating Plaintiffs injuries set forth above and treatment due to the November 8, 2022, collision), his diagnosis, the mechanism of injury, his course of treatment, any other courses of treatment undertaken by Plaintiff related to the motor vehicle collision and his prognosis for future treatments and/or complaints.
