Officer responded to a Non Injury Accident on Shelbyville Road between New LaGrange Road and Oxmoor
Lane, Louisville, Kentucky 40222.
Unit 2 was stopped in traffic facing E/B in lane 2 on Shelbyville Road. Unit 1was traveling E/B on
Shelbyville Road in lane 1. Unit 3 was traveling E/B on Shelbyville Road in lane 2. Unit 1 attempted to
change from lane 1 to lane 2 and struck Unit 2 on the rear bumper and failed to yield the right-of-way to
Unit 3 causing Unit 3 to strike her vehicle on the rear bumper.
Unit 1 sustained minor damage to the front bumper and moderate damage to the rear bumper.
Unit 2 sustained minor damage to the rear bumper.
Unit 3 sustained moderate damage to the front bumper.
There were no injuries at the scene.
