necessary to make the request inclusive rather than exclusive.

"without limitation."

pertaining to, evidencing, or any way relevant to any issue, claim, defense, or question posed by 
the interrogatory or request within the meaning of CR 26 or any other applicable Kentucky Rule 
of Civil Procedure or Evidence. 
 D. “Document” as used herein means anything created or reduced to writing whether 
produced on paper or by electronic mean including but not limited to letters, faxes, emails, 
telephone messages, texts, tweets, memoranda, data stored on any computer hardware or software, 
in the cloud, on an electronic system or otherwise. 
 E. Plural shall include the singular and the singular shall include the plural. 
 F. The term "present” used herein to refer to a point in time shall mean the date upon 
which Responses to the Requests are being made. 
 G. “You” or “Your” or “Defendant” as used herein means the Defendant CAAL 
Worldwide, Inc. and any and all of the Defendant’s employees, agents, or third persons who are 
under the supervision or control of the Defendant. 
 H. “Identify” or “explain” or “state” as used in these Interrogatories with respect to 
any person or thing means to state fully all who or what the thing or person is and if relating to a 
person to give the person’s full name, address, and other contact information to the extent known
