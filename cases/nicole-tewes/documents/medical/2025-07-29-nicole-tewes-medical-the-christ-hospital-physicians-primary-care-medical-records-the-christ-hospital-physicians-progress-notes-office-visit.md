daily considered due to anticipated stress. Excessive sweating noted, possibly from bupropion, not severe enough to change

whiplash, exacerbated by stress and position during accident.
- Recommend chiropractic care for neck pain management.
- Use existing muscle relaxants as needed, primarily at night due to sedative effects.
- Take ibuprofen or similar NSAID daily for inflammation.
- Consider imaging if symptoms do not improve with current management.
Excessive sweating (generalized hyperhidrosis)
Experiences excessive sweating,exacerbated by bupropion. Sweating not severe enough to interfere with daily life or selfconfidence.
- Monitor sweating symptoms and discuss alternative medications if it becomes bothersome.
