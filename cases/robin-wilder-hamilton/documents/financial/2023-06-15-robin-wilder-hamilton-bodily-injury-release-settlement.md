representatives, predecessors, affiliates, subsidiaries, parents, employees, successors, legal 
representatives and insurers attorneys of and from any and all claims, actions, causes of action,

releasee now has/have or which may hereafter accrue on account of or in any way growing out of any

and that the payment made is not to be construed as an admission of liability on the party of the party or

warrants that it will, dispose of all liability of releasees to claimant(s), his/her heirs, assigns, or any other

made by any person or entity for which the releasors might be liable, directly or indirectly, plaintiff, on 
behalf of himself and his heirs and assigns, agrees to and will, hold harmless, defend and indemnify the 
releasees, which are collectively referred to as “indemnitees” from and against any and all claims, 
demands, suits, causes of actions, and/or liabilities arising from or associated with the conduct and/or 
claim above and from any and all liability for such a lien, including all costs, expenses and attorney's 
fees in defending such lien. Releasor’s duty and obligation to defend, and indemnify releasee are 
intended to operate and be applicable even if the claim, demand, or cause of action is based in whole or 
part on any one or more of the indemnitee’s alleged, sole, joint, several or concurrent negligence, gross 
negligence, malice, intentional act, fraud, strict liability, statutory violation or breach of contract 
The undersigned hereby declare(s) and represent(s) that the injuries sustained are or may be 
permanent and progressive and that recovery there from is uncertain and indefinite and in making this 
Release it is understood and agreed, that the undersigned rely(ies) wholly upon the undersigned’s 
judgment, belief and knowledge of the nature, extent, effect and duration of said injuries and liability 
therefore and is made without reliance upon any statement or representation of the party or parties 
hereby released or their representatives or by any physician or surgeon by them employed. 
The undersigned further declare(s) and represent(s) that no promise, inducement or agreement not 
herein expressed has been made to the undersigned, and that this Release contains the entire agreement 
between the parties thereto, and that terms of this Release are contractual and not a mere recital.
