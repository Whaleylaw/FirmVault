Unit 1 was traveling South on KY377 and made a left turn into a construction site and into the path of unit 2
which was traveling North on KY377.
The operator of Unit 1 advised that he did not see Unit 2 approaching and began to make a left turn into
the construction site.
The operator of Unit 2 advised she was traveling on the road and noticed Unit 1 making the left turn in front
of her and was unable to stop prior to impact.
The operator of Unit 1 did not complain of any injuries and the operator of Unit 2 complained of Chest and
Knee Pain but refused Medical Treatment and Transport.
Unit 1 received moderate damage to the front bumper area and remained in operation.
Unit 2 received severe damage to the front bumper and hood area and was towed by Jerry's Towing.
