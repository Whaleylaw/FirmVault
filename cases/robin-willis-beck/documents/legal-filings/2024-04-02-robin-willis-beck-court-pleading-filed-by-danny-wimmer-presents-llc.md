AFFIRMATIVE DEFENSES
l. Plaintiffs Complaint fails, in whole or in part, to state a claim against DWP upon
which relief can be granted.
2. DWP will offer proof of the defenses of primary and/or implied assumption of the
risk and/or contributory/comparative negligence as these defenses may develop during the discovery
of this action.
3. The alleged damages claimed were caused by the acts and/or omissions of someone
other than DWP over whom DWP had no control.
4. Plaintiffs damages were caused by unforeseeable, independent, intervening, or

omissions, if any, were superseded by such unforeseeable, independent, intervening, or superseding
