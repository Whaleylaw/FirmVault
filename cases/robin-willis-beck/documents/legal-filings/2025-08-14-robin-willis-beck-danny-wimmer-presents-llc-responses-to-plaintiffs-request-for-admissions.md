Set of Requests for Admissions as set forth below. DWP’s specific responses to each Interrogatory 
and Request are made subject to, and without waiving, these General Objections, which are 
incorporated by reference into each of DWP’s responses. The failure to list a specific General 
Objection within a response should not be construed as a waiver of that objection.

of, litigation, or that are protected by the attorney-client privilege, the work-product doctrine, the

neither produce nor log for privileged documents generated after the commencement of this litigation.
