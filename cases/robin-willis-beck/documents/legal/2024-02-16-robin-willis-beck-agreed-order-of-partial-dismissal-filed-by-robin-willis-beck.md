AGREED ORDER OF PARTIAL DISMISSAL
Upon Agreement of the Plaintiff, Robin W. Beck, and Defendants, Kentucky State FairBoard,
Kevin McCoy, and Shawn Hensler, and the Court being otherwise sufficiently advised:
IT IS HEREBY ORDERED AND ADJUDGED that all claims asserted herein against
Defendants Kentucky State Fair Board, Kevin McCoy, and Shawn Hensler are DISMISSED in their
entirety without prejudice. Both parties shall bear their own costs herein.
Nothing in this Order shall be construed as releasing Plaintiffs claims against Defendant
Danny Wimmer Presents, LLC, and the case shall remain active on the Court’s docket until the
remaining claims are resolved.
