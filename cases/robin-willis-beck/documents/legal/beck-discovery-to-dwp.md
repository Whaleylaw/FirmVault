**          * * * * * * * ***

Comes the Plaintiff, Robin Willis Beck, by counsel, and requests the Defendant, Danny Wimmer Presents, LLC (“Defendant”) answer under Oath the following Interrogatories and Requests for Admissions and produce the following documents and/or things within thirty (30) days of service hereof, inasmuch as these discovery requests were served contemporaneously with Plaintiff’s Complaint. 

These Interrogatories and Requests for Production of Documents and Things shall be deemed continuing. Knowledge of the attorneys for the Defendant(s) or the claims agent(s) or any other agent(s) of the Defendant(s) shall be deemed to be the knowledge of the Defendant. Anything coming to your (meaning the Defendant) knowledge after serving Answers to these Interrogatories and/or Responses to these Requests for Production of Documents and Things which would change your original answer or response, should be contained in an Amended Answer or an Amended Response and served upon Plaintiff’s attorneys immediately after the same is brought to your attention.  

<u>**DEFINITIONS**</u>

A.The word "person" means any individual, proprietorship, corporation, partnership, association, joint venture, or any other entity whatsoever, including but not limited to the Defendant and/or any other employee or agent of the Defendant or anyone having done work for the Defendant on the premises at issue in this case.   

B.The words "and" and "or" shall be construed conjunctively or disjunctively as necessary to make the request inclusive rather than exclusive.

C.The use of the words "include(s)" and "including" shall be construed to mean "without limitation."

D.The words "related to" and "relating" mean relating to, referring to, reflecting, pertaining to, evidencing, or any way relevant within the meaning of CR 26.02(1).

E.Plural shall include the singular and the singular shall include the plural.

F.Reference to any business entity, including but not limited to, a corporation or partnership, shall include a reference to any predecessor in interest, to any division or wholly or partly owned subsidiary of the entity specified, where applicable, and to any officer, shareholder, director, agent, representative, employer, or other person acting on behalf of the entity specified.

G.The term "present” used herein to refer to a point in time shall mean the date upon which Responses to the Requests are being made.

H.“You” or “Your” or “Defendant” as used herein and in the Interrogatories, Document Production Requests and Requests for Admission refers to Defendant Danny Wimmer Presents, LLC, any parent companies or subsidiaries Defendant and all agents of Defendant (whether an employee, attorney, independent contractor, officer, director or otherwise), any company or entity contracted by or with Defendant for any purpose (including but not limited to parent companies, maintenance companies, property owners or otherwise) for any purpose, or any other company or entity related, in any manner, by common ownership or control Defendant or otherwise connected by legal relationship in the broadest possible sense to Defendant.

I.“Identify” or “explain” or “state” as used in these Interrogatories with respect to any person or thing means to state fully and in detail who or what the thing or person is and if relating to a person to give the person’s full name, address, and other contact information to the extent known or available to the Defendant or other person contributing to the answers and responses and for that person to state and explain fully all of the information that person or persons has in connection with the subject matter of the request herein, and if relating to a business entity to provide that entity’s full contact information including the address and phone number and the point of contact if known to the Defendant, and to provide the full information requested as set forth to the same extent requested of any person. 

J. The “incident” as used in these Interrogatories refers to the fall suffered by Plaintiff Robin Willis Beck which occurred at the Louder Than Life music festival, as alleged in the Complaint.   

L.The term “Premises” means the grounds and walkways used for the Louder Than Life music festival held at the Kentucky Exposition Center on or about September 21, 2023, including all concrete, asphalt, and grass surfaces, vendor areas, access routes, lighting fixtures, and surrounding areas under the control or management of Danny Wimmer Presents, LLC.. 

M.The term “Hazard” shall refer to the unmarked drop-off or change in elevation between the grass and adjacent concrete or asphalt walkway where Plaintiff alleges she fell, as described in her deposition and shown in photographs taken by her husband the day after the incident.

<u>**INSTRUCTIONS**</u>

A.It is expected that before answering the Interrogatories and responding to any Document Request or Request for Admission, a good faith and complete inquiry by the Defendant, making reference to all documents, correspondence, memoranda, records, books or other papers in the Defendant’s and its employee’s possession, custody or control will be done so as to enable the Defendant to make full and truthful answers to the following. 

B.All documents provided in response to these Requests shall be appropriately numbered or marked to the specific number of each Request.

C.If any document or thing requested is withheld on a claim of privilege or work product, state for each such document: 

1.  The nature of the privilege claimed;

2.  The name of the author and recipient of the document;

3.  The general nature of and subject matter of the document;

4.  The date of the document; and 

5.  Identify the custodian of such document; and, 

6.  Provide a full privilege log of all such documents. 

D.In the event that any documents or things conforming to these requests no longer exist or are not in the custody or possession of Defendant or defense counsel or any other agent of the Defendant,  Defendant shall so state under oath and in writing within the time prescribed for their production.  In the case of documents or things which no longer exist, the Defendant shall further state when and by whom the documents or things were disposed of or destroyed.  In the case of documents or things which are not in the custody or possession of the Defendant or their counsel or agent(s), Defendant shall further state the name and address of the person presently having custody or possession of them. 

E.If part but not all of a document Request is objected to, any part of the document Request to which no objection is made should be fully complied with, and the parts of document Request should be considered severable for this purpose.

F.If a Request is objected to, the nature of the objection and the nature of the information relating to the objection made shall be fully set forth.
