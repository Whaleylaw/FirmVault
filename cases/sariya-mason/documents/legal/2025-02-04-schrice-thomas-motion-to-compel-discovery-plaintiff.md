MOTION TO COMPEL DESPOTION OF DEFENDANTS 
Comes now the Plaintiff, Shrice Thomas as next of kin on behalf of Sariya Mason, a minor, 
by and through counsel, and pursuant to all applicable Civil Rules and/or Local Rules hereby move 
the Court for an Order compelling the Defendant Esther Ricardo Vilches to attend and participate 
in a deposition. 
In support, the undersigned states that they first attempted to obtain the deposition of 
Defendant Esther Ricardo Vilches on December 3, 2024. On January 9, 2025, the undersigned 
again attempted to schedule the deposition of Defendant Esther Ricardo Vilches via email to 
defense counsel. After receiving no response, the undersigned again attempted on January 24, 
2025, to schedule the depositions. Plaintiff’s counsel has attempted to contact defense counsel with 
regard to scheduling but has not received a response. To date, there has been no response to any 
correspondence regarding the deposition of Defendant Esther Ricardo Vilches. 

2 
Plaintiffs’ counsel affirmatively states that he has attempted to confer in good faith with 
Defendant’s counsel in an effort to obtain the deposition identified herein, as detailed above, and 
believes that only a Court Order will result a due and sufficient response by the Defendant. An 
Order is appropriate under these circumstances pursuant to CR 37.01. 
WHEREFORE, the Plaintiff, by counsel, moved the Court to enter her tendered Order.
