8/19/22
I arrived at 2328 Quinn regarding investigate bite. When i arrived EMS was already on scene and Shively Pd had pulled up 
right after me. We got out and met with the victims mother along with EMS. The victims mother stated that the dog from next 
door had gotten out and attacked her daughter and she wanted to know what we were going to do. I asked her if her daughter 
sustained any injuries and she stated that she did and walked me inside. Once inside i saw the vicitm laying on the couch with 
EMS. I asked them if they were transporting her and they stated that they werent and the mother was just going to take her 
herself. I was able to see her injuries which were a couple of puncture wounds on her left thigh and a scratch mark in her 
back. She also showed me a couple of marks on her right shoulder and stated that it was from the same dog a couple of 
months ago. I got the victims and mothers info for the report and they explained to me that the victim was getting ready to 
walk to the store when the dog was loose and attacked her. While speaking with her Shively PD attempted to make contact 
with the dog owner. I gave her 2 VWS, one for her to fill out and one for her daughter. I aslo had her sign a RA for me and 
keep one for herself. I explained to her the PDD process and she asked me if i was taking the dog. I told her it would depend 
on if we have history or not. She stated that the dog 3 years ago bit her younger daughter and we came out and told her that 
we would take the dog if it happens again. I told her if we have history we might take the dog but we might not. I asked her if 
she would fill out the VWS now and she stated that she was going to take her daughter to the hospital and would fill them out 
there and send them to me. 
I thanked her and walked over to 2326 Quinn. I knocked on the door and met with 2 juveniles. I told them who i was and why i 
was here. They stated that the dog owner one of the juveniles Mother Surelis Castillo was in cuba and would be back in 
sunday. I explained to them the PDD process and told them we would be back. They thanked me and i cleared the scene. A 
follow up will be made to colllect the VWS from the victims and then issue all necessarry paperwork to the dog owner.
End Memo
