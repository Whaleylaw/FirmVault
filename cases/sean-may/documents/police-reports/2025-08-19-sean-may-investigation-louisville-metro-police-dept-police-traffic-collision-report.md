Pre Collision
Unit 1 was entering W Broadway from a parking lot on the North side of the street.
Unit 2 was driving westbound on W Broadway approaching S 13th St.
There were two lanes of Westbound traffic on W Broadway.
Collision
As Unit 1 was attempting to exit the parking lot, an unknown person in the lane closest to him allegedly
waved him on. Unit 1 was unable to see traffic in the furthest lane from him. Unit 1 pulled out of the parking
lot and onto W Broadway.
Unit 2 was driving W on Broadway with the right of way, when his front end collided with the driver side
quarter panel of Unit 1's vehicle.
Post Collision
Unit 1's front bumper was beginning to fall off, and also had moderate damage to the driver side quarter
panel but the vehicle was still drivable.
Unit 2's vehicle was unable to drive, with moderate damage to the front end of the vehicle and was pushed
to the side of the road and towed.
Neither Unit 1 or Unit 2 had any injuries from the collision.
