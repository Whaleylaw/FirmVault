Comes Defendant, PAUL RAFFERTY, by counsel, and submits to Plaintiff, SHANNON COLVIN, the following Interrogatories and Request for Production of Documents and demands they be answered under oath within thirty days of service as provided by the Kentucky Rules of Civil Procedure.

**INTERROGATORIES**

1.Please state your full name, including maiden name (if applicable), middle name and all previous names which you have ever used, your current residence address, date of birth, Social Security number, your occupation, name of supervisor and name, address and telephone number of your employer at the time of the accident.

**ANSWER:**

2.Have you rejected the provisions of the Kentucky Motor Vehicle Reparations Act (KRS 304.39) refusing to consent to the limitation of your right to sue for personal injuries?  If so, furnish and file a copy of the rejection form filed with the Kentucky Department of Insurance.

**ANSWER:**

3.If the answer to Question No. 2 aforesaid is in the negative, then answer the following questions:

a.As a result of the subject collision of this action have you incurred medical expenses for the treatment of said injuries in an amount exceeding $1,000.00?  If you have, furnish true and correct copies of bills and statements evidencing this medical expense and furnish copy of a medical report from each attending physician.

b.As a result of the injuries incurred in the subject collision, did you receive a permanent disfigurement, a fracture to a bone, a compound, comminuted, displaced or compressed fracture, loss of a body member, permanent injury within reasonable probability, or permanent loss of bodily function?  If your answer to any of these is in the affirmative, state specifically which one and furnish a medical report from a treating physician to substantiate your answer.

c.Has any doctor told you that you had received a permanent disfigurement, a fracture to a bone, a compound, comminuted, displaced or compressed fracture, loss of a body member, permanent injury within reasonable probability, or permanent loss of bodily function?  If your answer to any of these is in the affirmative, state specifically which one and furnish the name, professional address, and a medical report from a treating physician to substantiate your answer.

**ANSWER:**

4.At the time of the automobile collision on October 1, 2023, were you covered by a policy of insurance providing you with basic reparation benefits (no-fault benefits)?  If so, give the name of the insurance company providing such benefits and the limits thereof.

**ANSWER:**

5.State whether or not at the time and occurrence of the accident on October 1, 2023, you were operating or occupying, and your operation or occupation thereof, was covered by a policy of automobile liability insurance?  If so, furnish a copy of the declaration sheet of the policy in force at the time of the accident.

**ANSWER:**

6.State whether or not you have made a claim for Basic Reparations Benefits to the automobile liability insurance carrier providing coverage to the automobile and your operation or occupation thereof at the time of the accident.  If your answer is in the affirmative, provide copies of all documentation which you have made to said company for Basic Reparations Benefits under said policy.

**ANSWER:**

7.Describe in detail all injuries, ailments, or pain which you claim you have suffered as a result of the alleged accident of October 1, 2023, stating in your answer the parts of your body so affected; the severity of such injuries, ailments or pains; the date when each such injury, ailment, or pain first manifested itself and how long each lasted.

**ANSWER:**

8.State the names and addresses of all doctors(including the name of the facility at which they practice), hospitals, clinics, pharmacies, or other health care providers or facilities which you claim provided treatment for any injuries which you claim were sustained as a result of the accident of October 1, 2023, to include any examining doctors, stating in detail the dates you consulted each health care provider and the treatment provided by each.

**ANSWER:**

9.Provide an itemized list of all medical expenses to date detailing the name of each provider or facility along with treatment dates and total charges by each provider.  Please also provide copies of all bills or expenses supporting each itemized charge which you claim resulted from the accident of October 1, 2023.

  **ANSWER:**

10.State the names, addresses, type of specialty, and dates of treatment for each doctor, hospital, clinic, pharmacies, or other health care or medical service provider or facility with whom you have consulted or seen for the preceding twenty (20) years, stating in detail the condition and diagnosis for which you received treatment.

**ANSWER:**

11.Pursuant to CR 8.01(2), state the amount claimed for each item of unliquidated damages, and for each state the basis for such amount, and the method of calculation of each amount.

**ANSWER:**

12.Did you ever have an application for life, health, accident, medical, hospital, liability, fire or casualty insurance rejected?  If so, for each rejection, state:

a.The date of application;

b.The date of rejection;

c.The type of insurance applied for;

d.The name and address of the insurance with which the application was filed;

e.The reason given for the rejection.

**ANSWER:**

13.With respect to each expert you expect to call as a witness at the trial of this action, state the following:

a.Give their names, business addresses, and telephone numbers;

b.The field or fields in which each witness will be offered as an expert;

c.A current Curriculum Vitae of each witness' qualifications in the field in which they are expected to testify;

d.The substance of the facts to which each such witness is expected to testify;

e.The substance of the opinions to which each such witness is expected to testify and a summary of the grounds for each opinion.

f.Identify each and every writing of any type upon which any expert referred to previously relies upon with a specific citation to the portion of the writing relied upon.

g.Identify specifically, including source of publication, each and every article, portion of text and other written material prepared by each such expert relating to any matter in issue in this action.

**ANSWER:**

14.  Provide the name, address and telephone number of all persons known to Plaintiff, his attorney or other representative:

a.Who is a witness to the accident.

b.Who possesses discoverable information or knowledge relevant or material to any issue in this litigation.

With respect to each individual that has been identified in this interrogatory, please state with specificity what information or knowledge they have relevant or material to any issue in this litigation or what eyewitness testimony they possess.  

**ANSWER:**

**ANSWER:**

Also provide the full name and address of your employers and places of business for the last five years giving dates of employment and specific titles or positions held.

**ANSWER:**

17.If you have suffered or claim any financial loss of any kind as a result of the accident alleged in your complaint, state in full detail such loss, itemizing as far as possible doctor's bills, medical bills, hospitalization, lost wages, salary or business and any other losses claimed.

**ANSWER:**

18.If, as a result of the alleged accident, you claim you lost any time from any occupation or employment, then state the beginning and ending dates for which you lost employment, the amount of wages or income you claim to have lost during these periods, your occupation for each period of lost employment, and the full name and address of each employer or place of business or other persons responsible for your wages or income.

**ANSWER:**

19.State the name, address, title and telephone number of each and every witness that you plan or intend to use or call as a witness at trial in the above-styled case.  

**ANSWER:**

20.If you have a Facebook account, TikTok Account, Instagram account or Twitter Account, please provide your account name, membership ID, or any other such identifying moniker, regardless of its title for each such account.

**ANSWER**:

**ANSWER**:

22.Have you received, submitted a claim for, or applied for Veterans Administration Benefits, Social Security disability benefits, or any other governmental benefits?  If your answer is "yes", please answer the following:

a.Identify the governmental agency by name and address, telephone number, and name of caseworker.

b.What were the results of any decision made by that agency on your claim or application for benefits and the current status of your claim?

c.Do you agree to complete, sign and return the attached authorization for the release of your disability claims records from that agency?

**ANSWER**:

23.Please state whether any individual or organization including, but not limited to, insurance companies, has ever requested that you submit to an independent medical, chiropractic, or other examination of any nature or kind setting forth the name, address and telephone number of the person or organization requesting said examination, the date of said request, and the name and address of the person to perform the examination.

**ANSWER:**

24.Please state whether or not any medical bills or other expenses which you allege resulted from this accident have been paid by an insurance company, government agency, or any other entity, specifically including, but not limited to, Medicaid, Medicare, Blue Cross/Blue Shield or any health insurance company, and if so, please give the exact name and address of all such entities, the amount paid by each, and whether you have assigned any rights to any such entities or whether any such entities have subrogation rights to the extent of such payments.

**ANSWER:**

Please provide all documentation concerning the payment of any governmental benefits or claims made under any governmental program.

26.Do you agree to supplement your answer to the preceding Interrogatory as soon as you obtain new information and do you further promise to send undersigned counsel a copy of all documents and letters received from Medicare, Medicaid, Passport Health, or any governmental entity? 

**ANSWER**:

a.Name of entity against whom claim was made;

b.Type of claim (i.e., automobile, fall down, products, etc.);

c.Date and location of injury or occurrence;

d.Name, address, claim number, claim representative, telephone number of any insurance company involved;

e.Nature of injury;
