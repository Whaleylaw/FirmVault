On [DOB-4], | responded to a check welfare in the 3200 Block of Scottsville Road. A caller advised that an older male was laying in the median of Scottsville Road. Another caller advised that the male appeared

to have been hit by a vehicle.

Upon arrival, | made contact with Stevie Hawks in front of Red Roof Inn (3140 Scottsville Road) who advised that he had been hit by a vehicle while attempting to cross Scottsville Road from the Red Roof Inn side of Scottsville Road to the America's Best Value Inn side of Scottsville Road at around 0420hrs. Hawks stated that a vehicle stopped for him and when he began to cross into the other lane, a vehicle struck him. Hawks had apparent injuries to his face and his left arm. He advised that he also had pain in his legs. Hawks was assessed by Medical Center EMS (Medic 55) at the scene and was transported to: BGFD Greenwood Fire Station by Medical Center EMS for helicopter transport. They advised that they would be flying him to Skyline in Nashville, Tenriessee to receive treatment tor his injuries.

On scene, Hawks' shoes, socks, and glasses were located in the roadway in the left most north bound lane. Officers also located small parts of what appears to be a black vehicle in the roadway in the left most North bound lane in front of Red Roof Inn and McDonald's. Officer's located a black driver's side mirror and parts of what appeared to be a vehicle's lower grill/fog light assembly in the roadway. | also located what appeared to be part of a headlight bulb in the roadway near the mirror. Hawks was unable to provide a vehicle description at this time.

| made contact with an employee of America's Best Value Inn after the roadway had been opened in an attempt to locate video footage. She allowed me to view the cameras. Upon viewing the footage, we are able determine, according to the time stamp on their cameras that Hawks left the hotel at 0432hrs and began walking towards Chick-fil-a. At 0435hrs, Hawks arrives at Speedway. At 0439hrs, Hawks leaves Speedway and begins walking up the access road towards The Red Roof Inn. Based on the time stamp on the video provided by America's Best Value Inn, Hawks was most likely hit at 0440hrs. In the video at 0440hrs, we see a white pick up truck stop in the right lane of the North bound lanes. While that truck is stopped, a black sedan is seen driving in the left North bound lane. The sedan doesn't appear to slow down. The sedan continues traveling North on Scottsville Road. After the sedan passes, the white truck stays stopped for just a moment and then continues traveling North on Scottsville Road. This video has been logged into evidence.

Hawks' brother, Michael Hawks [PHONE-5]), also showed up on scene and advised that he often walks from America's Best Value Inn to Speedway at about this time every morning. He advised that he did not see the incident occur. Stevie Hawks' daughter, Cindi Wade [PHONE-6]), contacted the Bowling Green Police Department regarding the incident and spoke with Sergeant Perry (Badge #201).

Photos of the scene have been taken and will be logged into evidence. Collision Reconstruction is pending and is being completed by Officer Rone (Badge #146).

This report will be supplemented with any new information received regarding the collision.
