Our office represents Taylor Thompson with regard to the above referenced claim. Please advise us in writing of whether your company will assert a right of subrogation for the benefits that have been paid on behalf of our insured. If you believe we have a duty to recognize any such lien, please provide us with the authority to do so in writing within ten (10) days of the date this letter is received.
