RESPONSE: Please see attached estimate.
REQUEST NO. 4: Please produce any and all statements, written or verbal, recorded

c) The date obtained.

REQUEST NO. 5: Please attach the curriculum vitae of each expert witness that the 
Defendant has hired or consulted to be a witness in this case. 
With regard to any doctor who has or will examine the Plaintiff, please produce the 
following: 
 (a) Copies of any and all state, local, or federal statutes, regulations, 
ordinance, rules or decisions, industry standards, “best practices”, or other 
obligations which you believe to be, or which you have been advised to be, 
applicable in this case. 
 (b) Their entire file on this case and all documents contained therein (cover to 
cover). 
 (c) Their current and complete Curriculum Vitae. 
 (d) All documents, including but not limited to, billing statements reflecting 
the hours the Doctor have expended on the subject lawsuit, the rate at which you 
are billing for your services, and any amounts billed thus far and/or paid thus far 
on behalf of any party herein. 
 (f) All correspondence, memoranda, reports, and other writings and 
documentation reviewed by the Doctor from or on behalf of any party or their 
attorney herein relative to this action. 
 (g) All reports, opinions, analyses, records, or documents which were 
furnished to the Doctor by other experts in this case, either directly or through 
Defendant’s counsel. 
 (h) All correspondence, memoranda, reports, written opinions, theories, 
impressions, requests for information, and other documentation generated by the 
Doctor relative to the Defendant’s and/or the subject litigation. 
 (i) All documents reflecting the date on which the Doctor was hired as an 
expert or otherwise by any of the parties herein, including but not limited to a 
complete copy of any and all contracts whereby your services were retained by 
any party herein relative to this action. 

 (j) All reports rendered by the Doctor or anyone on the Doctor’s behalf 
relative to this action. 
 (k) All computer printouts with all e-mail stored messages from computers 
used by the Doctor or the Doctor’s agent, servants, and employees relative to this 
action. 
 (l) All handwritten or typed/computer notes taken or prepared by the Doctor 
or the Doctor’s agents, servants, or employees which were prepared for this case, 
i.e., any working papers. 
 (m) All publications or source materials which the Doctor have consulted 
and/or relied upon in this case in formulating or rendering your opinions. 
 (n) Copies of all correspondence written by the Doctor to anyone concerning 
this case. 
 (o) Copies of all correspondence written to the Doctor from anyone 
concerning this case. 
 (p) Copies of all documents provided to the Doctor by any Defendant, 
Defendant’s counsel, or any other person or entity. 
 (q) All documents indicating the case name, county and court where pending, 
case number, attorney who hired the Doctor, and attorneys representing all parties 
in the last five (5) years in which you have been retained in a consulting expert or 
testifying expert capacity. 
 (r) All deposition transcripts, affidavits, and trial transcripts of you or your 
sworn testimony in matters from the past five (5) years which are in the Doctor’s 
possession. 
 (s) The Doctor’s fee schedule for work you would be hired to perform in 
lawsuits as an expert witness either before or after a suit is filed. 
 (t) Drafts or final exhibits which you have prepared or have been asked to 
prepare for the Doctor’s use of the use of others as a demonstrative exhibit or as 
an admitted exhibit in the Hearing of this case. 
 
 (u) Any videotapes or photographs in your possession having anything to do 
with this action or the parties to this action. 
 (v) All reports the Doctor, the Doctor’s office, or staff have prepared 
regarding your (including your assistants and consultants’) observations, 
evaluations, opinions, and any other information related to the Defendants and/or 
the Doctor’s involvement in this case. 

 (w) All histories, questionnaires, and other records obtained by the examining 
Doctor from the Defendant in this action. 
 (x) Any document describing or memorializing the Doctor’s general litigation 
experience including the percentage of work for Plaintiffs and Defendants. 
 (y) A list of those persons who assisted the Doctor in his/her evaluation of this 
case. 
 (z) Any data, literature, reports, treatises, peer reviewed articles, or textbooks 
to which the Doctor have referred in developing, offering, or finalizing the 
Doctor‘s opinions in this case.
